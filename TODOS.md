- **R-BIOSPHERE (candidate).** How a generated world is populated with many plant and animal varieties, and how edibility, nutrition, toxicity, and medicinal value are determined, without hand-authoring each species. The on-ideology direction: a species is a generated point over the trait axes the flora and fauna structures already declare (sampled through seed-keyed counter-RNG, validated against a food-web-closure and biome-fit constraint, then radiated into ecotypes and daughter species by the genome machinery over deep time), and edibility is a measured relational consequence of an organism's composition vector and a consumer's physiology (nutrient needs, toxin tolerances, digestive capability), never a stored flag, so the same berry is food to one race, poison to another, and medicine to a third. Composition axes are heritable, so defensive toxins and the domestication syndrome fall out of selection rather than being scripted, and dietary knowledge is emergent and fallible through the evidence system. Reserved and not guessed: the trait-axis catalogue, the nutrient, toxin, and digestibility composition axes, the niche and closure validation criteria, the biome-fit and edibility and harm law forms, and the seeding parameters (founder counts, diversity targets, resample bounds). Vehicle: `docs/working/BIOSPHERE_GENERATION_GUIDE.md`. Couples to the stocks-and-flows, flora, fauna, materials, drives, disease-and-poison, technique, genome, anatomy, and evidence systems, and to the physics-and-materials substrate it reads its composition axes from; sequenced after that substrate and the genome are built. Directly informs Track 1: the edibility-as-relation reframe is the faithful way to build "what is edible" in the living-world needs loop. Owner direction (2026-06-30): run the biosphere's deep-time evolution as a distinct pre-dawn genesis epoch at the aggregate (allele-frequency pool) tier, so plants and animals seed, radiate into ecotypes and daughter species, co-evolve (defensive toxins, domestication-ready stock), and go extinct over deep time before the dawn, and the people arrive into a mature, self-made ecology with established niches, edibility, and toxicity to discover. This is the natural endpoint of the Part 25 deep-time evolution plus this candidate; it sequences the genome inheritance and the two-tier allele-frequency pools (the evolution engine) and the composition/edibility substrate ahead of the dawn, the whole pre-dawn epoch deterministic and seed-keyed so it is part of the world's reproducible identity, with individual organisms promoted from the pools on demand only when the people interact with them. The world-genesis sequence becomes: worldgen, then the pre-dawn biosphere epoch, then the dawn, then play.

## Working principle: nothing chosen is final (owner standing instruction, 2026-06-30)

Every reserved value and every method fork surfaced to the owner is provisional, not a commitment. The assumption is that a setting will not survive scale unchanged: where a system eats the compute budget, tune its detail down (fewer loci, a coarser sampler, a shorter pre-dawn, an approximation for an exact sum); where there is headroom, add detail back. The dials ride a sliding scale between fidelity and cost, and a value set today is a hypothesis awaiting a playtest. Recorded in the `calibration/reserved.toml` header and the test-worlds doc.
