# Ideation-Discovery Handoff: let a being learn what works, and let the built affordances get used

This is a self-contained brief for the agent building the ideation / experiential-discovery arc, the arc the owner directs as next. Read `CLAUDE.md` first. The arc's one job: make a being LEARN WHAT WORKS and DISCOVER NEW ACTIONS, so the affordances the materials arc built (grasp, extract, craft, dig, geophage, release) stop being dormant (founder-zero, reachable only by blind evolutionary search) and get USED. It is the reward complement of the shipped harm-learning arc: the same associative engine, keyed on a positive interoceptive delta instead of a harm bit. Nothing is scripted. "Chopping a tree for wood" is a high-weight belief PATH through primitives, never a coded verb. File:line anchors are verified against the tree; re-grep after any edit.

# HANDOFF BRIEF: THE IDEATION / EXPERIENTIAL-DISCOVERY ARC

The arc after materials. Its one job: give a being the loop that turns a felt reserve RISE into a repeatable, teachable action, so the world's affordances become discoveries a lineage keeps, rather than dead output channels that only random mutation ever lights up.

---

## 1. THE GAP: the affordances are built, and nobody uses them

The materials arc gave the world matter and gave bodies affordances that read raw physics against it. A being can in principle grasp a graspable thing, strike a fracturable thing, extract, dig, or ingest for composition. The affordance registry gates each on a derived capability (`AffordanceRegistry::afforded` / `afforded_structure`, `homeostasis.rs:606,642`), the physics oracle for the contests exists (`fracture_onset` `laws.rs:317`, `cut_penetrate` `:239`, `contact_pressure` `:218`), and the controller emits one output slot per affordance in canonical id order (`OutputSlot`, `controller.rs:112`).

Two facts make all of that inert. First, every founder expresses ZERO weight on the feature and affordance channels, so only blind evolutionary search can ever lift an affordance off zero, on the timescale of selection rather than a lifetime. Second, most affordances have no enactment: `step_with_field_dirs` runs the controller, gathers the afforded set, calls `layout.decide`, and matches the single winner against `MOVE => walk`, `INGEST => Liebig ingest`, `_ => {}` (`locomotion.rs:789-869`), where that catch-all is labelled in the source as "an affordance the engine has no enactment for yet: idle." So an affordance that wins the tick does nothing, and even if it did, nothing in the being's lifetime would learn that it paid off.

The missing capability is symmetric with the one harm-learning already filled. Harm-learning gave a being the AVERSIVE half: correlate a felt reserve FALL with a perceived feature, form a defeasible belief, steer away, transmit it, and let it persist by selection (`HARM_LEARNING_HANDOFF.md`; `is_harm_tick`, `homeostasis.rs:806`; `feature_observations`, `learn.rs:180`). What is absent is the APPETITIVE half and everything it unlocks: a being that correlates a felt reserve RISE with an action it took, compresses what worked into a belief, spreads it by the shipped gossip channel, and plans by searching its own beliefs. Fill that half on the same engine and the affordances stop being dormant, because a lifetime of experience can now find them and a lineage can keep them.

**The single loop that closes it.** Perceive raw physics-derived affordances, propose a candidate PRIMITIVE SEQUENCE (a hypothesis), execute it against the physics oracle, credit the outcome by the interoceptive delta, compress what worked into a belief (a wildcard template over affordance predicates), spread it by grounded gossip, and plan by belief-graph search. Every term is already substrate the harm arc shipped or the materials arc built, keyed differently. Hold the loop and "chopping" emerges as a high-confidence belief path; break it anywhere by coding a verb, a percept tag, or a reward table, and you have authored the thing the design forbids.

## 2. THE GOAL

Extend the shipped associative substrate into a discovery loop, inventing no second engine. The reward learner IS `evidence::InferenceFrame` (`add_evidence`/`commit`, `evidence.rs:155,181`) and `good_weight` (`evidence.rs:305`), the identical order-independent fixed-point log-odds accumulator harm-learning writes through, keyed on a new reserved attr and a new subject band. The mechanism is fixed Rust; the reward likelihoods, the sequence vocabulary, the affordance-percept membership, and the reserved calibrations are data and grow with the world.

The hard constraints on the shape:

- **No coded technique verb.** There is no `Chop`, `Knap`, `Cook`, or `Forage` anywhere in the arc. A technique is a high-weight PATH through primitives against matter; it must emerge from sequencing primitives and be kept by selection, never arrive as its own enum arm. This is the owner's endorsed steering line (`MATERIAL_SUBSTRATE_HANDOFF.md`, "primitives are coded, techniques emerge").
- **Raw-feature percepts only.** The affordance scalars a being perceives (graspability, fracture-potential, sharpness) are DERIVED from the physics laws over nearby matter's geometry and material, quantized like `feature_bucket` (`percept.rs:150`) and keyed by a physics law or axis id. Never an `IsTree`, `IsAxe`, or object-kind tag, mirroring how `Composition::sensed` returns a valence-blind raw amount (`edibility.rs:99`).
- **Primitive-sequence beliefs.** A discovered action is a wildcard template, `grasp(sharp), actuate(fracturable) -> reward`, stored as an ordinary committing `(subject, attr)` frame so the shipped `gossip`/`converse` channel carries it for free (`world.rs:3400,3449`) with zero transmission code. Never a hardcoded `(primitive_a, primitive_b)` pair.
- **The reward sign is the being's own interoception.** A positive `ReserveMemory::delta` (`homeostasis.rs:769`) past a reserved reward-noise floor is the ONLY reward signal, the exact sign complement of `is_harm_tick`. No per-affordance reward table, no "wood is good" constant, no branch on which reserve rose.
- **Generic search only.** Belief-graph planning is generic path search whose heuristic is drawn ONLY from belief confidence (`knowledge_level`, `belief.rs:165`) and `InferenceFrame` support. Never a hand-tuned goal-to-action table.

The feasibility is favorable because the harm arc already stood up most of the substrate. Six readers of the tree confirmed the appetitive learner keys on a positive reserve delta on the SAME engine with zero engine change: `InferenceFrame`/`consider`/`add_evidence`/`commit` are sign-agnostic and already commit any two-hypothesis frame; `good_weight` is the reward weight over two new likelihoods with no new math; `ReserveMemory::delta` is already signed and its own docstring names "a reserve FALLING felt as harm and rising as relief" (`homeostasis.rs:742`), so the appetitive signal SOURCE already exists; and the `Stimulus::Observe` -> `consider` path (`world.rs:2424`), the per-tick runner loop shape (`runner.rs:1450-1491`), and the empty-registry opt-in pattern are all reused verbatim. The unbuilt pieces are the reward attr and its calibration mirror, the sequence-template subject key, the eligibility trace that carries delayed credit, the affordance-percept registry and its binding-graph sampler, the forward model and its prediction-error exploration drive, and the belief-graph planner.

## 3. THE ORDERED, INCREMENTAL BUILD PLAN

Use the read-then-wire slice cadence the anatomy, harm-learning, and materials arcs proved. The READ half adds the substrate, the derivation, and the tests OFF the run path and hash-neutral, so every existing scenario stays byte-identical. The WIRE half, which changes `state_hash` by folding a new per-being trace, minting into a new subject band, or drawing a new RNG phase, is isolated into its own slice and made OPT-IN (an empty default registry or trace, the `PerceptRegistry::empty` / `ReserveMemory::is_empty` pattern, `percept.rs:67`, `homeostasis.rs:792`), so a scenario that does not opt in replays bit-for-bit. Nothing hash-changing lands in the same slice as its substrate.

The four pieces are in strict priority order. Piece 1 is first because it is the piece that makes the built affordances get USED: it is the reward half of the loop, and its milestone is a being that learns an affordance pays off and repeats it, observed in a run, not scripted. Pieces 2 through 4 widen what can be discovered, represented, explored, and planned, each reading the credit signal piece 1 supplies.

### Piece 1 (first, the one that lights the affordances): THE APPETITIVE LEARNER

The reward complement of the built aversive harm-learner. When a reserve RISES after a primitive sequence, credit that sequence and the percepts present, by temporal-difference credit assignment.

**Slice 1a (READ, hash-neutral): the reward twin of the harm signal.** Add a benefit predicate that is the literal sign complement of `is_harm_tick`: `delta > 0 && delta > reward_noise_floor`, one small mirror function reading the SAME `ReserveMemory::delta` already snapshotted each tick (`homeostasis.rs:769`, snapshot at `locomotion.rs:723`). Add a new reserved attr `REWARD_ATTR = AttrKindId(u32::MAX - 3)`, disjoint from `HARM_ATTR` (`u32::MAX - 2`, `learn.rs:50`) so a reward belief never aliases a harm belief, with hypotheses `REWARDS`/`NEUTRAL` mirroring `HARMS`/`BENIGN` (`learn.rs:54,57`). Add a mirror calib block (`p_reward_given_rewards`, `p_reward_given_neutral`) reusing `certainty_clamp = evidence.log_odds_clamp` exactly as `HarmLearningCalib` does (`learn.rs:125`). In its degenerate single-tick form (credit the current percept when a reserve rises) this is a near-verbatim clone of `feature_observations` with `toward = REWARDS`. Pure, RNG-free, tested off the run path.

**Slice 1b (READ, hash-neutral): the sequence-template subject and the eligibility trace.** The one real gap is crediting a primitive SEQUENCE, not a single-tick feature; the harm learner is memoryless and correlates this tick's harm bit with this tick's standing-on-feature percept, with no action and no temporal trace. Two additions, both off the run path. First, extend subject keying from `feature_subject(channel, bucket)` (a percept key, `learn.rs:77`) to a SEQUENCE key: a canonical, RNG-free bit-pack of the executed `[(primitive, target-affordance-bucket, param-bucket)]` sequence, minted in a NEW reserved band disjoint from `FEATURE_SUBJECT_BASE = 1 << 62` and below `1 << 63`, reusing the `reserved-base | packed-fields` idiom `feature_subject` established. This IS the wildcard-template belief `grasp(sharp), actuate(fracturable) -> reward`. Second, an ELIGIBILITY TRACE, the new mechanism: per-being short memory of recently-executed sequences and their bound target-affordance buckets, decayed by recency (the TD lambda). It parallels `ReserveMemory` (per-being dynamic state) and must fold into `state_hash` in canonical order and be empty-by-default.

**Slice 1c (WIRE, opt-in, hash-changing): route delayed credit through the shipped path.** When a positive delta fires, contribute credit to the sequence-subject that preceded it, scaled by the eligibility factor. Crucially, that decay enters through the EXISTING `weight` argument of `consider`, exactly as `plasticity` already scales it (`learn.rs:187`), so no engine change; only the credit SOURCE (a trace instead of the current cell) is new. Emit each credit as a `Stimulus::Observe { subject: sequence_subject, attr: REWARD_ATTR, hyps: [REWARDS, NEUTRAL], toward, weight, from: self }` on the identical runner path harm uses (`runner.rs:1479-1491`), applied in `World::tick` via `mind.consider` (`world.rs:2424-2439`). The belief plasticity scaling reuses the existing heritable `Mind::plasticity` (neutral at one), so no fabricated learning rate enters here.

- **Dependency.** None upstream beyond the shipped harm substrate and one enacted affordance to produce a reserve rise. `INGEST` already has an enactment arm and raises a reserve (`locomotion.rs:838`), so piece 1 is demonstrable before any new enactment arm exists; crediting grasp/extract/craft awaits the materials arc's enactment arms (coupling, section 5).
- **Guards.** *Determinism:* the credit step draws NO randomness (the harm learner is RNG-free); the eligibility trace folds into `state_hash` in canonical (BTreeMap sequence) order exactly as `ReserveMemory.prev` does at `runner.rs:2132`, and is empty-by-default so opted-out runs stay byte-identical. The accumulation is a plain i128 add, worker-width- and order-independent, clamp applied only at read (`evidence.rs:170`). *Principle 8/9:* the sequence subject is minted from RAW affordance buckets, never an object id or an `IsTree` tag; the reward sign comes from the being's own `ReserveMemory::delta`, never a coded value; ONE reward attr serves all sequences, with identity in the subject, so no branch on which reserve rose. *Principle 11:* the calib is fail-loud from the manifest (`HarmLearningCalib::from_manifest` pattern, `learn.rs:119`).
- **Milestone (the arc's headline).** In a recorded run, a being with an enacted affordance that raises a reserve executes it, its reward learner commits a belief that the sequence in that percept-context pays off, and the being REPEATS it at a rate above a naive control that never took the action, with the belief decaying if the payoff is removed. A being learns an affordance pays off and repeats it, observed in the log, not scripted.

### Piece 2: ACTION-AS-HYPOTHESIS REPRESENTATION

An action is a parameterized `[(primitive, target, params)]` sequence; the action space is the product of primitives, targets, and params. Discovery is affordance-bound sampling.

**Slice 2a (READ, hash-neutral): affordance scalars as raw percepts.** Today `afforded`/`afforded_structure` return only a present/absent id list (`homeostasis.rs:606,642`), and the physics laws are consumed only as capability gates, never surfaced as scalars. Add an affordance-percept registry, a sibling of `PerceptRegistry` (empty-by-default, canonical-id-ordered, `perceive -> Vec<Fixed>`, `feature_bucket` quantization to a belief-subject key), whose entries derive each scalar from the existing pure `derive_capabilities` dispatch (`capability.rs:440`) run over NEARBY MATTER's `geo(axis)`/`mat(axis)` accessors: sharpness is the existing Pierce score (`capability.rs:172`), fracture-potential derives from `mat.fracture_strength`/`fracture_onset` (`laws.rs:317`), graspability is a NEW `CapabilityKernel` variant bound to a floor law over geometry, added the physics-registry way (a data entry plus reserved refs), never a special-case branch. Each scalar sits in the raw feature block like the substance channels, keyed by a physics law or axis id.

**Slice 2b (READ, hash-neutral): the binding graph and the belief-biased sampler.** Build a binding graph where a sharp thing meets a fracturable thing, from the raw derived scalars, and sample candidate paths biased by need (a reserve near its floor) and belief confidence over matching templates. Beliefs store as wildcard templates keyed on affordance BUCKETS (the piece-1 sequence subject), so a template learned on one sharp-and-fracturable pair generalizes to another, exactly as the quantized feature bucket generalizes a harm belief.

**Slice 2c (WIRE, opt-in, hash-changing): a multi-step stepper and the enactment surface.** Today the atom is one `ControllerDecision { affordance, activation, heading }` chosen by `decide`'s single-winner argmax (`controller.rs:315,481`); the recurrent hidden state (`controller.rs:145`) is the only cross-tick carrier and it carries activations, not a plan. Add a sequence type and a stepper that holds and advances a candidate across ticks, and, where the materials arc has not yet supplied it, an enactment arm replacing the `_ => idle` catch-all (`locomotion.rs:869`) that is a pure physics-law application gated only by the derived affordance, with the which-and-when left to the evolved controller weights and learned beliefs.

- **Dependency.** Piece 1 for the credit signal that scores a sampled path; the materials-arc enactment arms and the derived-capability reads.
- **Guards.** *Determinism:* any candidate-sequence SAMPLING uses counter-keyed deterministic RNG folded via `state_hash` under a declared Phase (section 4), and any new argmax keeps a canonical tie-break by id order, never insertion or HashMap order (`decide` ties break by lowest id, `controller.rs:334`). Perception stays RNG-free. *Principle 8/9:* the affordance scalar is a raw `[0,1]` derived read keyed by a physics id, never a kind label or an authored valence; the template is a wildcard over affordance predicates, never a coded primitive pair; the enactment arm supplies mechanism (what the physics does), never policy. *Principle 11:* the affordance-percept quantization granularity is reserved-with-basis (section 3.5).
- **Milestone.** A being with a low reserve samples a path binding a perceived sharp thing to a perceived fracturable thing, executes it, and (through piece 1) forms a wildcard template that a later, differently-located sharp-and-fracturable pair satisfies, so the discovery generalizes without a per-target branch.

### Piece 3: FORWARD MODEL + PREDICTION-ERROR EXPLORATION DRIVE

The being predicts the interoceptive outcome of an action; when the prediction fails (surprise), motor variance inflates, biased along the DIMENSION of the error.

**Slice 3a (READ, hash-neutral): the forward model and the prediction-error read.** A per-being forward model maps a candidate sequence and its bound affordance buckets to a predicted interoceptive delta, and the prediction error is the signed difference between predicted and felt `ReserveMemory::delta`. The model is a light associative read over the same belief substrate (a template's committed reward is its prediction); no new engine. A force-error perturbs force params, never "try a stone," because the error is carried per param DIMENSION of the sequence.

**Slice 3b (WIRE, opt-in, hash-changing): variance inflation on surprise.** When the prediction error exceeds a reserved threshold, inflate motor variance on the param dimension of the error, using the existing counter-keyed pattern `DrawKey::entity(being, epoch, Phase::X).rng(seed)` (`locomotion.rs:930`, `core/rng.rs:94`) under a NEW Phase variant (a hypothesis/actuate phase) so it cannot collide on counter zero with `EXPLORE` draws, keyed on `(being, tick or sequence-step)` so a replay reproduces bit-for-bit. Use `civsim_core::gauss::gaussian` (`core/gauss.rs:133`) for the mean-zero deviate, since it is already counter-keyed and tail-bounded. Exploration is the homeostatic drive to minimize surprise, trading off against survival: a well-predicted action draws near-zero variance, a surprising one widens the search along the surprising dimension.

- **Dependency.** Piece 1 for the felt delta the prediction is scored against, and piece 2 for the parameterized sequence whose dimensions the error biases.
- **Guards.** *Determinism:* all noise is a pure function of `(master_seed, being, phase, counter)`, never wall-clock or camera; the new Phase must be registered (no borrowing `EXPLORE`), and every draw folds into `state_hash`. *Principle 8/9:* the exploration drive is a homeostatic pressure the evolved controller weights act on, never a subtracted term in the heading; the error dimension comes from the sequence params, never a coded "try material X" rule. *Principle 11:* the prediction-error threshold and the exploration variance gain are reserved-with-basis (section 3.5).
- **Milestone.** A being whose forward model mispredicts an action's outcome widens its motor variance along the mispredicted param and, over ticks, converges its prediction toward the felt outcome, so exploration falls as surprise falls, all replay-identical.

### Piece 4: BELIEF-GRAPH SEARCH (planning and insight)

Given a goal, search the belief graph for a path. Insight is finding an untraversed path; innovation is combining beliefs from disparate domains.

**Slice 4a (READ, hash-neutral): the generic planner.** A generic path search over `BeliefPool`/`InferenceFrame` in the cognition tier, whose heuristic is drawn ONLY from belief confidence (`knowledge_level`, `belief.rs:165`) and frame support, with a reserved planning depth cap. No search over beliefs exists today; this is new bookkeeping over the shipped store, reading no domain knowledge.

**Slice 4b (WIRE, opt-in, hash-changing): the grounding gate on consumption.** A received or recalled template becomes an executable candidate ONLY when the receiver's own sensorium currently perceives affordances that bind its wildcard slots, mirroring `avoidance_gradient`'s subject-match test (`learn.rs:239-244`), and only through a heritable action-bias weight lifted off founder-zero by selection. This gate lives in the planner's consumption step, NEVER in gossip; transmission stays "carry any committing `(subject, attr)` frame," with no branch on the new attr. A hearer acquires a template by presence but can act on it only where its senses find the matching affordances, so social spread stays emergent and an idea still dies out where no holder stands and no matching context is perceived.

- **Dependency.** Pieces 1 and 2 for the templates the graph is built from; the shipped gossip channel for their spread (`world.rs:3400,3449`).
- **Guards.** *Determinism:* the planner is a pure deterministic search over a `BTreeMap`-backed store in canonical order, drawing no RNG (any tie-break is by id or a hashed total order); its output folds into no new hash surface beyond the candidate it selects. *Principle 8/9:* the heuristic reads only confidence, never a goal-to-action table; the consumption gate keeps a taught template inert until the receiver's senses bind it, so no belief injects a behavior. *Principle 11:* the planning depth cap is reserved-with-basis (section 3.5).
- **Milestone.** A being reaches a goal reserve by traversing a belief path it did not itself execute end-to-end (an untraversed combination of two learned templates), and a co-located naive being, taught the template by the shipped gossip, acts on it only once its own senses bind the wildcard slots, never on receipt.

## 3.5 RESERVED VALUES, EACH WITH ITS BASIS

Every constant the loop needs is the owner's to set, surfaced with the basis on which he would set it, fail-loud on unset, never fabricated (Section 7 of `CLAUDE.md`). The mechanism is fixed Rust; these are the numbers.

- **Reward-noise floor** (the positive-delta threshold in slice 1a). Basis: the largest per-tick reserve RISE ordinary metabolism and recovery produce (the resting recovery rate scaled to the tick), the sign-mirror of the harm-noise floor's basis (`homeostasis.rs:802-805`), so only a supra-recovery rise registers as reward.
- **Reward likelihoods** `p_reward_given_rewards`, `p_reward_given_neutral` (slice 1a). Basis: the base rate of a felt reserve rise on a truly-beneficial sequence versus a neutral one, the reward mirror of `p_harm_given_harms`/`p_harm_given_benign` (`learn.rs:103-108`), set as the harm arc sets its likelihoods; the `certainty_clamp` reuses `evidence.log_odds_clamp` by construction, so a single credit cannot exceed the engine ceiling.
- **Reward-credit rate** (the eligibility-trace decay, the TD lambda, slice 1b). Basis: the interoceptive lag between an action and its felt reserve rise the physiology already implies, bounded by the existing retention or transmission loss window the belief system forgets on, so credit reaches back only as far as the substrate already remembers. It enters through the existing `weight` argument, so it fabricates no new engine constant.
- **Affordance-percept quantization granularity** (slice 2a). Basis: the sensorium's per-channel just-noticeable difference for the derived affordance scalar (`percept.rs:143-149`), coarse enough that ordinary spatial variation in a material reads as one or two affordance kinds rather than a continuum, the same basis as `feature_granularity`.
- **Prediction-error threshold** (slice 3b). Basis: the interoceptive noise floor already defined for reward and harm, so a predicted-versus-felt delta below the being's own perceptual resolution is not read as surprise; a performance-and-resolution bound, not a realism one.
- **Exploration variance gain** (slice 3b). Basis: the existing `EXPLORE` heading-noise scale and the sensorium JND, set so the gain maps a unit surprise (in reserve-delta units) to a param perturbation on the order of one perceptual step, and a well-predicted action draws near-zero variance.
- **Belief plasticity** (the learning rate, throughout). It reuses the EXISTING heritable `Mind::plasticity` (neutral at one, `learn.rs:187`), so no new constant is fabricated; it is named here only to record that the reward learner scales its credit by the same evolved plasticity the harm learner does, never a bespoke reward learning rate.
- **Planning depth cap** (slice 4a). Basis: the per-tick cognition budget and the depth at which the marginal gain of a longer plan falls below the belief-confidence noise, a performance bound rather than a realism one, surfaced for the owner and left unset until profiled.

## 4. DETERMINISM AND STEERING GUARDS (on every slice)

Non-negotiable, and the reason the arc rides the shipped substrate rather than a new one.

- **Everything is float-free.** Weights are `Fixed`, log-odds totals are i128 bits, `ln`/`exp` are the pinned CORDIC and series integer kernels (`evidence.rs:305,238`). Accumulation is a plain sum, worker-width- and reduction-order-independent (R-REDUCE-ORDER), with the clamp applied only at read (`evidence.rs:170`) so order-independence never breaks. Frame hypotheses are sorted and deduped canonical, `Mind.beliefs` is a `BTreeMap`, and every belief folds into `Mind::state_hash` (`agent.rs:328`). The arc adds nothing that perturbs this.
- **New per-being state folds into `state_hash` in canonical order, empty-by-default.** The eligibility trace (piece 1), the sequence-in-progress and templates (piece 2), and the forward-model state (piece 3) each fold after the world fold, in canonical id or sequence order, exactly as `ReserveMemory.prev` does (`runner.rs:2132`), and each is opt-in via an empty default registry or trace (`PerceptRegistry::empty`, `ReserveMemory::is_empty`), so a scenario that does not opt in replays bit-for-bit.
- **The crediting path draws NO randomness.** Piece 1 is RNG-free, inheriting the harm learner's discipline. The sequence-subject mint is a pure, canonical, RNG-free bit-pack in a reserved band (the `feature_subject` idiom), so two beings that discover the same affordance-typed sequence mint the IDENTICAL `StableId` and hash identically; otherwise gossiped copies would diverge.
- **Only the sampling and exploration draws use RNG, counter-keyed and phase-separated.** Pieces 2 and 3 draw through `DrawKey::entity(being, epoch, Phase::X).rng(seed)` (`core/rng.rs:94`) under NEW registered Phase variants (never borrowing `EXPLORE`), keyed on `(being, tick or sequence-step)`, folded into `state_hash`, and use `gauss::gaussian` for a mean-zero deviate. Transmission stays randomness-free; keep the discovery loop's RNG out of the gossip beat.
- **The four emergence guards, one per piece.** (1) No coded technique verb: "chopping" is a high-confidence belief PATH, never an enum arm; the sequence subject is minted from quantized affordance scalars, structurally identical to `feature_subject(channel, bucket)`. (2) Raw-feature percepts: affordance scalars derive from `derive_capabilities` over matter's geo/mat, a `[0,1]` scalar keyed by a physics id, never a class name; graspability and fracture-potential enter as data-defined kernel entries, never `if is_graspable`/`Fracturable` branches. (3) Primitive-sequence beliefs: templates are wildcards over affordance predicates, credited only by the interoceptive delta, so the belief is a description of what worked, never a designer's recipe. (4) Generic search: the planner's heuristic reads only belief confidence and support, never a domain goal-to-action table. Across all four: the reward sign is the being's own `ReserveMemory::delta`, ONE reward attr serves every sequence, and the grounding gate keeps a taught template inert until the receiver's senses bind it and a heritable weight lifts it off founder-zero, so no belief injects a behavior (mirroring `avoidance_gradient`, `learn.rs:204-244`).
- **Reserved constants are fail-loud from the manifest.** Every value in section 3.5 defaults to a sentinel that refuses to build if used unset, never a silent plausible default (`HarmLearningCalib::from_manifest`, `learn.rs:119`). The agent surfaces the number with its basis and stops.
- **Tooling gate.** `cargo fmt --all` before every commit (CI enforces `--check` on main), `cargo clippy --all-targets`, and `verify.sh` green. The consensus-roadmap stop hook requires updating `docs/working/CONSENSUS_ROADMAP.md` (each item's gate cited) whenever source or design files change.

## 5. HONEST LIMITS AND COUPLINGS

This arc does not stand alone. Name the seams so they are not silently assumed.

- **The harm-learning arc is the aversive twin this arc extends.** The appetitive learner is the sign complement of `is_harm_tick` (`homeostasis.rs:806`) on the SAME `InferenceFrame`/`consider`/`commit` engine (`evidence.rs:155,181`) and the SAME `feature_observations` -> `Stimulus::Observe` runner path (`learn.rs:180`, `runner.rs:1479`), keyed on `REWARD_ATTR`/`REWARDS`/`NEUTRAL` disjoint from `HARM_ATTR`/`HARMS`/`BENIGN`. It reuses the shipped gossip transmission with no new channel, because a rewarded sequence-belief is an ordinary committing `(subject, attr)` frame `committed_beliefs` already fans out (`agent.rs:242`, `world.rs:3449`). If the harm substrate's subject-mint or the evidence engine changes, this arc moves with it; keep the two attrs and their subject bands disjoint so a reward belief and a harm belief never alias.
- **The materials arc supplies the affordances this arc makes usable.** Piece 1's credit signal needs an enacted affordance that produces a reserve change to correlate against. `INGEST` already has an enactment arm and raises a reserve (`locomotion.rs:838`), so the appetitive learner is demonstrable today on ingestion; crediting grasp, extract, craft, dig, and geophage requires the materials arc to replace the `_ => idle` catch-all (`locomotion.rs:869`) with pure physics-law enactment arms, and to expose nearby matter's `geo(axis)`/`mat(axis)` accessors so piece 2's affordance scalars can be derived. Until those land, only ingestion and any already-enacted affordance are exercisable, and the affordance-binding half of piece 2 has thin matter to bind. That dependency is upstream and firm.
