# R-DEEPTECH reserved decisions queue

The owner decisions the north-star R-DEEPTECH cluster audit surfaced
(`audits/deeptech-northstar-2026-06-29.md`). Each is a research-and-design
choice the four open sub-questions need, surfaced with its basis and never
decided by the agent. These are distinct from the calibration manifest
(`calibration/reserved.toml`), which holds numeric calibrations for resolved
mechanisms; these are the design choices for items still open, and they graduate
into the design document only as each sub-question is resolved under the standard
workflow. No value here is fabricated, and the physics catalogue contents (the
axis set, the law forms, the tiers, and every number) stay owner-authored and are
not invented in this file.

Each entry: the decision, then the basis on which to decide it. The basis leans on
already-resolved mechanisms wherever one exists, so the choice is informed and
consistent rather than arbitrary.
