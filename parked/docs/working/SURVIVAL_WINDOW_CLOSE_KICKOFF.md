## The four levers, grounded at source

The metabolic draw DERIVES from the being's own body. `physiology.rs:599-639` (`base_drain_from`) is the Kleiber basal rate over the being's own mass plus the thermoregulatory heat loss over its own surface, bridged to a reserve fraction; `homeostasis.rs:495-518` (`metabolize_derived`) applies the per-being derived vector. The only authored pieces are the two owner anchors, `metabolism.kleiber_coefficient` and `metabolism.body_mass_kg_scale`, each reserved-with-basis. It is derive-clean.

The taxis gathering rate DERIVES. The per-tick intake (`physiology.rs:654-685`, `physical_intake`, called from the `INGEST` arm at `locomotion.rs:1658-1764`) is the physical food content through the body's own mass and storage-density bridge, bounded by the reserve room (satiation) and the cell supply. The derived-taxis floor rates (`locomotion.rs:1184-1290`) derive from the being's own resting drain and the reserved `harm.noise_floor`. The load-bearing limit: the floor changes only WHERE a being walks, not the intake per bite, so it can steer a being onto food but cannot make a bite exceed the burn when the food scale sits below the drain scale.

The founder endowment DERIVES from the body. `homeostasis.rs:348-375` (`Homeostasis::new`) sets each reserve capacity from the development-weighted sum of the organs' tissue composition on that floor axis (the `bio.energy_density` of fat-body, glycogen-store, and viscera, `anatomy.rs:616-643`), and the reserve starts full. The capacity is a normalized composition quantity, not a reserved constant. It is derive-clean.

The reserve-drain-versus-food-scale calibration is the crux, and it is an authored-reserved mismatch the source documents explicitly. `food_energy_density = 3000` (`locomotion.rs:195`) is the placeholder that bridges the abstract-productivity supply to the reserve joules the Kleiber drain is paid in, its basis stated as "set so a being's forage intake at a plausible standing density offsets its Kleiber drain (the calibration target: the dev world survives)." On a real-composition cell (`is_real_composition`, `locomotion.rs:1728`) the 3000 multiply is skipped and the content is the supply directly, which in `living` is the derived carbon-fixation energy, at the real `bio.energy_density` scale (kJ/g, at most about 38) or the roughly 21 W/m^2 fixation flux, some two orders of magnitude below the 3000 the drain was calibrated against. `worldbuild.rs:464-474` names the consequence: "a grazer starves until the owner recalibrates the reserve-drain scale to the real food scale (measured in --scenario living: it collapses by starvation; surfaced, never tuned, as no autonomous value may author the survival outcome)."

## The lever the evidence names

Three of the four mechanisms derive correctly from the being's own body: the drain, the intake, the endowment, and the taxis floor rates all key on the being's mass, surface, tissue, and drain, never an authored gather rate or survival knob. The one unresolved element is a single authored-reserved calibration: the absolute-energy scale bridge between the derived food and the derived drain. The derived Kleiber drain is paid in reserve joules anchored to the old 3000-scaled abstract food; the derived fixation food is delivered at its real physical scale, two orders of magnitude smaller; the two are not in commensurable units, so a grazer's maximum per-tick gather at the real scale cannot exceed its per-tick burn. This is exactly the fixed starvation curve, invariant to food volume (a too-small energy density times any volume factor stays below the drain) and to the per-bite anchor (the anchor is bypassed on the real-composition cells the founders eat, which is why the anchor diagnostic on background food moved nothing).

So the lever is the reserve-drain-versus-real-food-scale calibration, the gate's option (a), an owner value surfaced with basis, not a mechanism defect to rebuild. This reconciles both earlier findings and matches the source's own explicit statement that the window closes on an owner recalibration.

## The (a-prime) derivation-hunter proof: the absolute-energy chain closes from the floor

The gate ruled (a-prime), the derive-clean joules bridge, and requires an exhaustive proof that the chain closes from the floor before anything is reserved. Traced at source, both sides reduce to joules with only measured, per-world, or fundamental coefficients, and the outcome-authoring `food_energy_density = 3000` is removed.

The reserve full-store is physical. A being's total reserve energy is `E_reserve = capacity_norm * body_mass * tissue_energy_density` joules: the normalized development-weighted organ composition (`homeostasis.rs:348-375`), the body mass from morphology, and the tissue `bio.energy_density` (the measured Atwater value, kJ per gram). All three key on the being's own body; none is a survival knob.

The drain is physical. Per tick a being burns `burn_J = (P_basal + P_heat_loss) * tick_seconds` joules: the Kleiber basal power `P_basal = kleiber_a * mass^(3/4)` watts (`physiology.rs:612`, the measured Kleiber coefficient over the being's own mass) plus the thermoregulatory heat loss over its own surface and emissivity (`physiology.rs:615`, whose radiative term carries the CODATA-derived Stefan-Boltzmann sigma), across the one-second base tick. The drain FRACTION `metabolic_drain_fraction` (`physiology.rs:631`) is exactly `burn_J / E_reserve`, dimensionless and already physical.

The intake is physical once the food carries joules. The reserve gain (`physiology.rs:654-685`, `physical_intake`) is `gain = content * assim * eta / (body_mass * tissue_energy_density)`, that is `content_J / E_reserve` times the assimilated fraction, the fraction of a being's full store one bite refills. For `gain` to be the correctly-scaled reserve fraction, `content` MUST be in joules. This is the crux the whole window turns on: on the abstract path `content = supply * food_energy_density` supplied the joules through the `3000` placeholder (tuned so the dev world survives), while on the derived path `content = supply` directly, but the derived supply is the carbon-fixation FLUX (watts per square metre), not joules. That dimensional gap, a flux where the intake needs an energy, is the whole mismatch; the `3000` was papering over it with a survival-tuned scalar.

The food joules derive from the floor. The energy a cell's fixation delivers per tick is `E_food = fixation_flux * cell_area * tick_seconds` joules: the derived carbon-fixation rate (watts per square metre, from the measured photosynthesis constants over the real insolation flux), times the cell area, times the tick. The cell area is `tile_edge^2`, and the tile edge derives (`locomotion.rs:88-93`) as `real_walking_speed * tick_seconds / base_speed`, the spatial scale one real creature's speed and the tick fix. So `E_food` bottoms out in the fixation flux (measured photosynthesis constants plus the per-world solar constant), the real walking speed (a measured 1.4 m/s), the base-speed spatial resolution (a per-world engine datum, not a survival knob), and the tick (the floor unit). No convenience scalar survives.

The fix, then, is to carry the derived producer food in joules-per-cell (`fixation_flux * cell_area * tick_seconds`) so `content = supply` is already the physical energy the intake math needs, and to retire `food_energy_density` from the armed real-composition path entirely. The intake fraction and the drain fraction then compare in the same physical joules, and Mirror's survival becomes a derived result rather than a tuned one, whatever that result is.

Every coefficient in the closed chain, classified: measured in a lab (the five photosynthesis constants, the Kleiber coefficient, the tissue Atwater energy density, the assimilation and ingestion efficiencies, the real walking speed); per-world or floor-unit (the solar constant, the base-speed spatial resolution, the one-second tick); fundamental (Stefan-Boltzmann sigma from CODATA). The one removed: `food_energy_density = 3000`, the outcome-authoring placeholder. No coefficient in the chain tunes the survival outcome; the survival outcome falls out of them.
