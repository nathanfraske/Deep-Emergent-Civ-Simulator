# Consolidated North-Star Audit: The R-DEEPTECH Cluster

## Executive summary

The R-DEEPTECH cluster is not five parallel research items. It is one dependency chain. Composition (Part 41, resolved) is its spine, the physics substrate is its floor and its ceiling, and the four open siblings (emergent science, the discoverable physics substrate, emergent prerequisite depth, and deep-time scale) plus the non-templatedness proof sit above it. The climb hangs together if and only if a single quantity propagates coherently through every level: a measured physical consequence, carried as a fixed-point integer interval, content-addressed, memoised, and conserved across the two-tier boundary.

The honest verdict, decomposed, is that the lower half of the climb is reachable now and the upper half is unsolved, and the boundary between them is set by emergent science and by physics-substrate completeness rather than by any flaw in how the pieces connect. Composition plus the cultural ratchet plus a small physics floor produce functional convergence and divergence on simple-to-moderate multi-part artifacts, and this rests on well-supported literature (Raup and McGhee morphospace convergence, Lehman-Stanley novelty, Mouret-Clune illumination, Hornby-Pollack generative reuse, DreamCoder compression promotion, the Tomasello and Henrich ratchet) and on resolved internal substrates. The recursive climb to complex technologies that are new in kind is partly reachable, with the deep claim unproven and hitting the known open-endedness ceilings. The full north-star, dawn-of-sentience craft climbing emergently and deterministically to the modern and far-future tier, is unsolved.

On the two central fears the cluster survives only with stated fixes, and both residual risks are high. The reason is structural and shared: the convergence audit's metric and the authored substrate share one coordinate basis, so a steering leak inside the physics reads as physics-forced; and content-addressing amplifies any one residual nondeterminism into an irreversible fork over deep time. Neither fear is defeated by the defenses already in place, and neither is fatal once the named fixes are built. The architecture is, in the owner's own standing, a synthesis of proven components rather than a proven whole, accepted as possibly unreachable and pursued for its own sake and for the paper-grade material a partial result yields.

## R-DEEPTECH-SCIENCE: knowing-how to knowing-why

Verdict: partly reachable.

The strongest grounded path treats a theory as a content-addressed symbolic program over the physics substrate's own axes and laws, the same bounded-but-extensible floor pattern as the four already-resolved substrates. It is induced by bounded stochastic search over a theory grammar, driven by counter-based RNG, from the regularities the resolved evidence engine already accumulates, and accepted by an integer description-length test, which is the BACON and DreamCoder line. It is validated by physics-as-teacher: a theory predicts a measured-consequence interval and is corroborated or refuted by the same integer log-odds accumulator that scores any belief, with Kuhnian and Lakatosian paradigm inertia mapped onto the existing R-AXIOM entrenchment gate rather than scripted. It is consumed as a widened proposal distribution at the conceive-intent step, so a held theory lets an agent target a physics-predicted-but-unobserved consequence. Representation, induction for shallow theories, and validation are reachable on established components. Each reuses settled machinery rather than inventing a new paradigm, which is the cluster's central structural discipline.

The hardest obstacle is twofold. The conceive-intent efficacy claim, that theory-enabled extrapolation opens design leaps tinkering cannot reach, is the central unproven north-star claim and may be unreachable: the bounded search over the theory grammar might collapse back into re-sampling the adjacent possible. Compounding it, the inducible-observable basis and the theory grammar are authored one level below where the convergence audit looks, theories have no external morphospace whose convergence has a real-world referent the way artifact morphospace borrows convergent evolution, and the description-length selection pressure rewards exactly the regularities the authored basis makes compressible. A grammar that gives the designer's expected law-form a short code is a steering leak wearing the costume of parsimony, and the documented Schmidt-Lipson caveat (an induction prior that presupposes Newton's second law) is the concrete precedent. Mathematics-as-pure-relational-theory has no physics observable to validate against, so Principle 9 has no enforcement there unless a formal theory is admitted only when exapted across two or more physics-grounded scopes.

## R-DEEPTECH-PHYSICS: the tiered, deepening substrate

Verdict: partly reachable.

The guide's locked representation is sound and should be kept: axes and closed-form integer interaction laws, substances as points, a bounded-but-extensible floor, magic on the same floor, every axis referencing a Part-55 dimension so law kernels are dimensionally checkable. Four law-form families map cleanly onto interval-bounded integer arithmetic: dimensional-analysis monomials, effective-theory tiering where a tier is a regime of validity rather than an unlock, reduced-order proxies baked offline from expensive float simulation then frozen as integer data, and engineering-handbook closed forms. Tier-opening becomes emergent reach-into-a-regime via instruments that are themselves composition nodes, gated by the already-stated physics-equals-reach bound, not by an authored schedule. Magic is an authored cultural input under Principle 9, which is permitted, bounded by a regime predicate and interval bound like any axis.

The hardest obstacle has a permanent part and an authoring part. The permanent part is the categorical reach boundary: transient and chaotic dynamics have no canonical closed form and survive only as offline-baked or wide-interval proxies, which is a surfaced capability ceiling rather than a defect. The authoring part is the master single point of failure of the whole cluster. The only mechanical neutrality test (an axis reduces to a base dimension) is blind to which dimensionally-valid quantities are present, at what resolution, and with which non-unique law form, since Buckingham Pi cannot rank or uniquely fix the dimensionless groups. The forcing field the convergence audit regresses against is computed from the same law set it audits, so a leak inside the physics reads as physics-forced, and the descriptor-invariance backstop is blind to a leak in a direction common to every reachable chart. Tier-as-regime relocates the unlock into the band-minus-threshold ratio, a free dial that authors rung spacing. Magic axes evade the only mechanical check by construction. Authoring-by-omission is invisible because the completeness criterion is checked against the composition proxy demand it co-defines.

## R-DEEPTECH-DEPTH: emergent prerequisite structure

Verdict: partly reachable.

Depth is mostly a read-out of the resolved composition piece rather than a new mechanism. The by-id child-reference graph is the prerequisite graph, generated rather than declared, and reachability is a pure deterministic function returning the composition nodes whose every child reference resolves to a held promoted primitive and whose leaf demands (materials, joins, physics axes and laws, and pending science, held theories) are all present. This is Kauffman's adjacent possible computed over culture state and Arthur's combinatorial recursion, with Simon's stable-intermediate-form requirement operationalised by the three-gate promotion. It wires into conceive-intent as a checked articulation precondition, not a scripted unlock: an agent can imagine a wild intent but cannot articulate-and-fabricate a composite whose validated children do not exist. The transistor's twin material-and-theory chains are the real-world model of necessity-gating.

The hardest obstacle is that the audit discriminator is upside-down. It flags edges that recur across seeds without a forcing dependency, but a conception-bias ladder produces edges that do have forcing dependencies, since the child is in fact needed, so it passes clean. The real ladder hides in the negative space, the physics-permitted compositions that the conception generator's observation-weighting and the authored descriptor space never let any seed conceive, and there is no statistic over it. The predicate over held state is a tautology over the past and can never certify that a deep target is reachable, so non-deadlock is an empirical property of the not-yet-authored physics, provable only by the deep-time extension of the Stage-14 test. Most deep prerequisites are processes and methods rather than artifacts, so the sub-question cannot fully resolve until Inconsistency 5, the Part 23 versus Part 41 technique-origination contradiction, is settled. A process is a non-commutative carried-state fold with often path-dependent behaviour, not the verb form of the same memoisable noun evaluator, and admitting that is part of the resolution.

## R-DEEPTECH-SCALE: deep-time aggregate running

Verdict: partly reachable, with a shaky core claim.

The strongest path reuses the Part 54 lifting and restriction operator pair under its conservation-plus-seeded-schedule guarantee, the Part 58 conserved-projection registry, and the Part 41 content-addressed memoised evaluator, specialised to technology and science state. A culture is carried at the pool tier as a compact library of promoted content-addressed ids plus per-culture transmission state plus, pending science, theory belief-mass-plus-accuracy; the quality-diversity search archive is promoted-tier-only working state and is not carried. Quiet cultures advance by a cheap closed-form integer coarse step, the equation-free coarse-projective-integration discipline. The determinism core is reachable: integer-associative reduction sidesteps the float-reduction hazard, the memoised content-addressed surrogate is drift-free by construction, and overflow routes through the per-quantity scale registry.

The hardest obstacle is that the load-bearing analogy fails at the one place it must hold. The lift and restrict operators must commute on a quantity that has no sufficient statistic. What a culture can invent next is the composition-graph adjacency, which a bag of ids plus scalars discards, and innovation yield is combinatorial-nonlinear, so exact aggregation in the Leontief and Theil sense requires a linearity it lacks, and the projective-integration literature gives commutation only under a clean fast-slow mode separation that a punctuated climb does not have. Worse, the schedule is a fixed point of the coarse stepper it drives, a recurrence Part 54's reproducibility proof never covered because that proof is scoped to a fixed tier sequence, so the tier sequence is not shown to resolve to one per seed. Pool-tier innovation cannot mint a content-addressed id because the design bytes to hash live only at the detailed tier. Belief mass crosses the boundary as a truncating mean, so the stability gate that decides promotion drifts non-conservatively over millennia. These are not flavour; they are the seam where determinism and steering coincide, because a convergence you cannot reproduce you cannot attribute to physics.

## The non-templatedness proof: the statistic and the experiment

Verdict: partly reachable, as a necessary screen and not a sufficiency proof.

The recommended acceptance battery has four parts, all fixed-point, deterministic, and run through the harness across a thread-count sweep. Part A reports a shape-and-depth statistic vector (maximum depth, depth distribution, branching and reuse-fan-in distributions, a content-id-aligned graph-edit distance) and a morphospace point cloud over physics-grounded axes only (occupation, sum-of-variances and sum-of-ranges disparity, occupied-region overlap, centroid distance, coverage, and quality-diversity score). Part B is the falsification gate, built on two nested nulls: a physics-off scrambled-constants null and a Brownian drift-only transmission null. A pass requires both that real convergence beats both nulls where physics constrains and that real divergence matches the drift null where physics underdetermines, with the graphs differing in shape and depth beyond label noise. The proof is falsified, and the architecture not trusted, when a region shows high cross-seed convergence with low physics-predicted forcing above the reserved steering threshold and that residual survives the descriptor-invariance check, or symmetrically when graphs are templated where physics underdetermines. Part C is the memoisation test (deep evaluation costs in the new nodes alone, repeat evaluation yields zero new cache misses) and Part D the compression-gate test (sub-linear library growth paired with a ratchet-liveness check so a stalled gate is not mistaken for a compressive one).

The killer obstacle is that the bare battery is conditioned on an artifact it cannot itself produce. Every statistic is binned along the authored axis set, and the physics-off null randomizes only law constants while holding the axes and kernel forms fixed, so it is blind to a steering leak in the choice of axes and functional forms, which is where the most sophisticated authored attractor lives. The only defence is to lift the battery into a descriptor-invariance and law-form-competition test: recompute the verdict under several independently-parameterised bases (including a learned descriptor frozen offline on an independent corpus, never trained online on the run) and under a competing cited law kernel, requiring physics-forced convergences to be invariant. Add a negative-space probe and a conception-layer probe to catch the ladder that hides in what no seed conceived. The convergence-detection literature itself warns that distance-based metrics conflate convergence with stasis and misidentify divergent lineages as convergent, so the framing must be defended by a null rather than asserted. The battery certifies only artifact technologies until Inconsistency 5 gives processes a morphospace, and it cannot witness an omission leak.

## The two central fears

### Fear 1: authored emergence

The cluster survives this fear only with stated fixes, and the residual risk is high. The structural defenses are real for the easy half: the content gate refuses an authored sword, recipe, or theory; the provenance requirement catches an artifact from nowhere; the per-culture stability gate makes trajectories diverge by people for free; and the composition spine is the field's one robust escape from the artifact-complexity ceiling. For artifacts, whose descriptors have an external referent in real convergent evolution, a leak in the chart drawn over the physics is detectable once the audit is lifted to descriptor-invariance.

It is high rather than moderate because the hard half is currently unfalsifiable under the very audit meant to falsify it, and the design overclaims that it is closed. The deepest hole is that the forcing field and the convergence statistic are both computed from the authored law set, so a leak inside the physics is by definition explained away and reads as physics-forced; there is no physics-independent oracle inside the engine, by Principle 9, and the descriptor-invariance backstop cannot see a leak common to every reachable chart, which a physics-floor leak is by construction. Emergent science is worse than the artifact case because theories have no external morphospace and the description-length surrogate rewards the regularities the authored basis makes compressible. The honest posture is that the cluster authors an admitted, surfaced capability ceiling and a bounded bias channel, and that given the axis set the convergence audit can certify convergence within the spanned space is physics-forced once it runs competing forms and re-projected bases. It cannot today certify convergence-is-physics-forced unqualified, and the project should stop claiming parity of guarantee, which Part 61 does not yet do.

### Fear 2: determinism at deep-time scale

The cluster survives this fear only with stated fixes, and the residual risk is high. Every individual break has a named fix already in the project's own backlog, none requiring a fabricated value or authored physics, which is the case against severe. The risk is high rather than moderate for three compounding reasons. The deepest bite, the lift and restrict commutation on a corpus with no sufficient statistic plus the schedule being a fixed point of the coarse stepper it drives, is not a backlog sweep but a mathematical property that must be engineered into existence and then proven by a harness that does not yet exercise it, and the multiscale literature says the natural approach does not give commutation without a spectral gap a punctuated climb lacks. Content-addressing makes the system unforgiving, amplifying any one residual nondeterminism into an irreversible non-re-converging fork over millennia. And the failure is currently unfalsified, because the harness as scoped checks only order-independent per-entity accumulation and the corpus that would expose the failure is the one quantity the projection registry has not registered. Calling the composition spine determinism-resolved overstates its status while its named non-associative combine, the product of efficiencies, sits unpinned. Survivable, but only by structural work and proof that do not yet exist.

## Prioritized recommendations

The high-priority work is structural and clusters around the two shared failure modes. Register the technological and scientific corpus as an exact id-identity conserved projection before any deep-time running is trusted, since the corpus is both the quantity that would expose the determinism failure and the quantity whose reproducibility the steering audit depends on. Close the product-of-efficiencies fold order before declaring the spine determinism-resolved. Lift the convergence audit from in-space clustering to a basis-independence and law-form-competition test, the only defence against a physics-floor leak reading as physics-forced. Prove the deep-time schedule is a pure function of seed and world by pinning the scheduling-loop order and confining theory induction to one canonical tier. Resolve the pool-tier id-minting gap structurally. And downgrade the stated guarantees in Part 61 to match the evidence, since the prime directive to prove before trusting requires ending the parity-of-guarantee claim.

The medium-priority work hardens the remaining seams: the external negative-coverage completeness ledger, derived-not-drawn tier-opening with a tier-order invariant, the negative-space and conception-layer depth probes, the demotion cascade with a fidelity-coupled critical mass, and the resolution of Inconsistency 5 and Inconsistency 6. The low-priority work constrains the predictive-kernel grammar to the pinned operation set, admits mathematics only by cross-scope exaptation, and verifies the marked literature claims against their named sources.

None of this moves the ceiling. The ceiling is owned by the open mechanisms the resolved spine depends on and feeds, and at least one mechanism-to-mechanism interface, the connection from science to the conceive-intent step, is itself unspecified rather than merely unproven. The cluster hangs together below that joint and is open above it. That is the genuine and useful state of the north-star: a coherent design with a visible load path, reachable at the bottom, unsolved at the top, and worth pursuing for the partial result and the paper-grade material it yields even if the full climb proves unreachable.
