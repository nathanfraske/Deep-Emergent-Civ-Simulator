# Audit and Remediation Log
### Companion to emergent_civ_simulator_design.md

## Status

The first audit classified every component in the design document. After review, Section A (the being model) was signed off with one correction, the items in Sections C and D were dispositioned, and this pass acted on them. Where a fix was decided or clearly correct, it was made. Where the right answer needs a session, the part was flagged in place and the question recorded in the Research Backlog below rather than guessed. This file now tracks both: what is fixed, and what is still open. This pass also codified the rule the whole audit serves as **Principle 11** in Part 0: everything even remotely worth tweaking is data-driven unless data-driving it carries massive implications or tradeoffs, which is exactly the test that decides whether an item is remediated immediately or sent to the backlog for a session first.

## Why this exists

The design document was written across many sessions, and in writing it, concrete implementation detail (struct fields, enums, fixed counts, specific models) was committed as though settled when much of it was synthesis that was never researched or brainstormed. The hardcoded `Needs` struct was one instance, caught in conversation. This log is the sweep that followed, kept current as items are remediated or sent to research.

## Methodology

About 140 type and constant definitions were inventoried. Each was placed in a bucket: grounded (basis on record), engine mechanic (code-defined is correct), fixed this pass (hardcoded converted to data-driven), model needs a brainstorm (data-driven now but the representation was never researched), queued content enum (closed world-content set that should be data-driven), inconsistency (two parts disagree, or a part contradicts its own thesis), or tuning constant (defensible but uncalibrated). The dividing line is engine-mechanic versus world-content: how the simulation runs may be closed code; what the world is made of must be data.

## Coverage

All 64 parts were swept. Prose that asserts a fixed model was checked against the code it describes. Items not named below fell into "grounded" or "engine mechanic" and are summarized in the Grounded section.

### 1b. Further remediations this round

- **Vitals is now part-level (your decision).** The single `Vitals.health` scalar was removed. An individual's physical condition is the part-level body (Part 35), built from the race's data-defined anatomy (Part 40) and reached by a `BodyRef` handle. Body parts carry a data-defined `kind` (`PartKindId`), data-defined `functions` (`FunctionId`), and part-level `damage`; aliveness derives from vital-part loss and fluid pools, and any aggregate health figure is derived, never stored as a competing truth. Parts 2 and 35 updated; runs at the fidelity each individual warrants (Principle 1).
- **ShapeKind is now an open, re-wireable registry.** The dramaturg's story shapes are no longer a fixed enum; the set is loaded from a config so shapes can be added, removed, or re-wired without engine changes (Part 29).
- **Fauna feeding is now data-driven.** `AnimalSpecies.diet` (a closed category) became `feeds_on: SmallVec<[FoodSource; 4]>`, a list of what the species draws on, read directly by the food web, so an exotic feeder is a data entry rather than an engine change (Part 17).
- **Flora feeding is now data-driven.** `PlantSpecies` gained `draws_on: SmallVec<[GrowthInput; 4]>`, so growth and competition read what each plant feeds on (light, water, soil, or an exotic input like a prey pool for a carnivorous plant or an arcane field for a mana-fern) from data, rather than assuming sun, water, and soil for every plant (Part 16).
- **`Q32.32` reconciled with Part 55.** Part 3 now states the single global scale as the default, with per-quantity scales for wide-range or accumulating quantities.

### 1i. R-EVIDENCE consolidated (belief formation from evidence, generalized and data-driven, signed off)

### The north-star ambition: open-ended technological evolution from the dawn of sentience (R-DEEPTECH cluster)

This is the project's declared north-star ambition: a world that begins at the dawn of sentience with stone-age craft and, through nothing but emergent need, conception, physics, and cultural accumulation, can climb across deep time toward the modern and the far-future tier, races that raise cities, then industry, then computers, then rockets. The owner has accepted that this may not be achievable and is worth attempting regardless, both for its own sake and because even a partial result is paper-grade material, and has asked that it be documented thoroughly now and taken super-deep when reached. It is recorded here as a cluster of researchable problems rather than a vague aspiration. Nothing in it is to be answered by invention; every approach must be grounded in research and in precedent, and every numeric or design specific is reserved.

The honest standing, stated in Parts 41 and 61, is the starting point. The mechanism's shape is right and already specified: technology is conceived from need and judged by physics, the design search is a quality-diversity search that illuminates a space rather than climbing to one target, and the cultural ratchet (high-fidelity transmission plus innovation accumulating capability across generations, writing collapsing copying error, loss below a critical practitioner mass) is the compounding engine that in reality carried craft from flint to silicon. Functional convergence on simple artifacts is well-supported by the literature; the open-ended invention of complex, multi-part technologies hits the complexity ceilings that literature reports and is partly unsolved, and the design demands the central claim be proven at small scale (Stage 14) before it is trusted. This cluster decomposes the climb from simple artifacts to the modern tier into four core problems and one cross-cutting integrative problem.

- **Composition (R-DEEPTECH-COMPOSE): resolved.** A technology is a content-addressed composition node (integer and enum), a leaf bottoming out at the data-defined primitive-material-join floor or a composite referencing validated child modules by stable id, with a fixed-width interface vector; the conceived intent is the emergent need-driven intent of Part 41 and never an authored enum (first hardening). A validated module is promoted to a reusable primitive by three gates together, physics validation, stabilisation in the per-culture cultural ratchet, and compressive reuse, the conjunction bounding the combinatorial explosion and the stability gate making trajectories diverge per people for free (per-race differentiation). Evaluation is a memoised bottom-up pass aggregating integer interval vectors by typed combinators from a data-defined extensible registry (second, main hardening, since the report fixed them at four), plus data-defined emergent system-level proxies and a graded interface-mismatch penalty. The interface axes, leaf floor, combinator registry, and proxy set are the etic grounding floor of composition, sibling to the value, semantic, and institution-function substrates and extensible with the physics (third hardening). Decision in Part 41, record in Part 62.10; reserved calibrations in section 1l and the Part 41 blockquote. The contact with technique origination is the unresolved Inconsistency 5. The four deeper cluster siblings (R-DEEPTECH-SCIENCE, R-DEEPTECH-PHYSICS, R-DEEPTECH-DEPTH, R-DEEPTECH-SCALE) remain open.

- **R-DEEPTECH-SCIENCE. The emergence of abstract, explanatory, mathematical knowledge as a compounding enabler.** Modern technology is derived from understood principles, not found by tinkering: a rocket comes from understood combustion and orbital mechanics, a computer from understood electromagnetism and logic. The engine models practical craft (technique know-how) and factual belief (about events and the world), but not the growth of general explanatory theory, the compression of observed regularity into a principle that predicts and generalises beyond the cases seen, or mathematics as a tool that opens design spaces. Without emergent science, the technological ceiling is roughly pre-industrial craft, so this is likely the deepest single addition the upper reaches require. The obstacle is representing abstract knowledge that generalises, can be tested against the world and so can be wrong and revised, and enables design leaps tinkering cannot reach, all emergently (no authored theories) and deterministically: the move from knowing-how to knowing-why. Decide how a theory is represented, how it is induced from observation and validated or refuted against the world, how it gates and expands the conceive-intent step of Part 41 (a theory is in effect an emergent compression over observed physics that widens what intents an agent can conceive), and how theories spread, drift, and are lost through the same transmission machinery as any knowledge. Ground in the philosophy and history of science (the structure of theories, explanation versus prediction, Popperian testability, Kuhnian paradigm shifts, Lakatos research programmes), the cognitive science of theory formation and the child-as-scientist or theory-theory account (Gopnik and colleagues), computational models of scientific discovery and theory induction as program synthesis (the Bayesian and program-induction views of concept and theory learning, and the historical automated-discovery systems such as BACON and successors), and the role of measurement and instruments; this connects directly to the resolved epistemics work, R-AXIOM (how a culture decides what is true) and R-EVIDENCE (how beliefs about the world form from observation and inference), and to Parts 9 and 37. Note that game and simulation precedent is essentially absent, since existing titles use a research-points tech tree rather than emergent science, so this is a research frontier with little to copy. The red and green audit sharpened the obstacle: the conceive-intent efficacy claim, that a held theory opens design leaps tinkering cannot reach rather than re-sampling the adjacent possible, is the central unproven north-star claim; theories carry no external morphospace whose convergence has a real-world referent the way artifact morphospace borrows convergent evolution, so the steering audit has weaker purchase here; the description-length selection pressure rewards exactly the regularities the authored theory-grammar makes compressible, so a grammar that gives a designer's expected law-form a short code is a steering leak in the costume of parsimony; and pure mathematics has no physics observable to validate against, so a formalism is admitted only when it is exapted across two or more physics-grounded scopes and refused otherwise as a reach ceiling (the BACON line of computational discovery and the Schmidt-Lipson implicit-prior caveat by Hillar and Sommer, both verified).
