## THE RESIDUAL LAW (the missing-EDGE detector, symmetric to the Gap Law)
The Gap Law audits NODES, not EDGES: it fires when a computed quantity sits near a degeneracy, and is SILENT when the model graph is missing an arrow. The seam-ledger history proves it: nearly every reviewer catch was a MISSING EDGE or module, not a value gap (atmosphere->tribology coupling, thermal tides torquing spin, the multi-body prerequisite for the secular spectrum, the conservation wiring, the ionosphere spending magnetization before magnetism existed, contact mechanics absent). 1. **Conservation:** the L/E double-entry generalizes to EVERY conserved current; any unexplained ledger residual is a missing-edge flag (the Earth-Moon 10% closure inverted from validation into a detector).
2. **Disequilibrium:** any steady state far from the disposer's equilibrium must CITE a named freezer barrier or a live flux; uncited disequilibrium means a missing mechanism (Lovelock's biosignature argument turned inward on the engine).
3. **Fluctuation-dissipation:** every dissipative coefficient must have its fluctuation partner and vice versa (Green-Kubo, Johnson-Nyquist, Einstein D-eta); damping without noise is declared incomplete.

**Honest limit:** BUDGET-NEUTRAL couplings escape all four (thermal tides conserve everything and merely move an attractor; only the regression battery catches them, and only for observable worlds).
