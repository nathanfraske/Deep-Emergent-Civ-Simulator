## The cascade (each rung: what derives, from what, and its seam)

**Orbital and seasonal (G plus geometry, no calibration).**
- Kepler's third law `T^2 = 4*pi^2*a^3/(G*M)` gives the year from the planet's orbit and the month from a satellite's orbit. Inputs: G (floor), the semi-major axis `a` and the central mass `M` (per-world state). This is the orbital-period north star already flagged: never re-author one year equals 365 days over it.
- Obliquity plus spherical trigonometry give the seasons and the day length: the subsolar latitude is the obliquity times the orbital-phase sine, and the day length follows from the sunrise hour angle (`cos H = -tan(latitude)*tan(declination)`). Zero parameters. SEAM: the obliquity itself is a per-world initial condition, not derived (it is set by the world's formation and impact history), so it joins the authored IC vector below.
- Insolation is the stellar luminosity over the inverse-square distance times the zenith-angle cosine, integrated over the day and the year. Derived once `L_star` (below) and the geometry are in hand.
- The equilibrium tidal bulge scales as `h ~ R*(m/M)*(R/d)^3` (the tidal potential over the surface gravity). Derived from the masses and the geometry, one order-unity factor. SEAM: the DYNAMIC ocean tide departs from the equilibrium bulge by basin resonance and geometry; the equilibrium form is the derivable floor, the resonant amplification is basin-specific.

**Stellar (the star's whole output from its mass).**
- Wien's law `lambda_max = b/T` is fully derived from Planck: the constant `b = h*c/(x*k_B)` with `x = 4.965` the transcendental root, so it is not a new authored constant. Star colour emerges from the surface temperature alone.
- The surface temperature is Stefan-Boltzmann over the star, `L = 4*pi*R_star^2*sigma*T^4`, with sigma already derived in relation (`2*pi^5*k_B^4/(15*h^3*c^2)`). So `T_star` derives from the luminosity and the radius, both of which themselves derive from the stellar mass (the mass-luminosity and mass-radius relations from the homology argument). The star's whole spectrum follows from its mass.
- The mass-luminosity relation `L ~ M^3.5` follows from hydrostatic equilibrium plus radiative transfer (the homology argument). SEAM (owner-flagged, confirmed): the exponent shifts with the opacity regime (about 3 for electron-scattering at high mass, up toward 5 for Kramers at low mass), and the opacity law carries a composition dependence, so the slope is derived up to the opacity regime rather than a single universal exponent.
- Main-sequence lifetime `tau ~ M/L ~ M^-2.5`: the time budget a world gets, derived. This is the clock on how long a biosphere has to evolve, and it hands the simulation its outer temporal bound.

**Atmospheric retention (the biggest single payoff, and it keys on the periodic table).**
- Jeans thermal escape compares the escape speed `v_esc = sqrt(2*G*M/R)` to the thermal speed `v_th = sqrt(2*k_B*T/m)` per species; the escape flux carries the derived factor `exp(-v_esc^2/v_th^2)`, and the practical rule is that a species is retained over geologic time when `v_esc >~ 6*v_th`. So WHICH gases a world keeps derives from its mass, radius, and exospheric temperature plus the molar masses from the periodic table: hydrogen and helium escape a warm Earth-mass world, nitrogen, oxygen, and carbon dioxide are retained. Atmospheric existence and rough composition become an output, not an authored fact.
- SEAM (semi-authored, owner-flagged): the exospheric temperature where escape happens is set by the stellar UV heating, not by the surface temperature, so it is the one semi-authored input. A derivation lead is below.
- SEAM (added by me, then corrected by the owner against measurement): Jeans is the THERMAL floor; the non-thermal channels are hydrodynamic blow-off (already in the stack via the energy-limited formula), sputtering (Sigmund cascade theory makes the yield derived in form, `Y ~ E_dep/U_0` with `U_0` the cohesive-energy anchor `E_b`), ion pickup, and photochemical escape. Photochemical escape is fully table-derived: dissociative recombination `O2+ + e -> O + O` releases `IE(O2) - D_0(O2) = 12.07 - 5.12 = 6.95 eV`, so 3.5 eV per atom, against the escape energy `(1/2)*m*v_esc^2` of 2.1 eV at Mars, 8.9 at Venus, 10.4 at Earth: one inequality sorts the three planets correctly with zero new numbers, and hot O from this channel is the operative escape at Mars. The correction: the magnetic field is NOT a shield with a known sign. Measured escape rates are similar for magnetised Earth and unmagnetised Venus and Mars (roughly 0.5 to 2 kg/s), and a planetary field can ENHANCE ionospheric escape (Gunell et al. 2018; Ramstad and Barabash 2021; Strangeway), so the field is a CHANNEL SELECTOR (pickup and sputtering off, polar-wind and cusp outflow on), not a retention shield. Mars' thin air owes at least as much to its 2.1 eV gravity gate as to its dead dynamo.

**Acoustics and weather (fluid dynamics from the same constants).**
- The speed of sound `c = sqrt(gamma*R*T/M)` has zero free parameters: `gamma = (f+2)/f` derives from the molecular degrees of freedom (the same degree-of-freedom count the volatile-thermodynamics `c_p` uses, so the two substrates agree by construction), `R = N_A*k_B`, and `M` is the molar mass from the periodic table. Minor caveat: the vibrational modes freeze out at low temperature, the same equipartition caveat the `c_p` cascade already carries.
- Coriolis `f = 2*Omega*sin(latitude)` is exact kinematics, geostrophic balance follows, and the Held-Hou angular-momentum-conserving argument derives the Hadley cell width (why the subtropical deserts sit near 30 degrees), scaling inversely with the rotation rate. So the rotation rate `Omega` becomes a proc-gen climate knob: a slow rotator gets a single pole-to-equator cell, a fast rotator gets banded jets. SEAM: Held-Hou is the axisymmetric ideal and over-predicts the cell intensity because it omits the eddy fluxes; it is the derivable floor, the eddy correction is the refinement. Its inputs (tropopause height, equator-pole contrast) are themselves emergent from radiative-convective equilibrium, so it chains.
- Rayleigh scattering follows from Maxwell plus dipole radiation: the `1/lambda^4` wavelength dependence is exact, so the blue sky and the red sunset are derived. SEAM (corrected by the owner): the ABSOLUTE cross-section (the sky's brightness and optical depth) needs the molecular polarizability, but that is not a bare measured datum. It DERIVES from the first ionization energy (a periodic-table column) through the Lorentz oscillator `alpha ~ e^2/(4*pi*eps_0*m_e*omega_0^2)` with `h_bar*omega_0` the IE, at tens-of-percent grade, bounded exactly by the Thomas-Reiche-Kuhn sum rule, with hydrogen an exact theorem (`alpha = (9/2)*a_0^3`). So the colour and the magnitude both derive from Z plus the IE column, with the refractive index (Clausius-Mossotti) and the sky brightness sharing the one datum, and the King anisotropy correction the few-percent residual.
- Hurricane intensity is a Carnot engine: Emanuel's potential intensity gives `V_max^2 = (C_k/C_d)*((T_s - T_o)/T_o)*(k_star - k)`, a derived ceiling from the air-sea thermodynamic disequilibrium and the Carnot efficiency, with one fitted enthalpy-to-drag ratio `C_k/C_d` (owner-flagged, confirmed as the single empirical residue here).

The decisive point about layer 4: the values are authored contingency, but their EVOLUTION, their ATTRACTORS, and even their PRIORS are derived, which is what makes it proc-gen rather than hand-authoring. ## A lead on the stellar-UV question

The exospheric heating is the stellar XUV and EUV flux, which tracks the star's magnetic activity, which tracks its Rossby number (the rotation period over the convective turnover time). Young, fast-rotating stars sit in the saturated regime (the XUV-to-bolometric ratio near 1e-3), then the activity decays as roughly `t^-1.5` as the star spins down (gyrochronology). So the stellar UV is semi-derivable: the SHAPE (saturated then decaying) derives from the stellar structure plus the spin-down law, and only the saturation ratio and the decay exponent are empirical coefficients. That turns "author the exospheric temperature" into "derive it from the star's mass and age up to two cited coefficients," which is the same pattern as the rest of this map.

## Stellar UV and the exospheric temperature: the closure

The owner's follow-on research closes most of the semi-authored exospheric-temperature seam. The whole thing is a three-iteration scalar solve per planet: the planetary response to a given UV flux is fully derivable, and only the stellar SUPPLY splits into a derived form plus a few empirical amplitudes.

Planet side, fully derivable. Where the flux deposits is the optical-depth-unity altitude, the column `N = 1/sigma`, with the EUV photoionization cross-section order-derivable as the geometric atom `sigma ~ pi*a_0^2 ~ 9e-17 cm^2` (good to a factor of several; the refinement is either authored or the uncertainty accepted). On Earth that lands at 120 to 150 km, exactly the thermospheric heating peak. How much of the flux becomes heat is the efficiency `epsilon`, semi-derivable from the photon energy ledger: a roughly 25 eV photon spends about 15 eV on ionization (much of which leaks back out as airglow) and the roughly 10 eV photoelectron thermalizes, so `epsilon ~ 0.3 to 0.5`, bounded by accounting rather than fitted freely. The floor is the radiative skin temperature, the mesopause near `T_eq / 2^(1/4)` (about 15 percent off observed, a cheap closure if one can be found). The closure to `T_inf` is that in steady state all the deposited heat conducts downward, using the same Chapman-Enskog thermal conductivity the gas-transport arc already carries.

The one flagged branch is the thermostat that explains Venus and Mars: if the thermosphere is carbon-dioxide-dominated rather than oxygen-and-nitrogen, it radiates at 15 micrometres rather than conducting, and `T_inf` collapses, which is why Venus and Mars both sit at 250 to 300 K aloft despite their enormous differences. This branch must be included; it is a composition-selected competition between conductive and radiative cooling, not an authored switch, so it derives from the atmospheric composition (itself an output of the retention budget).

Stellar side, the supply, the part that splits. The coronal temperature scale is self-regulating at the point where a thermal Parker wind ignites, so it takes one solar calibration anchor and then propagates. The time dependence derives from the magnetized-wind braking torque scaling as `Omega^3`, which integrates down to the observed Skumanich spin-down (`Omega ~ t^(-1/2)`). Activity then hands `L_EUV` its derived FORM; the decay exponent refinement and the `L_X/L_bol` saturation amplitude (near 1e-3 for young fast rotators, lower for the modern Sun) are the surfaced empirical numbers.

Two seams to add. First, the geometric `sigma` is a single number, but the real photoionization cross-section falls steeply above threshold and carries per-species edges, so the DEPOSITION ALTITUDE is spectrum-dependent (soft EUV deposits high, hard X-rays deep); the single `sigma` is right for the TOTAL heat that sets `T_inf` and rough for the altitude profile, which is the refinement to author-or-accept. Second, the same derived `L_EUV(M_star, age)` is doubly load-bearing for atmospheric retention: it sets `T_inf`, hence the Jeans escape parameter, AND it directly drives energy-limited hydrodynamic escape for the lightest species, so the stellar-UV derivation feeds both escape channels from one quantity.

## The magnetosphere and space weather: one dipole number

One optional per-world number, the magnetic dipole moment, plus the stellar-wind machinery already derived, yields the whole space-weather layer. The stellar wind (the Parker wind whose strength and time history the closure above fixes) meets the planetary dipole at the magnetopause standoff, the pressure balance between the wind ram pressure and the dipole field, which sets the auroral-oval latitude and the storm dynamics. From there: auroral ovals, storm-time radio blackouts (which couple straight back to the Radio Age chain below), and Carrington-class grid events as the extreme tail, all on a frequency set by the star's age (a younger, more active star delivers more frequent extreme storms). The flare energy-frequency distribution is a power law `dN/dE ~ E^(-alpha)`, so once `alpha` and the age-set activity are in hand, the rate of super-threshold Carrington-class events is derived, never scripted.

The dipole is DERIVED, not authored, and the chain is known: the authored isotope column feeds the radiogenic decay law, which sets the core heat budget, which drives the dynamo when the core-mantle-boundary heat flux beats adiabatic conduction (the on/off is a derived thermal criterion; the residue is the core iron conductivity `k_core`, unsettled at 20 to 150 W/m/K, the Pozzo versus Konopkova disagreement, which even bears on Earth's young-inner-core paradox). The field MAGNITUDE follows the Christensen-Aubert buoyancy-flux scaling (one fitted prefactor spanning Earth to Jupiter to fast M dwarfs, famously rotation-independent in the strong regime, with the rotation rate instead gating dipolar versus multipolar geometry), and the magnetopause standoff is exact pressure balance `R_mp = R*(B^2/(2*mu_0*rho_sw*v^2))^(1/6)`, about 8 to 10 Earth radii for Earth. So the dipole steers the stellar wind into the auroral ovals, sets the grid-event exposure a technological civilization inherits, and SELECTS the escape-channel mix (rather than shielding, per the correction above). The full chain reads: authored isotope column, decay law, core heat, dynamo on/off, magnetosphere geometry, escape-channel mix, with `k_core` and the stellar wind mass-loss history `M_dot_star(t)` (Wood's astrosphere scaling) as the flagged residues.

Grounding to confirm the chain: Rishbeth and Garriott for the ionosphere and the Chapman-layer physics, Hunten (1987) and Donahue (1982) for atmospheric escape, and Luger and Barnes (2015) for the XUV-driven escape and its habitability coupling.

## The emergent payoff: derived technological eras with historical texture

The reason this map matters for an emergent civilization: the derived planetary and stellar substrate turns whole technological eras into EMERGENT, derived affordances rather than authored tech-tree nodes (Principle 8, carried all the way up).

The Radio Age rides on the same EUV cascade. The ionosphere is a Chapman layer: the EUV deposition at optical-depth unity sets the electron density, and the radio-reflection cutoff is the plasma frequency `f_p = (1/2pi)*sqrt(N_e*e^2/(eps_0*m_e))`, built from three fundamental constants (`e`, `eps_0`, `m_e`) and the derived electron density. So over-the-horizon shortwave skip, its day-night swing (the layer thins at night as recombination wins with no EUV), its solar-cycle modulation (more EUV at solar maximum lifts the usable frequency), and the flare-driven radio blackout all fall out of the same `L_EUV(M_star, age)`. A world's radio propagation carries real historical texture, none of it scripted.

The Space Age gets a derived throttle. The atmospheric scale height `H = kT/(m*g)`, the thermospheric density the EUV heating sets (so the thermosphere swells at solar maximum and drags satellites down faster, a real historical effect), the orbit and escape delta-v from `sqrt(GM/R)`, and the reentry heating are all outputs of the per-world state, so the difficulty of spaceflight is derived: a high-gravity or thick-atmosphere world faces a harder or foreclosed Space Age, a low-gravity thin-atmosphere world an easier one.

Atmospheres diverge over time and write history as they do (the R-COEVOLVE north star made concrete): outgassing, escape, and biological modification move the composition, which feeds back through the carbon-dioxide-versus-conduction thermostat, the retention budget, and the climate. The atmospheric history becomes an emergent narrative coupled to the biosphere, life changing the air and the air changing what life can do.

The honest boundary holds (Principle 9): the physics supplies the AFFORDANCE and the throttle, what is possible and how hard, with its historical texture. Whether a civilization discovers and exploits the ionosphere or reaches orbit still has to EMERGE from the beings, their cognition, tool-use, accumulated knowledge, and coordination, which is the rest of the project. The physics is the derived substrate the history is written ON, never the history itself.
