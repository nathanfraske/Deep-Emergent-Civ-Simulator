# Threat-and-flee emergence: making the predator-avoidance pole emerge (design-first)

This is the design opener for the follow-on arc the gate ruled after the creature-selection-loop arc (#144). That arc completed the creature reproduction-and-behaviour-selection loop and proved the APPROACH pole of the predator-prey reaction: a founder-zero resource-density weight is driven to the adaptive pole under a real fitness gradient, control-isolated and deterministic (the matter-channel scavenge-and-forage proof). This arc builds the other pole, the FLEE: a founder-zero being-signal weight carried AWAY from a harmful emitter under selection. Design-first, grounded against source, posted for the gate's ruling before any code. No mechanism here.

## Why this is a separate arc (the seam the #144 threat probe surfaced)

The #144 arc attempted the threat-avoidance pole on the same selection harness and surfaced a real seam rather than a result, verified at source: the authored predator is causally INERT in the stepped selection path. A predator placed at range and a run with no predator at all are BIT-IDENTICAL at every generation, so the predator has zero effect on the prey. Pinned precisely: a predator placed CO-LOCATED with the prey does cull one (the strike and the whole-body INTEGRITY cull work), so the range inertness is not the wound. It is that a being cannot perceive the predator: `spawn_predator` never seeds the predator's `body_temp`, so its thermal emission is absent and the being-directed percept reads nothing from it. Without perception there is no toward-or-away-predator signal for a being-weight to select on, so the flee gradient does not flow. Two seams block the clean pole, and both deserve a proper build rather than a bolt-on:

## Seam 1: derive the predator's emission from its own physiology (the #120 emission seam, derive-first)

A being emits a perceivable signal because it is a warm body: the being-percept keystone computes `radiant_emission(body_temp)` from a being's core temperature. The authored predator emits nothing because its `body_temp` is never established. The fix is NOT to seed an authored temperature onto the predator scaffold (that would author a value in the path of world content, the value-authoring line). The predator's `body_temp` must DERIVE from its own physiology, the same thermoregulation a real being runs (its metabolic heat against its medium exchange), so its emission is a consequence of what the predator IS, never a stamped number. If the authored predator lacks the physiology to derive a body temperature (it is a non-metabolizing environmental given by construction, so it maintains no core temperature of its own), that absence is the substrate question this arc must answer: either the predator is given a real (data-defined) thermoregulating physiology so its emission derives, or the hazard is modelled as an emitter whose signal derives from a declared physical property it does carry (a heat source's own temperature, read the same way any emitter's is). Whichever, the emission is a physics read, and any value it needs is surfaced reserved-with-basis and run through the derivation-hunter, never invented.

## Seam 2: land the 2b per-feature discrimination on the being channel

Even once the predator emits, the magnitude-only being channel (the B3 percept armed in #144) has a prey perceiving EVERY emitter by signal magnitude, including other prey. So a prey can sort emitters only by "stronger versus weaker", the magnitude proxy, never "predator versus prey". The clean flee pole ("avoid the predator specifically") needs the 2b per-feature discrimination (amendment 3's flagged follow-on): the being-signal weight keys on a distinguishing physical FEATURE of the emitter (an optical or thermal feature the predator carries and prey do not), so selection can separate the harmful emitter from a harmless neighbour. The 2b percept-feature floor is built (#126); this arc arms it on the being channel for the threat case and lets the discriminating weight emerge under selection.

## The emergence proof (on the harness #144 built)

Once the predator emits (seam 1) and the being channel can discriminate it (seam 2), the flee emergence proof reuses the selection arena the matter-channel proof established: a symmetric zero-mean seed on the being-signal weight (half toward the emitter, half away), the reproduction beat armed, and the authored predator as the fitness gradient. Under the gradient (predator present, emitting, discriminable), a lineage whose weight carries it AWAY from the predator survives the strike-to-death loop and breeds, so the population-mean weight is driven to the avoidance pole; in the matched HELD CONTROL (no predator, or the predator absent) the same regime leaves the weight at its zero seed. The contrast is the proof that selection moved it, deterministic and replayed bit-for-bit, the same rigor the approach-pole proof met. The honest scope holds: nothing about approach-versus-flee is authored, the sign emerges from selection, and a lineage that evolves neither response is a valid outcome.

## Hard constraints (carried from #144 and the owner's rulings)

Nothing about approach-versus-flee is authored: the sign and strength of the being-signal weight emerge from cross-generational selection (Principle 8, Principle 9). The predator's emission is a physics read (derived from its own physiology or a declared physical property), never a stamped temperature (the value-authoring line). Admit-the-alien: a non-fleeing lineage is a valid selected outcome; the emission and the discrimination key on the being's own data. Active HUNT of live prey (approaching a live emitter to kill it) stays scoped to the magnitude proxy or the per-feature discrimination this arc lands, never a two-way sort the channel cannot deliver.
