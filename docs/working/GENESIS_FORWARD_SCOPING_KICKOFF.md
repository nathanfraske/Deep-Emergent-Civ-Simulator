# Genesis-forward foundational arc: the scoping kickoff (design-first)

This is the kickoff for the owner's overnight priority: stand the Mirror world up from the beginning, in
causal order, each layer built on a verified one beneath it, rather than the middle-out patching that
produced the soil-bootstrap deadlock. It is the design-first opener; the deliverable that follows here is
the full PLAN OF ATTACK, which the gate gates before a line of it is built.

## What this arc is, and is not

This is a CODE-TO-SPEC arc, not research. The hard research, derivation, and reasoning are already done and
cited in the working docs (the atmosphere pipeline verified against Catling and Kasting, the geology
packet, the periodic-table-as-cache map, the physics floor, and the design parts). The work is to
translate settled design into Rust, slice by slice, standing each layer on the verified layer beneath. The
failure mode the owner named is reading the scope as an open-ended research mountain; it is not one. The
opposite failure, the one that produced the soil deadlock, is standing a middle layer up on an unbuilt one
beneath it, so the plan's whole point is the causal order.

I own and drive this arc and hold the main thread. Once the plan is gated, the gate carves the
parallelizable disjoint-file slices to B as the overnight speedup while I keep the main thread. The plan
itself is single-threaded scoping by me (the standing cost directive: no spawned multi-agent workflow).

## The causal order (the stages the plan details)

The plan of attack details each stage in this order, and for each: exactly what needs standing up, in what
order within the stage, what it depends on beneath it, what is already built, what the spec reserves (a
value surfaced with its basis, never invented), and where a real gap needs flagging rather than stalling
the whole.

1. The Sun and what it needs in Mirror: the stellar source, its luminosity, and the flux the whole energy
   budget hangs on. Current substrate: `DiurnalSky` carries a `solar_constant` flux ANCHOR that the
   insolation and the #156 photosynthesis already read, so the flux is present as a constant; stage 1 is
   deriving that flux from the stellar source rather than anchoring it.
2. Early Earth and its geodynamics, as researched and derived. Source: `GEOLOGY_ARC_PACKET.md` (and the
   `GEODYNAMICS_ARC_PROPOSAL.md` it deepens), the emergent minerals, rocks, ores, and the geodynamic
   substrate the #156 mineral-weathering floor already touches at its base.
3. The orbit and cycles. Axial tilt and seasons already ride in `DiurnalSky::mirror` (Earth's 23.44-degree
   obliquity, the declination term, the synodic hour angle); this stage extends that to the full set of
   orbital and rotational cycles the climate rests on.
4. Atmosphere emergence, via the full arc. Source: `EMERGENT_ATMOSPHERE_PIPELINE_DERIVE_MAP.md` (verified
   against Catling and Kasting), the atmospheric composition and column the surface-energy and hydrology
   arcs currently take as reserved or absent (the `{x_i}` composition the volatile-saturation arc found the
   world does not yet carry, the greenhouse column the surface-energy arc flagged).
5. Only then the rest, standing on the four verified layers beneath.

## Discipline

The plan is a gated deliverable: no code until the gate gates it. Both guardrails hold at every stage:
where the spec RESERVES a value, it is surfaced with its basis and never invented to finish the spec; where
a real GAP is found, it is flagged rather than stalling the whole arc. Each major milestone gets the
section-9 lenses run once by me (the cost directive; the gate runs the adversarial audit). The build, when
it starts, is byte-neutral-or-stated per slice, with each re-pin enumerated for the owner and never tuned
to a target.
