# Geodynamics arc proposal: making the world's geology and geography come alive

This is the owner-requested scoping (2026-07-10, overnight): what is needed to place into the roadmap so the world's geology and geography (erosion, volcanism, biomes, rivers, lakes, continents, mountains) come alive rather than sit as static worldgen. It was produced by a six-agent derive-first scoping workflow (one agent per subsystem plus a synthesis), each grounded in `PHYSICS_FLOOR_REGISTRY.md` and the live worldgen and environ code, judged against Principles 8, 9, 10, 11, admit-the-alien, and determinism. It is a scoping, not a build: it names the arcs, the floor growth, the shared substrate, and the decisions that are the owner's, and it fabricates no value.

## Headline: the floor already reaches most of the way

The per-cell physics this whole domain needs is largely floored and verified: `law.thermal_buoyancy` (the Boussinesq convection driver, the engine of mantle convection), `law.buoyant_force` (Archimedes, so isostatic elevation is a direct read), `thermal_diffusivity` and `law.conduction` (the geotherm), the strength axes (`yield_strength`, `fracture_strength`, `compressive_strength`) that failure reads, `saturation_vapor_pressure` and `evaporation_rate` (the solvent cycle), and `radiative_equilibrium` (the energy balance). The live machinery is present and running too: `environ.rs` steps the solvent cycle, salt advection, and downhill routing as pinned integer stencils, and `EarthworkField` plus `recouple_terrain` already carry an elevation-delta ledger and re-route drainage over a changed surface (off the run path today).

So the work is NOT new physics. It is three things. First, UNFREEZING the base elevation (today pure fractal noise, transported over as the frozen worldgen elevation) into a resident field that tectonics writes, erosion and hydrology carve, and biomes read for slope and aspect. Second, growing a small set of source terms and rheology kernels the floor lacks (internal-heat production and its decay, solid-state creep viscosity, a pressure-dependent solidus, resident crustal-thickness and strain fields). Third, standing up the largest new determinism surface in the project, an iterative fixed-point convection and flow solver, against the R-GPU-CANON-PIN canon.

## The load-bearing question: reconciling geological time with sim time

A mountain cannot visibly rise in a lifetime, but an earthquake can end a city in a tick. One unified three-tier hybrid strategy across all five subsystems:

- **Tier 1, accelerated worldgen spin-up.** Before play, run convection to plates to crustal thickening to isostasy to erosion to drainage to climate at a coarse spatial LOD over deep simulated time, relaxing to a quasi-steady present so the world opens with continents assembled, ranges at convergences, graded dendritic drainage, lakes at their sills, and equilibrated climate zones, all earned rather than painted. The stop criterion is physical (an approximate uplift-versus-erosion or radiative-convective steady state) but MUST run under a FIXED iteration cap tested by an integer Fixed tolerance, never an unbounded until-converged loop, or replay and worker-invariance break.
- **Tier 2, live coarse-LOD background.** Advance the slow fields every N ticks: plate drift is sub-tile per generation, erosion and deposition become small perturbations on the near-steady state, biome edges track the moving equilibrium. Low-resolution field passes, never per-agent.
- **Tier 3, event-driven punctuation.** The visible, felt layer at human timescale: earthquakes, eruptions, landslides, floods, lake-outbursts, as discrete threshold crossings of slowly accumulated state (elastic strain against yield strength, magma overpressure against fracture strength, slope shear against the derived angle of repose).

All three tiers read one shared set of accumulating fields, so a background that has quietly loaded stress for a century is what makes the eventual quake land where and when it does. Recommendation: front-load the slow bulk into Tier 1 so the live tiers carry only small perturbations plus the events a player can witness, and make every cadence and iteration count a reserved performance bound, tick-counted by a pinned counter, never wall-clock or float-convergence gated.

## The shared substrate (build once, ahead of the arcs)

1. **The unfrozen elevation ledger (the hinge the whole domain turns on).** Base elevation stops being frozen fractal noise and becomes a resident effective-elevation = base + delta field, where `EarthworkField` is the delta ledger and `recouple_terrain` re-derives the downhill graph over the changed surface (both present, off the run path today). Promoting it to the single ledger that tectonics writes, erosion and hydrology carve, and biomes read is the hinge, and it is where a within-tick canonical sequence (uplift, then weather, then transport, then deposit, then re-route) must be pinned so every arc reads the previous step's committed surface.
2. **A resident crustal-thickness field [m]**, the quantity convergence and divergence integrate and isostasy reads to give elevation (shared by Arcs 1 and 2).
3. **A sediment (transported-mass) field**, a sibling to the existing salt field, carried through the same conservative double-buffered stencil that already yields salt flats (shared by Arcs 3 and 4).
4. **A prevailing-wind field** (a reduced Hadley-plus-Coriolis parameterization, shared by Arc 5 and any future weather or aeolian arc).
5. **Determinism canon extensions:** the resolved R-GPU-CANON-PIN fixed-point sqrt/div, plus two primitives to build once against it, a canonical connected-components labelling (plate and watershed identity keyed by lowest cell index, worker-invariant) and a fixed-point fractional-power primitive for stream power (currently an unresolved gate, not a floored primitive).

## The five arcs, in dependency order

1. **Tectonics and the lithospheric substrate (built first, everything reads it).** Replace the fractal-noise base elevation with a tectonically-derived relief from an accelerated deep-time genesis convection-and-plate pass: plates, boundaries, continents, and mountains all emergent, then a coarse-LOD live drift plus event-driven quakes and eruptions during play. Floor growth: an internal-heat-production axis [W/kg] with a radiogenic decay law (decay constants floored, isotope abundance per-world data); a solid-state Arrhenius creep-viscosity kernel reading the existing `chem.activation_energy` plus a per-world reference viscosity; a resident crustal-thickness field; a resident tectonic-strain field; the critical Rayleigh number (~1e3) as a universal law-floor constant, sibling to sigma and R. Plates EMERGE as connected coherent domains of the convection-driven surface velocity field; boundary type reads from velocity divergence and shear; elevation is the isostatic `buoyant_force` read over thickness and density. The plate layout is DERIVED from mantle convection, not world data that then moves.
2. **Volcanism and orogeny (depends on Arc 1's plate field).** Decompression and flux melting, magma ascent, chamber pressurisation, eruption, and orogenic uplift at convergent boundaries, all emergent, turning Part 26's Eruption and Earthquake from an authored enum into derived events. Floor growth: a Clapeyron solidus-slope axis [K/MPa] per material; a partial-melt-fraction kernel; a convergence crustal-thickening kernel by mass conservation; an isostasy/Airy kernel; a Rayleigh regime kernel deciding mobile-lid versus stagnant-lid versus no-tectonics. Eruption threshold is the floored `fracture_strength` (read), quake threshold the floored `yield_strength` (read), uplift rate derives from convergence velocity by mass conservation.
3. **Erosion and weathering (the mass-balance sink, coupled to Arc 4).** Live mechanical, chemical, and abrasive weathering deriving detachment from `law.thermal_stress`, `law.corrosion`, `law.dissolution`, and `law.wear` reading a per-column substance and the live climate into a sediment field, transported by the existing conservative downhill stencil, depositing where transport capacity drops, writing elevation deltas into `EarthworkField`. Floor growth: a per-column substrate-material identity keyed to the ground-substance registry (data, extensible), from which erodibility DERIVES (erodibility cannot derive from a bare height scalar, so this lithology field is the load-bearing prerequisite); a detached-sediment field; a per-substance grain-size and grain-density datum; possibly a `law.entrainment` shear gate if Archard wear proves insufficient.
4. **Hydrology (rivers, lakes, watersheds, the coupled transport half of Arc 3).** Rivers, lakes, watersheds, and drainage that emerge from flow accumulation over the downhill spanning tree, deterministic priority-flood depression and sill routing (so lakes fill and overflow), stream-power incision, and grain-sorted deposition, over an erodible elevation and a declared solvent so water, methane, and brine worlds are data rows. Floor growth: a fluvial shear-stress kernel (rho g R S), an incision kernel, transport-capacity and deposition kernels; the solvent as a per-world reference substance carrying its own density, viscosity, surface tension, latent heat, and boiling point (closing the tracked solvent-is-water gap); the fixed-point fractional-power primitive for discharge^m slope^n.
5. **Biomes and climate zones (the emergent read-out).** Climate zones and biomes that emerge live and migrate, opening from an accelerated spin-up steady state, driven by live insolation through the lapse rate, slope aspect, orographic precipitation, and heat and moisture advection, with the biome read out as the emergent species assemblage from the existing `Niche::suitability` rather than the authored `BiomeSet` classifier (demoted to display). Depends HARD on the day-night/insolation arc (#112, in progress). Floor growth: a lapse-rate law Gamma = g/c_p (one fixed-point divide); a surface-normal insolation law; the prevailing-wind field; a heat-and-moisture advection stencil; per-world axial obliquity and orbital eccentricity for seasons.

## The decisions that are the owner's (surfaced with basis, none fabricated)

- **Class A, timescale and performance bounds (one dial family, recurs in every arc):** the genesis deep-time budget, the live-drift/erosion/climate coarse-LOD cadences, the spin-up iteration counts and steady-state tolerances. Basis: the fewest iterations or coarsest cadence at which the process reaches quasi-steady and fires events at the correct historical rate, traded against compute, a performance-versus-maturity bound not a realism one. Cannot be set without a genesis compute-cost profiling pass (not yet done).
- **Class B, per-world material and astronomy DATA (Mirror-calibrated to the real body with reasoning and a citation, alien by data):** mantle and crust reference creep viscosity; radiogenic heat production and isotope abundances; crust and mantle density and composition; the Clapeyron solidus slope; per-substance grain-size and grain-density; the stream-power exponents m and n (near 0.5 and 1, Whipple and Tucker); axial obliquity and eccentricity (Mirror = Earth 23.4 degrees, near-circular); atmosphere composition.
- **Class C, design-intent forks (true owner calls, not numbers):** (1) initial-crust seed versus a genesis differentiation pass; (2) lithology depth, a single surface id versus a vertical stratum stack erosion exposes; (3) incision-law form, the floored Archard wear kernel versus an explicit stream-power law; (4) uplift source, static versus live tectonics versus zero for a peneplain; (5) erosion writes the shared `EarthworkField` versus a dedicated erosion-delta field; (6) worldgen pre-relaxation to maturity versus fully-live erosion from raw noise; (7) the solvent as a declared reference substance versus hardcoded water (admit-alien and the tracked floor gap both push to data); (8) whether the named-biome DISPLAY is kept over the emergent regionality (the label already drives nothing, so presentation only); (9) circulation altitude, a reduced parameterization versus a fuller dynamical wind; (10) erosion live on the run path versus worldgen-only for a first pass.
- **OWNER RULING 2026-07-10 on forks (4) and (6): live tectonics is a LATER arc, and the substrate must not FORBID it.** Build the first pass static (an initial-crust seed, worldgen relaxation to maturity), but the elevation field, the lithology, and the erosion sink must all admit a moving crust added later with no rewrite, so live drift, event-driven quakes and eruptions, and live uplift are a future arc bolted onto an unchanged substrate rather than a re-foundation. The rest of Class C stays open for the owner.
- **Class D, calibrations mapping a derived quantity onto a scale (reserved with a cited basis):** the strain-energy-to-magnitude mapping (Gutenberg-Richter); the ash-to-climate winter severity (consistent with the environ climate stack); the biome-migration hysteresis (the field noise floor versus vegetation turnover, so edges do not flicker); the orographic condensation efficiency (consistent with the existing `hydrology.precipitation_rate`); the channel-defining discharge threshold (a reader and glyph target, drainage density, not a physics value).
- **Class E, a universal law-floor constant, NOT an owner call:** the critical Rayleigh number (~1e3), on the law-constant floor like sigma or R.

Worth stating plainly: a large set of quantities are DERIVED and so not the owner's to set: the quake threshold (= `yield_strength`), the eruption threshold (= `fracture_strength`), the uplift rate (mass conservation from convergence), the plate wavelength (the Rayleigh cell), the angle of repose (from `mech.static_friction`), the lapse rate (g/c_p), and the isostatic elevation (`buoyant_force` over thickness and density).

## How it stays alien-general

Every subsystem keys off per-world MATERIAL, HEAT-BUDGET, and ASTRONOMY data over one shared code path, never a Terran plate count, a hardcoded continent number, or an Earth biome enum. The strongest guarantee is that the tectonic REGIME itself EMERGES from the Rayleigh number (buoyancy against creep-viscous plus diffusive resistance) rather than being flagged: a stagnant-lid Mars or Venus is low internal heat or high creep viscosity dropping the ratio sub-critical, so the lithosphere never fragments and elevation is the isostatic response to an initial crustal-thickness field; a no-tectonics world is the internal-heat axis near zero, a static lid and a peneplain via a zero uplift source; an ice-tectonic Europa carries water-ice's density, melting temperature, and creep viscosity with the internal-heat axis filled from a TIDAL source (heat-per-mass regardless of source), and because the kernels read the actual densities the ice-lighter-than-its-liquid inversion is handled, so cryovolcanism derives from the same overpressure-versus-fracture rule; a Titan methane hydrology is one solvent substance row (methane's viscosity, density, surface tension, latent heat, boiling point), so methane rivers, lakes, and deltas fall out of the same fluvial and saturation laws; a different-gravity world reads its own `mech.gravitational_acceleration` into buoyancy, the Rayleigh number, shear stress, settling velocity, and the eruption threshold, all rescaled with no code change; a binary or tidally-locked world is a data star-list the insolation already sums over; obliquity as a datum gives a zero-tilt world no seasons and a Uranus-like world extreme ones. Each alien world is a data row, and an alien biome (a methane marsh, a chemosynthetic vent field) is a region where the alien conditions co-occur and the alien niche-fit concentrates species, never a painted label.

## Honest limits

This is a scoping, not a build. The mantle convection and Stokes-flow solve is the largest new determinism surface in the project, scoped against the R-GPU-CANON-PIN canon but proven at neither scale nor completeness, and an iterative flow solver may need fixed-point operations beyond the resolved sqrt and div (a matrix solve, an integer-residual convergence test) that are not yet floored. The fixed-point fractional-power primitive for stream power (discharge^m slope^n, m near 0.5) is an unresolved determinism GATE, not an existing primitive; until it is built the incision-law form is constrained to exact-root exponents or deferred, and that constraint feeds the owner's incision-law fork. Depth is a real gap: magma chambers and crustal roots are inherently vertical but the map is z=0 today, so the arc either carries the column as 2.5D per-cell attributes or takes a soft dependency on the flagged volumetric-medium arc; the 2.5D path keeps the alien admissible but is a workaround. Whether the floored Archard wear kernel reproduces realistic drainage density, or an explicit stream-power law is needed, is an empirical question the spin-up must answer. Erosion and hydrology are one coupled mass balance presented as two arcs, and the within-tick sequencing over a moving elevation target is asserted deterministic here, not demonstrated. Every per-world material datum (mantle rheology, isotope abundance, petrologic solidus slope, grain-size distribution) needs its own cited research before any world runs; none is fabricated, so none is ready. The domain resolves much of R-CATASTROPHE by turning Eruption and Earthquake into derived events, but GreatFlood and Meteor remain, and the couplings to Part 12 (ores by province from boundary type), Part 18 (climate baseline and volcanic winter), and `celestial.rs` (tidal heating on a moon) are seams identified, not wired.
