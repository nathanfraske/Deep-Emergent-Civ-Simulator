## 1. Overview

R-BIOSPHERE is the distinct reader that populates a world's living content over the resolved biology-and-composition floor (R-PHYS-BIO) and the resolved Part 25 genome, pool, and speciation engine, re-resolving neither. It runs as a bounded pre-dawn worldgen pass on its own generation counter, seeded by counter-RNG keyed on the world seed, and composes as one pure function: world seed -> region field and abiotic-source profiles (Phase::WORLDGEN) -> a generate-and-validate founder sampler (Phase::BIOSPHERE_SAMPLE) whose candidates are validated in a fixed order (representability, biome-fit, food-web closure, niche openness) with a bounded resample to K_max and a deterministic fallback that never relaxes closure -> a per-tissue composition Substance and a per-race consumer physiology vector drawn at genesis (Phase::GENESIS) and seeded into GenePool loci -> a deterministic radiation over G_predawn generations (drift on the built Phase::EVOLVE, selection through a piecewise-linear environment-to-coefficient kernel feeding the built GenePool::select, founder-fork on Phase::FOUND, speciation and Dobzhansky-Muller growth on Phase::SPECIATE recorded in a dedicated species/lineage registry, and deterministic pool-tier extinction) -> the dawn, where World::seed_dawn_populations promotes the mature pools and sentient races seed fresh (the epoch is flora and fauna only). Closure is the HYBRID: topological at seed time, then Part 15 stock dynamics cull the closed-but-under-supplied pools and log the die-offs as Part 17 extinction history. Every law is a closed-form fixed-point integer kernel with an interval bound formed by checked arithmetic, order-independent, no float in canonical state, no product-before-divide; every membership (axes, edges, relation kinds, incompatibility pairs) is a registry that grows by demand-closure rather than a closed enum; every outcome is a measured quantity, never a stored verdict. The Part 62 record takes the four-paragraph shape (the question R-BIOSPHERE posed: how to generate a whole biosphere without hand-authoring species while keeping it deterministic, data-driven, and per-race differentiable; what was found across the three clusters and the reserved edibility refinements; the decision taken, signed off, general and per-race differentiable, with the HYBRID closure and the forks F5/F6/F7/F9/F10/F11/F12/F17 adopted; and what remains to be proven, the reserved calibrations and the open siblings). The Decided-and-reserved blockquote states the composed mechanism in two sentences, names the fixed Rust (the four kernels, the four new Phases, the closure walk, the stick-breaking additivity spine) versus the data (axes, edges, relation kinds, per-race bindings, the calibration manifest), lists every reserved value with its basis, and names the honest limits and the open siblings.

## 2. The generator (generate-and-validate)

The generator is a seed-keyed generate-and-validate sampler that emits validated points over authored trait axes, never a hand-authored roster. Per niche draw site it keys through DrawKey::entity(niche_locus, generation, Phase::BIOSPHERE_SAMPLE).in_region(region).slot(k), where niche_locus encodes biome, trophic layer, and niche ordinal, generation is the pre-dawn generation index carried in the tick coordinate exactly as the built EVOLVE and WORLDGEN draws do, and slot k is the attempt ordinal so candidate sampling (k=0) and each resample are distinct non-colliding streams on counter zero (the R-RNG-COORD slot rule proven by the keys.rs slot and phase tests). Within one attempt, axis j is drawn at counter j via rng.unit_fixed(j) and mapped onto axis j's reserved [lo, hi) range by lo + u*(hi-lo) with the multiply guarded (checked_mul routed to hi on overflow, so the product never precedes the interval clamp). Validation runs cheapest-and-hardest-first: (i) fixed-point representability (a candidate not representable in Q32.32 is a failed candidate, never coerced); (ii) biome-fit, suitability S computed as the Liebig minimum (the laws.rs net_nutrition min-fold) of per-axis piecewise-linear tent responses r_a over the environmental axis subset (elevation, moisture, temperature from the three terrain.rs normalized [0,ONE) fields, plus soil-fertility read as soil.amount.checked_div(soil.capacity.max(ONE)) matching the Part 15 Stock::pressure guard), where r_a = ONE - d_a.checked_div(w_a) inside the breadth with d_a = (v_a - o_a).abs(), r_a = ZERO outside (d_a >= w_a), and a zero-breadth knife-edge guard, the only divide having d_a < w_a so the quotient is provably in [0,ONE) with no product before it, accepted iff S >= tau_fit; (iii) food-web closure, a monotone least-fixed-point graph walk (no Fixed arithmetic, no RNG) that grounds the region's accepted pool set upward from the present abiotic sources, an edge resolving only against a present source or an already-grounded pool (Vegetation against a grounded producer, Species(t) against a grounded pool in the same region or, for a migratory pool, in the grounded set of a reachable region, Carrion against a demand-closure dead-biomass Stock created only where a web carries the edge), reaching its least fixed point G* in at most |P_r| rounds at O(|P_r| * |E|), a pool outside G* being an orphan, edges resolved against the pool and Part 58 axis registry rather than FoodSource/GrowthInput enum cases (F5, so the source-vector lift is a later data change); (iv) niche openness, the minimum trait/genome distance (the interim biallelic GenePool::distance) to every accepted co-resident being >= the reserved diversity target D_min (limiting similarity). A best-so-far candidate is kept by a strict deterministic total order (higher S, then greater closure headroom, then lower attempt ordinal k), all Fixed comparisons; the first valid candidate is accepted. If no candidate is valid within the hard bound K_max, the fallback accepts the best-so-far candidate that at least closes topologically (closure is the one hard invariant, never relaxed, so the web can never orphan), and if none closed, emits a logged empty-niche event recorded as history. Termination is guaranteed because the loop counter is bounded by the fixed integer K_max and the fallback branch is taken unconditionally at k == K_max. Seeding parameters (founder count per world N_world, founder count per biome N_biome, diversity targets D and D_min, resample bound K_max, biome-fit cutoff tau_fit, and pre-dawn generation count G_predawn) are all reserved-with-basis through the fail-loud calibration manifest, none fabricated.

## 3. The wiring (organism composition and consumer physiology)

Wiring binds each generated organism and each consumer to the built floor kernels without re-resolving them. Every organism carries, per tissue, a Part 58 Substance (a BTreeMap over floor axis ids to Fixed values, content-addressed by the built canonical-walk hash so identical tissues dedup identically on every machine), drawn at genesis under Phase::GENESIS with a per-axis slot(axis_ordinal). A tissue's gross composition is a mass-fraction simplex over the macronutrient axes plus a derived inert residual summing to exactly ONE, and each partial lump that resolves into Tier-1 children (protein into indispensable_aa, lipid into essential_fatty_acid, mineral ash into essential_element) carries a residual child so children plus residual equal the parent exactly. The additivity kernel is a deterministic stick-breaking walk in canonical registry order: remainder starts at the parent value P (<= ONE), child_i = remainder.checked_mul(u_i) with the heritable stick fraction u_i in [0,1] (a None routes to ZERO, and since both operands are <= 1 the product never exceeds remainder), then remainder = remainder - child_i (ordered subtraction, provably >= 0), and residual = the final remainder, so sum(child_i) + residual == P bit-exactly with no divide and no product exceeding P. The hardening seam: the heritable payload is the stick-breaking coordinates u_i, never the absolute children or the residual, because storing children directly and deriving the residual lets independent drift push the residual negative; stick coordinates are closed under independent drift, so the simplex and every lump identity survive selection by construction. Trace-concentration families (toxins) stay SUM-UP (the built saturating_sum, no simplex, no residual), so the resolution direction is per-axis-family registry data, not a hardcoded notion. Consumer physiology is a per-race vector (requirement, assimilation, fermentation, retention, reference_tolerance, hill_exponent, and demand-closure axes) assembled at genesis into a stored heritable vector and evolved thereafter (the confirmed hybrid, F9), never recomputed live. Requirement is the ASSEMBLED read of a per-race RequirementBinding that mirrors the built AppraisalBinding exactly in shape: it maps each nutritive drive (a Part 20 DriveDef) to (nutrient-class AxisId, requirement magnitude, optional allometric coupling) entries, and consumer.requirement[c] is the order-independent saturating_sum over the race's drives that feed class c, with the not-required None sentinel preserved distinct from a stored zero (the built satisfaction() reads None as fully satisfied). Body-size coupling is per-race data honoring Part 20's rule that whether a drive's magnitude relates to body size is a race's choice: the Liebig quality floor defaults size-independent while the absolute-intake (food_need) scaling carries the optional Kleiber coupling, expressed as a reserved piecewise-linear tent CurveId over the body_mass axis approximating M^exponent, the exact fractional-power kernel deferred to R-GPU-CANON-PIN. Digestive capability (assimilation, fermentation, retention) is PRIMITIVE per-race heritable data read directly by the floor kernels via GeneSet::express and clamped to the biology_floor.toml ranges (assimilation and retention in [0,1], fermentation in [0,0.85]), recommended primitive rather than anatomy-derived because R-BUILD-PHYS is open, Part 25 keeps anatomy out of the genome so an anatomy-derived capability could not yet drift while digestion must be heritable (the domestication syndrome, the fermentation microbiome), the floor already resolved the consumer half as heritable data, and an anatomy-to-capability law does not exist and would be a new authored mapping; a forward-compatibility clause lets an anatomy contribution later feed digestion through the express() environment offset with no rewrite. The composition and consumer axes ride one unified axis-keyed genome channel over the Part 58 AxisId namespace (sibling to the built TraitSetpoint(TraitId) channel), the final enum shape (one variant or a Composition/Consumer split) deferred to the F10 cluster that owns the channel addition. The harm-side founder axes (reference_tolerance, hill_exponent) come from a reserved anchoring-taxon figure scaled allometrically, never a human default. The additive mass (children plus residual equals parent, gross simplex equals ONE) is the R-TIER-CONSIST conserved projection registered for the ecology subsystem, remainder to the lowest child id; nonlinear law outputs (harm, available energy) are left tier-resolution-dependent by construction, an honest non-invariance.

## 4. The pre-dawn epoch (radiation, speciation, extinction)

The pre-dawn epoch is a bounded worldgen pass on its own generation counter (F6) that radiates the founder pools into a mature ecology over the built genome engine, with all new state in a dedicated species/lineage registry so genome.rs stays pure mechanism (F7). Selection derives per-locus coefficients fed to the BUILT GenePool::select unchanged (p' = p(1+s)/(1+p*s)) from a piecewise-linear kernel: per environmentally coupled channel C, the mean phenotype xbar_C is the saturating_sum over feeding loci of (p_L*effect1_C + (ONE-p_L)*effect0_C)*weight_C clamped to the axis range; the normalized signed mismatch mhat = checked_div(xbar_C - mu_C, w_C) is taken FIRST and clamped to [-ONE, ONE] (divide-before-multiply, the only quotient bounded before any product); a climate term s_clim = sigma * |mhat| * g_L * h2_C * k_sel is formed by successive checked_mul with every factor except k_sel bounded to [0,ONE]; a pathogen term is the correct monotone ramp s_path = d_L * P * k_path (not a tent); a resource term is the Liebig deficit s_res = -d_L * clamp(checked_div(demand-supply, supply), 0, ONE) * k_res; and s_L is the clamp of their saturating_sum to [s_min, s_max] with s_min >= -ONE + eps so 1+p*s stays positive. At the optimum mhat is zero so only drift acts, making directional radiation versus neutral drift a measured consequence of the map. Speciation is recorded in the registry: per cadence sweep in canonical pool-id order, for a daughter deme and its sister the built GenePool::reproductively_isolated is called (unchanged), and on isolation a SpeciesId is minted by a deterministic monotone counter (mirroring the existing Registry::mint), a parent-pointer LineageNode is recorded, and a Speciation event is appended. Dobzhansky-Muller incompatibilities grow the IncompatibilityTable MEMBERSHIP only (the mechanism and IncompatibilityKind stay fixed Rust): over a diverging pair, candidate complementary locus pairs are enumerated in canonical order over the immutable frequency snapshot, and for each not-present pair a draw keyed on the ordered pair identity and the locus pair on Phase::SPECIATE with distinct slots per roll adds the pair with probability mu_DM (kind Lethal vs Sterilizing by a second slotted draw against severity_lethal_share), the Orr snowball emerging because the candidate count grows quadratically with fixed substitutions. Founder-fork is a pure-mechanism GenePool::found that performs one Bernoulli-sum drift step at the reduced founder effective size Ne_f on a distinct Phase::FOUND (so it cannot collide with the parent's same-generation EVOLVE drift on the shared coordinate), the daughter frequencies a non-representative slice recovering toward the biome carrying-capacity Ne, cost O(Ne_f * loci) cheap by construction. Extinction is deterministic and permanent: per generation in canonical pool-id order a pool is dropped and an Extinction event logged when any trigger crosses (demographic, its Part 15 stock <= extinction_floor set to the collapse boundary, the HYBRID cull; trophic, no live pool satisfies its resolved feeds_on or draws_on; genetic, the Liebig tent-fitness <= survival_floor for survival_window generations or heterozygosity <= diversity_floor), all logged into the built append-only EventLog with an EcologyEvent schema whose kind is a small fixed enum and whose cause is a measured attribution tag naming which metric crossed. The distance-measure and cheap-path couplings are bounding disciplines, not laws: distance_measure_id (interim_biallelic_v0, the built mean-absolute-frequency-difference distance) is stamped into the world identity via the built StateHasher so the radiated set is an explicit versioned function of (seed, measure_id) and settling the fixation-index-versus-Nei or multi-allele choice becomes a distinct world rather than a silent divergence, with the measure-independent incompatibility-count speciation criterion the stable backbone and the distance-threshold criterion the measure-bounded supplement; and the exact-O(Ne) Bernoulli drift gives G_predawn a performance ceiling, so predawn_generations and per-pool drift-effective Ne_e (orders below census) are bounded jointly under a reserved draw budget B by predawn_generations * sum(2*Ne_e*loci) <= B, a bounded first epoch affordable on the exact path at effective sizes while the unbuilt cheap large-Ne integer-Gaussian path (reserved under 25.10, deferring to R-GPU-CANON-PIN) is a prerequisite only for deep census-scale long epochs. The promote/demote linkage-disequilibrium loss caveat is moot during the pure-frequency-tier epoch (no promoted individuals accrue LD), re-entering only at dawn promotion.

## 5. Dynamic edibility (medicinal, preparation, dose, heritability, harm sink)

Edibility is not stored on any organism: whether a tissue is food, poison, or medicine, and to whom, is the measured tuple the built floor law returns, (net_nutrition, net_harm, margin, medicinal_value), with no value-laden verdict scalar and no combination form in the physics layer. The gain-versus-danger weighting (the former w_n, w_h) lives in the Part 8/20 agent decision layer, heritable per-race and acted on by selection, so the one authored physics layer carries no risk attitude; the founding risk prior is surfaced there with its basis because a prior centered near a 1:1 attitude would reinstate the authored attitude the relocation removes. Medicinal value is derived relationally, never from a substance-side compound-to-benefit map: it is the share of net_nutrition that fills a nutrient class the consumer is deficient in, attenuated as net_harm rises, read from the tuple and the consumer's own deficiency vector, so the same tissue is medicine to a deficient consumer and inert to a replete one (the exact relational combination form is reserved, a modelling choice, not a fact to copy). Toxin hormesis is the reserved biphasic credit: the Calabrese biphasic dose-response shape expressed as a fixed-point difference or product of Hill terms sharing the same r_sat guard ordering and an order-independent saturating_sum reduction, reserved rather than consolidated because the curve shape is a modelling choice. Preparation-lability is a reserved per-class-by-operation matrix (cook, soak, ferment to detoxify, Tier 0 for heat and soak) whose entries are the class-by-operation detoxification literature; it makes toxicity dose-and-preparation-dependent so a tuber that is poison raw becomes safe processed, routing through technique (Part 23) on the open Inconsistency 5, so a people can discover a staple must be processed or fail and poison itself, an emergent culinary and medicinal history rather than an authored property. The microbiome contribution to fermentation and detox is an open sub-question (dive sub-question 16): whether it folds into the consumer fermentation and detox axes (the primitive-axis default) or is modelled as an evolving symbiont pool with its own conserved projection, left open. Composition heritability rides the unified axis-keyed genome channel (F10, Channel::Composition(AxisId) or the recommended unified substrate variant), and this synthesis fixes that for mass-fraction families the heritable payload is the stick-breaking coordinates so additivity is drift-safe under selection; defensive toxin content and its domestication loss then fall out of selection on the composition coordinates rather than from an authored floor, no Tier-0 toxin floor being mandated (defensive chemistry emerges). The harm sink is deferred: net_harm routes to a unified affliction substrate shared with R-WOUND (F17), where Part 22 carries the affliction, Part 35 the condition, and Part 52 the treatment, through a shared AfflictionKind registry, so R-BIOSPHERE reads around it rather than resolving it. The reference-potency axis is a pure unit convention (the scale in which the consumer's per-class tolerance is read), never a substance-side potency multiplier, so no Tier-0 path lets a substance-side ordering become a consumer-independent danger ranking; the harm law reads only dose and the consumer's per-class tolerance, and reference_tolerance stays fail-loud reserved with a per-toxin-class scale under R-UNITS-PIN because its pg/kg-to-g/kg envelope exceeds one Q32.32 scale.

## 6. The world-genesis sequence

Worldgen runs first: Phase::WORLDGEN draws the terrain lattice, producing the three normalized [0,ONE) fields (elevation, moisture, temperature), the soil stock, the biome classification, and the per-region abiotic-source presence registry (Light, Water, SoilNutrient, and any Resource stock the region carries). The biome-fit tents and the closure walk both read these, so the field profiles are the first input the biosphere composes on. The pre-dawn biosphere epoch attaches next as a bounded worldgen pass on its own pre-dawn generation counter with pool ids minted in a range disjoint from post-dawn ids. Its founder generation (generation 0) runs the generate-and-validate sampler (Phase::BIOSPHERE_SAMPLE) in trophic-dependency order, producers first then consumers, each candidate validated against representability, biome-fit (tau_fit), the incremental food-web closure fixed point, and niche openness (D_min), with bounded resample to K_max and the closure-preserving fallback; each accepted founder is given its per-tissue composition Substance and its per-race consumer physiology vector drawn at genesis (Phase::GENESIS), which seed the GenePool consumer and composition loci. The epoch then radiates over G_predawn generations: drift on the built Phase::EVOLVE keyed on the pre-dawn generation counter, selection through the piecewise-linear environment-to-coefficient kernel feeding the built GenePool::select, founder-fork on Phase::FOUND, speciation and Dobzhansky-Muller growth on Phase::SPECIATE recorded in the species/lineage registry, and deterministic pool-tier extinction, with Part 15 stock dynamics culling the topologically-closed-but-under-supplied pools and every speciation, founder-fork, die-off, and extinction appended to the built EventLog as the deep-time history the dawn arrives into. The dawn attaches at World::seed_dawn_populations: the mature pre-dawn pools are what the fail-loud calibration path promotes founders from (Race.pool.promote, Hardy-Weinberg, the two-tier crossing of 25.8), and the sentient races seed fresh here because the epoch is flora and fauna only (intelligence-dial cut-points are per-world data). Play attaches last: on-demand promotion replays every organism from (pool.seed, slot) under Phase::PROMOTE so the camera never drives canonical fidelity (R-VIEW-ELAB, Principle 10), the built floor edibility/nutrition/harm laws read the composition and consumer vectors live to return the measured tuple, and cultures accumulate, transmit, and lose the fallible dietary, agricultural, and medicinal knowledge and the preparation techniques (Parts 23 and 9) over the biosphere the epoch produced. The whole chain, worldgen to epoch to dawn to play, is one replay-identical function of the world seed and the reserved parameters, with no float in canonical state.

## 7. Steering audit (Principle 9)

- Biome placement: a species is placed where its niche vector scores against the physical climate field (the Liebig-minimum tent), never where an authored habitat table assigns it; the score is a measured niche-match and the biomes/successional/trophic labels are derived from it, never stored (Principle 8/9).
- Food-web shape: closure is topological reachability from the abiotic base, a measured graph property, so no food-web template or trophic structure is authored; whatever grounds, grounds, and trophic level is derived from the resolved graph (F11), never stored.
- Which pools survive: the quantitative viability cull runs entirely through the existing Part 15 collapse and Part 17 extinction machinery (the HYBRID), so which pools die is emergent logged history, not an authored ecological outcome.
- Unfillable niches: the resample fallback never relaxes closure and never fabricates a fit, so a world that cannot fill a niche records an honest empty-niche event rather than an authored filler, keeping the ecology's gaps a measured outcome.
- Species roster: the sampler emits a validated point over authored axes keyed on the world seed, never a hand-authored species; hand-authoring stays reserved for the memorable, and the accepted set is a measured consequence of the seed.
- Tissue composition: a tissue carries only measured composition quantities (mass fractions, concentrations), never an edible/poison/medicine verdict and never a food-side digestibility map; defensive toxin content and its domestication loss fall out of selection on the composition coordinates, not from an authored floor.
- Requirement: consumer.requirement is a measured deficiency floor derived from what the race's own drives draw on, never a normative diet; the mechanism authors no food-to-need table (mirroring AppraisalBinding authoring no event-to-emotion table).
- Digestibility and essentiality: these are the consumer's measured data, never a property of the food and never a verdict, so the same tissue reads as nourishing to one consumer and inert to another purely from its axes.
- Risk attitude: the gain-versus-danger weighting is relocated to the Part 8/20 agent layer, heritable and selectable there, so the one authored physics layer carries no risk attitude and no combination form; the founding prior is surfaced so a near-1:1 default does not reinstate the authored attitude.
- Medicinal value: derived relationally from the consumer's own deficiency state attenuated by harm, never a global substance-side compound-to-benefit map, so no authored medicine ranking exists.
- Fitness: measured as envelope mismatch against the physical climate field (zero at the optimum so only drift acts), no ecotype authored as favoured, and the scales k_sel/k_res/k_path reserved with basis rather than tuned to hit a target ecology.
- Speciation and incompatibilities: speciation is declared from measured divergence crossing reserved thresholds and DM incompatibilities arise probabilistically from measured complementary fixations, never scripted or placed by hand; the tree shape is whatever the seed and map produce.
- Founder-fork: the non-representative founder slice is drift, not selection; which alleles the founders carry is the seed's, with no authored founder genotype.
- Extinction: a measured demographic, trophic, or genetic consequence turned into logged history, the cause a data tag over which metric crossed, never a stored reason or an authored cull.
- Reference-potency: recast as a pure unit convention (the scale the consumer's tolerance is read in), never a substance-side potency multiplier, so no Tier-0 path lets a substance-side ordering become a consumer-independent danger ranking (NEW-STEER-4).

## 8. Determinism audit (Principle 3)

- Phase/slot registrations: four new registered Phases reserved with basis, reconciled to distinct ids (BIOSPHERE_SAMPLE, GENESIS, FOUND, SPECIATE) so no two draw sites collide on counter zero, resolving the cross-cluster overlap where two clusters both claimed 0x0D; the built EVOLVE (0x09), PROMOTE (0x0A), REPRODUCE (0x08), and WORLDGEN (0x10) are reused unchanged, and the reserved AXIOM_INHERIT (0x0B) stays unused because the epoch is flora and fauna only.
- Slot discipline: BIOSPHERE_SAMPLE uses slot = attempt ordinal and counter = axis index (candidate sampling and each resample distinct streams); GENESIS uses slot = axis ordinal; SPECIATE uses distinct slots per roll type (arise, kind); FOUND uses the Bernoulli index k as the draw counter; the generation index is carried in the tick coordinate exactly as EVOLVE and WORLDGEN do, and pre-dawn pool ids are minted disjoint from post-dawn ids (the keys.rs slot and phase tests prove non-collision on counter zero).
- Fixed-point kernels: every law is closed-form integer with an interval bound formed by checked arithmetic routed to a physical limit, order-independent (the net_nutrition min-fold and every saturating_sum are associative and commutative), no float in canonical state, no product-before-divide (mhat and every tent divide are taken and clamped before any multiply, the closure walk uses no Fixed arithmetic), and the piecewise-linear tent is preferred over exp/Gaussian which defers to R-GPU-CANON-PIN.
- Closure fixed point: the monotone least-fixed-point graph walk is a pure function of the accepted-pool set and the region source registry, independent of visitation order and thread count, reaching G* in at most |P_r| rounds, so closure is bit-reproducible.
- Bounded resample and deterministic fallback: the loop counter is bounded by the fixed integer K_max, the best-so-far selection uses a strict deterministic total order with no RNG, and the fallback branch is taken unconditionally at k == K_max, so termination is guaranteed and the outcome is a pure function of the K_max sampled candidates.
- Replay from (pool.seed, slot): on-demand promotion and founder-fork sampling replay under Phase::PROMOTE and Phase::FOUND, so re-promotion reproduces the organism and the composition/consumer vectors bit for bit and the camera never drives canonical fidelity (R-VIEW-ELAB, Principle 10).
- Stick-breaking additivity: the additivity spine uses only checked multiply and ordered subtraction (no divide, no product exceeding P <= 1), and the heritable payload is the stick coordinates, so drift and selection cannot break sum(children)+residual == P; the walk is order-fixed by canonical registry order.
- Conserved projection: the additive mass (children+residual=parent, gross simplex=ONE) is the R-TIER-CONSIST conserved projection for the ecology subsystem, remainder to the lowest child id, with nonlinear law outputs left tier-resolution-dependent, an honest cross-tier non-invariance.
- Speciation and extinction determinism: SpeciesId minting is a monotone counter advanced in a fixed canonical pool-id sweep, DM candidate pairs are enumerated in canonical order over the immutable frequency snapshot with per-roll slots, and extinction triggers are threshold comparisons in canonical pool-id order with ties broken by pool id, so the lineage tree, the added incompatibility set, and the event-log order are replay-exact.
- Epoch identity bound: distance_measure_id is stamped into the world identity via the built StateHasher and the exact-O(Ne) drift budget B bounds predawn_generations * sum(2*Ne_e*loci), so the radiated set is an explicit versioned function of (seed, measure_id) and the placeholder-dependence is honest rather than hidden.

## 9. Reserved values (surfaced with basis, never set here)

- **tau_fit (biome-fit suitability cutoff)**: the minimum suitability S at which the Part 15 logistic carrying capacity for a pool is positive (the fit below which regeneration cannot sustain a population), a steady-state feasibility bound from carrying-capacity and climate-tolerance literature, in the normalized [0,ONE] range
- **K_max (hard resample bound per niche draw site)**: the smallest cap that clears valid niches with high probability while guaranteeing deterministic termination (expected samples to hit a valid niche given the acceptance rate times a safety factor), capped so total pre-dawn sampling stays within the worldgen budget; a performance bound, not a realism one
- **N_world (founder species count per world)**: the founder-to-radiated ratio in real adaptive radiations inverted from the profile's target richness and the pre-dawn radiation multiplier; framed profile-derived rather than a terran richness target to avoid steering the ecology's shape
- **N_biome (founder species count per biome/region)**: the profile's biome area and net primary productivity partition N_world across biomes (larger, more productive biomes carry more founders); an ecological-productivity envelope
- **D and D_min (diversity targets: niches per trophic layer per biome, and minimum co-resident trait/genome distance)**: profile-derived richness (species-area and productivity-diversity relationships) for the niche count, and the limiting-similarity envelope for D_min; framed profile-derived to avoid steering the ecology's shape
- **G_predawn / predawn_generations (pre-dawn epoch generation count) and its drift-selection schedule**: the span for founders to radiate into mature ecotypes without collapsing onto the selection optimum, bounded above by the exact-O(Ne) drift ceiling (G_predawn times Ne_e must stay within the draw budget B, since the cheap large-Ne path is unbuilt)
- **B (exact-path draw budget per epoch)**: the worldgen time budget; a performance bound enforcing predawn_generations * sum(2*Ne_e*loci) <= B on the exact O(Ne) Bernoulli drift
- **per-axis niche (optimum, breadth) ranges and axis [lo, hi) sampling ranges**: already reserved-with-basis in the trait-axis catalogue (real ecology/nutrition/toxicology envelopes at or just above the documented maximum, fantasy axes profile-gated); the biome-fit law and sampler consume them, they do not re-set them
- **soil-fertility normalization reference**: the soil Stock capacity as the normalizing denominator (amount/capacity.max(ONE)), matching the Part 15 Stock::pressure guard, so soil-fertility reads on the same normalized [0,ONE) scale as the terrain fields
- **founder tissue-composition draw distribution per axis (central tendency and spread)**: the fixed-point range already set in biology_floor.toml per axis, with per-tissue-class central tendency and spread from USDA FoodData Central and FAO/INFOODS food-composition tables, set at or just above the documented maximum, never fabricated
