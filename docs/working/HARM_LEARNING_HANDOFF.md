# HANDOFF BRIEF: Experiential-Learning + Belief-to-Behaviour Arc

Scope note: this is the conversation-persistence closer, owner-ruled to land BEFORE the material-substrate arc. It converts one authored belief into a general capability: a being that learns from what it feels, acts on what it learns, and passes it on.

## (1) THE PROBLEM: three counts of ignorance, one missing capability

The salt-flat hazard belief is a puppet. It is wired at three points, and all three are the absence of the same loop.

**Count one, injected-not-reasoned.** The runner authors the belief. In `Runner::couple_conversation` it reads a high-level fact, `env.salinity_dose(c.x,c.y,calib) > live.hazard_dose_threshold` (`crates/sim/src/runner.rs:1407`), and for every being over that dose it pushes a pre-formed `Stimulus::Observe{subject:HAZARD_SUBJECT, attr:HAZARD_ATTR, toward:HAZARD_PRESENT, weight:live.hazard_weight}` (runner.rs:1408-1420). The belief identity is a hardcoded global, one subject for every salt flat: `HAZARD_SUBJECT=StableId(u64::MAX-1)`, `HAZARD_ATTR=AttrKindId(u32::MAX-1)`, `HAZARD_PRESENT=1` (runner.rs:144-153). The authored knowledge lives in the reserved `LivelinessCalib.hazard_dose_threshold` and `.hazard_weight` (runner.rs:174-181). `world.tick` merely routes the Observe to `mind.consider` (world.rs:2424-2440), and the demo re-seeds the same fixed ids into the dawn band (`crates/sim/examples/run_world.rs:1300-1323`). The being reasons nothing. The runner decides, off a dose threshold no being can perceive.

**Count two, inert-not-acted-on.** Even the injected belief changes no behaviour. The evolved controller reads a flat per-axis percept vector in `ControllerLayout::build_input` (controller.rs:220-243): per axis a reserve level, a here-flag, a direction to a known source, a signed setpoint-deviation, plus a bias. There is no belief input slot. CONDITION is a registered axis (homeostasis.rs:176-184) that already occupies five controller slots, but its here/dir/signed slots sit permanently at zero (no source, no field entry), and the founding seed leaves its direction-to-heading weights at founding-zero. A committed `HAZARD_PRESENT` never enters `decide` (controller.rs:250-282). A being can hold "a hazard is here" and walk straight onto the flat.

**Count three, does-not-persist.** The belief thins as the origin cohort ages out, which the reader's own note records (run_world.rs near 928). The cause is that it is injected AT the flat (runner.rs:1407) onto the marginal wanderers who then die there, so holders are UNDER-represented among survivors: the belief is anti-adaptive. The overhearing transmission (`World::gossip`, world.rs:3400, fanning `committed_beliefs` at 3449) carries it, but there is nothing adaptive to sustain, so prevalence decays to zero inside a cohort.

The three are one hole. There is no experiential, associative learning loop: no formation from felt experience (so it is injected), no behaviour consumer (so it is inert), and therefore no selective advantage to ride into the next generation (so it does not persist). Fill the loop once, generally, and all three close together.

## (2) THE GOAL: one general primitive, nothing hazard-specific

Build a general experiential/associative-learning primitive plus a belief-to-behaviour path, and let the whole loop run: **correlate felt harm with a perceived feature, form a belief, act (avoid), survive, transmit, persist.** And its falsifier: remove the harm and the belief dies out.

Concretely, the associative learner IS the existing evidence engine, keyed differently. `InferenceFrame` (evidence.rs:93-226) is a complete order-independent fixed-point log-odds accumulator over a `(subject, attr)` question with `ValueId` hypotheses, committing past a reserved threshold and margin, clamping only at read; `good_weight` (evidence.rs:305) is the signed per-observation primitive, general over any two probabilities, reading no kind, race, or attr. The generalization is the SUBJECT KEYING: the one global `HAZARD_SUBJECT` becomes a per-feature subject minted from the raw ground-signature a being senses underfoot, and the attr becomes one generic "does-standing-here-harm-me". Sign and magnitude come from the being's OWN raw interoceptive delta this tick. Because the belief is then an ordinary `(subject, attr)` frame, the shipped overhearing transmission carries it for free, and a belief-derived expected-harm percept can enter the evolved controller as a weighted directional input, so avoidance emerges from selection, never an authored branch. The identical loop then learns any ground-kind, any good place, any food that sickens, with zero per-hazard code.

## (3) THE ORDERED, INCREMENTAL BUILD PLAN

Read-then-wire slice cadence, the one the anatomy arc proved: build the pure reads and the mechanism off the run path (hash-neutral, unit-tested), and isolate the hash-changing run wiring into its own commit. The four natural slices depend forward in order.

### Slice (a): the interoceptive harm signal + the raw perceived-feature vector

- **Mechanism.** Two pieces of raw plumbing, both blind to cause. First, a per-tick reserve DELTA: retain the previous-tick level per axis and expose `delta(axis) = level_now - level_prev`. CONDITION's per-tick change already nets `condition_recovery - harm` (locomotion.rs:854), so its delta is the net interoceptive harm signal for free, and keying off the homeostatic registry generalizes it past CONDITION to any reserve (a food that sickens is energy or a toxin-backed reserve falling after ingestion). Second, a raw per-cell FEATURE percept: a new input block surfacing the cell's `Composition` channels (each toxin and optionally each nutrient class as a raw `Fixed` from `resources.composition(here)`) as generic numbered channels keyed by biology-floor class string, exactly the way the signed thermoreceptor already surfaces a raw scalar (runner.rs:1648-1669 into the SIGNED_SLOT).
- **Dependency.** None upstream; consumes the homeostatic registry, the tile `Composition` already read inside harm (locomotion.rs:715-731), and the signed-slot template.
- **Guards.** *Determinism:* the previous-level snapshot is new per-being dynamic state and must fold into `state_hash` alongside the reserve levels (runner.rs:2008-2018), computed by pure fixed-point subtraction in canonical axis order drawing no RNG. *Principle 8/9 (read-no-high-level-fact):* the feature percept must surface ALL declared classes as generic channels off the class-string registry, never a hardcoded `{salinity}` slot and never a thresholded named-hazard flag; the interoceptive signal must be a raw reserve delta, not the `net_harm` scalar labelled as toxin harm. Both are pure reads of already-hashed physical state with no thresholding and no identity read. *Principle 11:* feature granularity (the quantization) surfaced reserved-with-basis; the plumbing itself invents nothing.
- **Milestone.** The controller input vector carries a per-cell feature channel and a per-axis delta; a dump shows a being on the flat reading a high `bio.salinity` channel and a negative CONDITION delta, reproducible across worker widths. The feature block grows `ControllerLayout::n_in` (controller.rs:154), rippling into `weight_count` and genome expression, so it is a NEW input block, not a widened `INPUTS_PER_AXIS`; that growth is the one hash-changing commit, held apart from the pure delta/feature reads.

### Slice (b): the associative learner that forms the belief, retiring the injected Observe

- **Mechanism.** A new per-being body-tick step, co-located with `step_embodiment` (runner.rs:1628-1786) where the feature underfoot and the reserves are in hand. Each tick it reads (i) the raw interoceptive harm signal (a this-tick fall below a reserved harm-noise threshold, so ordinary metabolic drain is not read as harm) and (ii) the raw feature `f` of the current cell, then calls `mind.consider(feature_subject(f), HARM_ATTR, {HARMS,BENIGN}, toward, w, self.id)` with `w = good_weight(P(harm|harms), P(harm|benign), clamp)`, toward HARMS on a harm tick and toward BENIGN otherwise. `feature_subject(f)` mints a per-feature subject through a reserved `FEATURE_SUBJECT_BASE` band, analogous to `CELL_PLACE_BASE` (runner.rs:139). Repeated harm-while-on-`f` drives the HARMS total past commit (evidence.rs:181-214); harm-free ticks push BENIGN, so absence self-corrects and the belief is defeasible for free. `Mind.plasticity` (agent.rs:99-101, carried but unwired) becomes the learning rate scaling `w` before the add; `RetentionLaw` (agent.rs:365-402) becomes the forgetting, decaying the totals when off `f`. Retire the salinity-dose injection (runner.rs:1406-1421), the dawn seed (run_world.rs:1300-1323), and the `HAZARD_*` constants plus `hazard_dose_threshold`/`hazard_weight`.
- **Dependency.** Slice (a) for the delta and the feature; the evidence substrate (`consider`/`belief`, agent.rs:169-196) and `good_weight`, both reused wholesale with no structural change.
- **Guards.** *Determinism:* the core accumulation draws NO RNG (a pure i128 add per tick, clamp at read) and folds into the existing Mind `state_hash` unchanged; the only piece that could draw is an acuity-gated feature-PERCEPTION roll if one is added mirroring `perceive()`, and it must be counter-keyed on a declared Phase slot on `(being, tick)`. No such slot exists for experiential learning yet (R-RNG-COORD), so a new slot must be declared rather than borrowed. *Principle 8/9:* the subject is a raw quantized feature-percept id, never the salinity dose, a hazard label, or a race id; `good_weight` sees only harm-fired versus the two reserved likelihoods, so "this feature harms me" emerges from correlation. The seam to harden: the feature quantizer must be a data-defined percept registry (sibling to the homeostatic and affordance registries, controller.rs:143-175), not a closed enum, and the `FEATURE_SUBJECT_BASE` band must not collide with being ids or the reserved-high ids. *Principle 11 reserved-with-basis:* the harm-noise threshold (basis: the noise floor of normal metabolic drain the physiology defines), `P(harm|harms)` and `P(harm|benign)` (basis: the base rate of the harm signal on a harmful versus neutral cell the dose and physics imply, set as `trace.rs` sets reliability and false-attribution), and the memory-scaled decay window (basis: the transmission subsystem's existing loss window). None fabricated; they replace the retired hazard calibs.
- **Milestone.** With behaviour still unchanged, a naive being that walks onto the flat and takes repeated CONDITION harm now COMMITS a HARMS belief about the flat's feature-kind on its own; a being that never touches the flat never forms it; a being that leaves decays it. No `HAZARD_*` constant remains on any canonical path.

### Slice (c): belief-to-behaviour, avoidance as an evolved weighted input

- **Mechanism.** The being computes, from its own beliefs over the raw features of its perceived surrounding cells, an expected-outcome gradient per axis: a unit direction away from cells it believes reduce that axis's reserve, toward those it believes raise it. For the salt flat the CONDITION expected-harm gradient points away from believed-harmful ground. *Minimal landing (S):* supply that CONDITION gradient through the EXISTING `field_dirs` map in `step_embodiment` (runner.rs:1636-1647); it lands in CONDITION's already-allocated-but-dead `dir_x`/`dir_y` slots, sums into the MOVE heading through the heritable CONDITION-dir-to-heading weights (controller.rs:549-554), and avoidance EMERGES when selection lifts those weights off founding-zero, with zero change to controller.rs. *Principled target (M):* give the belief gradient its own per-axis slot (`INPUTS_PER_AXIS` 5 to 7, a `BELIEF_SLOT`, a `belief_dirs` argument threaded through `build_input` and `step_with_field_dirs`) so the belief gradient COMBINES with the known-source pull rather than overwriting it, and every axis enters the same way.
- **Dependency.** Slice (b) for a committed HARMS belief keyed on the perceived feature.
- **Guards.** *Principle 8/9:* the gradient reaches behaviour ONLY as an input the controller weights, steering solely through evolved weights exactly as the TEMPERATURE gradient does (runner.rs:1636, locomotion.rs:738); the MOVE arm (locomotion.rs:762-786) stays "walk `d.heading` from the controller decision" and must NOT subtract or blend any harm term itself, and the gradient is computed from the being's own belief associating a raw feature with a raw reserve fall, never by reading a hazard label, `HAZARD_PRESENT`, or a race id. *Determinism:* `build_input` and `evaluate` are already integer fixed-point, order-independent, RNG-free; the S path leaves `n_in`, `weight_count`, and the `ControllerParamId` index space untouched, while the M path shifts them and must reconcile genome expression and every layout assertion in lockstep. *Feasibility:* the founding seed (`forage_taxis_weights`, controller.rs:381-411) leaves CONDITION-dir-to-heading weights at zero, so selection needs a mutational path; the weight already EXISTS in the matrix because CONDITION is a registered axis, so mutation has a gradient to act on rather than needing a new gene.
- **Milestone.** A being that holds the HARMS belief steers away from the flat once its evolved CONDITION-dir weight is non-zero; the evolve harness shows survival selection lifting that weight off zero, so avoidance appears with no authored branch. Start on S; graduate to M only when a second axis (a good place, a sickening food) needs to combine with the source pull.

### Slice (d): let persistence emerge, re-tune the harness if it destabilizes

- **Mechanism.** Persistence is emergent once the belief is adaptive, which (b) and (c) supply; this slice builds the ACCEPTANCE instrumentation, not a new mechanism. Extend the belief-spread reader (run_world.rs:932) from an instantaneous holder count to four persistence signatures over time: prevalence sustained ACROSS generations rather than within one cohort; correlation of holding with survival; a transmission-versus-inheritance discriminator showing a newborn acquired the belief by co-location, not by a race-clustered inherited axiom (the impostor `inherited_beliefs`, world.rs:1591, that the section-8 verifier pinned in AGENTIC_ADDENDUM.md:199); and die-out on hazard removal. Then assemble and run the section-8 blind concept-verification battery on a dawn run with recorded seeds, including the hazard-removal falsifiability control and a positive control. If avoidance destabilizes the tuned movement or evolve fixtures, re-tune deliberately at the cause. If the reproducing-core co-location is too sparse (survivors disperse from the frozen dawn-band spawn site, runner.rs:1907; capped dispersal flagged in CONSENSUS_ROADMAP line ~72), spawn newborns at the PARENT'S live cell rather than the frozen band place, a draw-free, emergence-neutral placement change.
- **Dependency.** Slices (b) and (c); the shipped `gossip` transmission (world.rs:3400/3449), the lifecycle (`life_cadence` world.rs:2005, `reproduce` 2127, mortality), and `evolve.rs`.
- **Guards.** *Determinism:* the reader is a pure read of hashed state (observer-side, non-canonical under Principle 10); the newborn-placement fallback reuses a deterministic coord with no new draw. *Principle 8/9:* the acceptance must ASSERT that no canonical path reads `HAZARD_SUBJECT`/`HAZARD_ATTR` once injection is retired, and that the identical loop runs for ANY experientially-formed belief; a hazard-specific persistence path would be authoring. *Principle 11:* any re-tune of the evolve or movement fixtures surfaces the changed reserved values with basis at the fixture or manifest.
- **Milestone.** On a dawn run, prevalence is sustained across generations, holding correlates with survival, a newborn is shown acquiring the belief by co-location, and prevalence decays to zero when the hazard is removed. The blind panel returns "realized" against the concept, superseding its earlier "not realized, and not even measured."

## (4) HONEST LIMITS AND COUPLINGS

The loop relies on the shipped overhearing transmission. `World::gossip` already fans a speaker's `committed_beliefs` to every co-located non-promoted listener bucketed by the live conversational cell (world.rs:3400/3449), and its own doc states the young acquire by presence and an idea dies out where no holder stands. So the loop rides it with no transmission work, but its reach is capped by co-location, and the reproducing-core co-location (a surviving holder standing where newborns spawn, against a frozen dawn-band spawn site while survivors migrate, runner.rs:1907) is dynamic and can be too sparse to persist. That is the real risk to persistence condition (2); measure it empirically before assuming the loop closes, and use the parent-cell spawn fallback if it does not.

The belief loop is invisible until instrumented. An observability event-stream is being added in parallel; it will make formation, transmission, and avoidance legible, and the acceptance reader in slice (d) depends on that legibility. The section-8 method has a stated limit: a realized loop surfaced by no reader verifies as "not observable," so the reader extension is load-bearing, not cosmetic. Coordinate so the reader and the event-stream key off the generic `(subject, attr)` question, never the retired hazard sentinels.

Avoidance may destabilize the tuned movement and evolve fixtures. Introducing a real avoidance pressure shifts the selection landscape those fixtures were tuned against, so a deliberate re-tune may be needed. It must be shown at the cause (the fixture or the manifest) with the changed reserved values surfaced-with-basis, not silently absorbed into a passing test.

The impostor is inheritance. Intrinsic axioms inherited at birth (world.rs:1591, a heritable-plus-encultured, frozen, race-clustered blend) mimic a spreading belief but are the fingerprint of inheritance the blind verifier caught. The acceptance must discriminate the experientially-formed, co-location-transmitted belief from that impostor, which is exactly what the transmission-versus-inheritance signature in slice (d) exists to do.

## (5) PRIME CONSTRAINTS AND THE GATE

Determinism plus the 5090 GPU floor: cognition stays on the CPU, so the GPU floor is untouched, and runs stay bit-identical across worker widths. Every new per-being state (the previous-tick levels, the per-feature `InferenceFrame`s) folds into `state_hash` in canonical order. Any new RNG draw (an acuity-gated perception roll, if added) must be counter-keyed under a DECLARED Phase slot on `(being, tick)`; experiential learning has none today (R-RNG-COORD), so a new slot must be declared rather than borrowed.

Principle 8/9 at every slice: the subject is a raw quantized feature percept, sign and magnitude come from the being's own raw interoceptive delta, and behaviour is steered only through evolved weights. Never a dose threshold, a hazard label, `HAZARD_PRESENT`, a race id, or genetic relatedness.

Principle 11: the feature vocabulary is a data-defined percept registry sibling to the existing registries; the retired `hazard_dose_threshold`/`hazard_weight` are replaced by reserved-with-basis fail-loud calibs (the harm-noise threshold, the two `good_weight` likelihoods, the feature granularity, the memory-decay window), each with its basis given, none fabricated.

Process gate: `cargo fmt --all` before every commit (the CI fmt gate breaks main for all branches on a violation), `cargo clippy --all-targets`, and `verify.sh`; the consensus-roadmap stop hook requires updating `docs/working/CONSENSUS_ROADMAP.md` with each item's gate whenever source or design files change.

Acceptance is a blind concept-verification on the dawn run that a belief forms from experience, guides avoidance, raises survival, spreads, and persists across generations, and still dies out if the hazard is removed. A load-bearing verdict wants a panel across models rather than one verifier, and the packet's Section B must be verbatim code, not pseudocode, or the panel converges on phantom findings.

## RECOMMENDED FIRST SLICE

Start with slice (a): the interoceptive harm signal plus the raw perceived-feature vector. It is the read half of the read-then-wire cadence and the interface both downstream slices consume: (b) correlates the delta against the feature, and (c) steers on the belief (b) builds from them. It is the lowest-risk work, pure reads of already-hashed physical state, the delta a pure fixed-point subtraction and the feature a pure `Composition` read, so the mechanism is built and unit-tested off the run path with only the input-vector growth isolated into one hash-changing commit. It also surfaces the two reserved calibrations with the clearest bases (feature granularity and the harm-noise floor) early, so the owner can rule on them before the associative learner consumes them. Beginning with formation or behaviour instead would leave nothing to correlate or steer from.
