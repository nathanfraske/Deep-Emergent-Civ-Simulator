# Derived-taxis survival floor: seeking as an always-on run-and-tumble biased by the felt reserve derivative (design-first)

This is the design opener for the arc the gate ruled highest-leverage after the predator-prey demonstrations (#144 approach, #146 avoid, #148 pay-off, #150 per-feature sort). It is the general fix for the recurring founder-extinction wall: a founder-zero controller cannot be forced to seek, so a founder starves before selection can tune a directed-locomotion weight. Nature's answer is that seeking is not a decision, it is a run-and-tumble biased random walk, always on, coupling motility to the being's OWN felt reserve change. This arc proposes that floor: a stochastic re-orientation whose RATE is modulated by the sign of the being's own interoceptive reserve derivative, so the walk is biased up any gradient that feeds the reserve with no authored direction and no controller weight. Design-first, grounded against source, posted for the gate's ruling before any build code. No mechanism decided here.

## What is already built (do not rebuild)

The interoceptive substrate is on main. `ReserveMemory` (`homeostasis.rs:1136`, per-Walker state at `locomotion.rs:609`) holds each reserve's previous normalised level and yields `delta(axis) = level_now - level_prev`, the being's OWN signed per-tick reserve change, snapshotted each body-tick in canonical order (RNG-free, deterministic). Its sign is already classified against a reserved noise floor by `is_harm_tick` (a fall beyond ordinary drain) and `is_reward_tick` (a rise beyond ordinary recovery), and folded into the felt-experience summary (`physiology::felt_salience`) and the forward model (`forward_model.rs`, `felt_delta`). Automatic uptake is already INDISCRIMINATE: the forage INGEST and the whole-body `geophage` bite ingest whatever backing substance is underfoot and credit the eater by its OWN assimilation, and nourishment-or-harm emerges downstream through `edibility` and digestion, never a "this is food" classification. The directional `forage_taxis_weights` (`controller.rs:1224`) exists but is the CONTRAST, not the tool: it follows a classified source's KNOWN direction, which reads a high-level fact (the location and identity of food) and is exactly what the derived-taxis floor must not do.

## What remains: the run-and-tumble floor beneath the controller

Every piece the floor reads exists; the floor itself does not. What is missing is an always-on locomotion primitive that re-orients STOCHASTICALLY at a rate modulated CONTINUOUSLY by the sign of the being's own reserve derivative: a reserve rising lowers the tumble rate so the being persists on its current heading (a run), a reserve falling raises it so the being re-orients (a tumble). The being never reads where food is; its walk is biased up any reserve-feeding gradient purely by the interoceptive derivative, because persisting when things improve and re-orienting when they worsen carries a walker up a gradient it cannot see. It is the survival FLOOR beneath the controller: eating stays automatic indiscriminate uptake, reproduction stays automatic division on the reserve threshold (a separate arc, flagged below), and this makes seeking automatic too, so a founder-zero controller survives on physics while selection tunes the directed-locomotion and per-feature discrimination refinements on top. The controller's own MOVE heading, when a lineage evolves one, composes with or overrides the floor (the composition is a design question below).

## The load-bearing design question (surfaced for the gate): is the coupling POLARITY floor law or selected sign?

The mechanism couples motility to the reserve derivative. The coupling's POLARITY (a rising reserve LOWERS the tumble rate, a falling reserve RAISES it) is the crux the gate's emergence constraint bears on: "the sign of the bias must EMERGE from the reserve dynamics, never be authored." Two readings, put to the gate:

(a) The polarity is a PHYSICS-FLOOR coupling law, admissible as the one authored place. The primitive is "persist while the reserve rises," which is not a per-outcome steering (it never says "go toward food"): it is the general causal primitive of any adaptive motility, "if what you are doing is feeding you, keep doing it." The DIRECTION the being ends up moving (up an arbitrary gradient) emerges from the being's own reserve responding to its own movement in a spatial field, never read as a fact. This is the causal-primitive-plus-proxy pattern: the primitive is persist-when-rising, the proxy is the reserve rising (which correlates with moving up-gradient), and foraging emerges from selection over where the gradients lie, never coded. Recommended, because the floor must be always-on at founder-zero to dissolve the extinction wall, and a floor whose polarity itself waited on selection would not survive a founder to be selected.

(b) The polarity is a HERITABLE selected sign (a founder-zero or freely-signed gain on the delta-to-tumble-rate coupling), so which way the bias runs emerges from cross-generational selection. This is the strict reading of "the sign must emerge," but it reopens the extinction wall the arc exists to close: a founder with a zero or wrong-signed gain does not seek, so the floor stops being a floor. If the gate rules (b), the honest consequence is that the floor is not a survival guarantee but another selected trait, and the extinction wall is addressed only in expectation over a seeded sign distribution.

The recommendation is (a) with the primitive stated as the floor law and the outcome (foraging) left to emerge from the spatial field and selection, exactly as a spring's restoring force is floor while the structures it shapes emerge. The gate's ruling on this fork sets the build.

## The emergence claim (for the section-11 self-check), de-narrativized

"A being with any reserve and any motility, carrying no directed-locomotion controller weight, re-orients stochastically at a rate that falls continuously as its own interoceptive reserve derivative rises and climbs as the derivative falls; placed in a spatial field whose value feeds that reserve, the being's unguided walk is biased UP the field, and it accumulates in the high-value region, with no percept of the field's direction, no classification of a source, and no authored heading. The bias direction is a consequence of the being persisting when its own reserve rises, never a read of where the value is; a being in a flat field performs an unbiased walk; a being whose reserve does not respond performs an unbiased walk."

## Section-11 smoke self-check (fail-closed, single-pass)

The claim was probed for the authored-steering seam on four attacks. (1) The floor reads the being's OWN reserve derivative (`ReserveMemory::delta`), a raw interoceptive quantity, never a source direction, a food identity, or a classified percept; it is the causal opposite of `forage_taxis_weights`, which reads a known source's direction (a high-level fact). (2) The directed OUTCOME (accumulating up-gradient) emerges from the being's own movement in a spatial field, not from an authored heading: in a flat field the walk is unbiased, and a being whose reserve does not respond is unbiased, so the direction is not coded. (3) Admit-the-alien: any being with a reserve and any motility has it, keyed on its own reserve and its own motility; a reserve-less or motility-less being degrades cleanly to no bias. (4) The coupling is CONTINUOUS in the derivative (a tumble rate that varies with the delta, not a switch at a threshold value), so no authored behaviour flips on at a magic reserve level. The one seam the smoke leaves OPEN, surfaced above rather than buried, is the polarity fork (a versus b): whether "rising lowers the tumble rate" is a floor coupling law or a selected sign. The smoke does not resolve that fork; it is the gate's to rule.

## Sibling flag (not this arc, surfaced for the record)

The reproduce-on-reserve-threshold is DERIVABLE, not a reserved number: a being divides when its reserve exceeds about twice the minimum viable daughter reserve, itself set by `base_drain` and capacity, and the division must route through the built asexual `GeneticScheme` (clone plus mutation). It is the founder-zero DEFAULT that an evolvable allocation controller later tunes, never a fixed life-history rule. Flagged here so the derived reproduction floor lands as a sibling of this motility floor; it is not built in this arc.

## Hard constraints (carried from the owner's rulings and the gate's corrections)

The floor is the STOCHASTIC temporal-difference tumble, never the directional forage-taxis: it modulates the tumble RATE by the sign of the being's own reserve derivative, with no directional percept and no source classification, coupled continuously to the derivative rather than switching at a threshold. Eating stays INDISCRIMINATE automatic uptake, with nourishment-or-harm emerging downstream through edibility and digestion.
