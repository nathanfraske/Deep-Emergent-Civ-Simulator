# 2b per-feature discrimination: sharp emitter-specific sorting emerges over the magnitude proxy (design-first)

This is the design opener for the arc the gate ruled next after both predator-prey poles (#144 approach, #146 avoid) and the predation-completion pay-off loop (#148) landed. It closes the honest gap those arcs each flagged: the being channel today keys on signal MAGNITUDE alone, which gives a single NET disposition over every emitter a creature perceives, so a lineage cannot both AVOID a predator and APPROACH a prey when both are warm emitters. Sharp, emitter-specific sorting requires the being channel to key on a distinguishing PHYSICAL FEATURE of the other. This arc demonstrates that such a sort EMERGES from selection over an evolvable per-feature discrimination weight, never an authored "predator" or "prey" identity. Design-first, grounded against source, posted for the gate's ruling before any build code. No mechanism decided here.

## What is already built (do not rebuild)

The per-feature discrimination SUBSTRATE is on main, byte-neutral and off by default. The 2b perceivable-feature floor (`PerceivableFeatureRegistry`, `perceivable_feature.rs`, merged #126) discriminates a scalar into a bucket by a per-channel resolution (`bucket`, `from_channels`). The being-FEATURE perceive pass is built (`runner.rs:6216` onward, a separate gated block so the magnitude-only path stays pin-untouched): a mind-less creature reads each perceived emitter's per-channel surface optical feature (`PerceivableFeatureRegistry::read_emitter` = `physiology::surface_optical_axis`, a DIRECT single-axis read off the emitter's OWN body covering material, `cov.mat(axis)`, zero where the covering declares none), bins it by the perceiver's own resolution, and accumulates the toward-emitter direction per (channel, bucket) through `creature_being_feature_directions` (`learn.rs:1038`). The perceiver's OWN heritable freely-signed per-bucket weight (the controller layout's `being_feature_input_base` block) turns a feature-bucket's accumulated direction into approach or flight. The whole pass is gated on `being_features.is_empty()` (empty by default, `set_being_features` arms it), so an unarmed world does nothing and is byte-identical. The magnitude proxy (`creature_being_direction`, the #118 B3 pole) and both selection poles (#144, #146) and the pay-off loop (#148) are proven.

## What remains: prove the two-way sort EMERGES, then arm it

Every piece exists in isolation. What is not demonstrated is that the per-feature discrimination weights are DRIVEN to OPPOSITE poles by selection when two emitter types carry distinguishing surface features and opposite fitness consequences: the feature-bucket of a wounding emitter to the avoid pole, the feature-bucket of a safe or paying emitter to the approach pole, so a sharp emitter-specific sort emerges that the magnitude-only channel (one net disposition over all emitters) cannot express. This is the mirror of the #144 matter proof and the #146 flee proof, now over TWO feature buckets read out as a two-way sort rather than one weight read out as a pole.

The load-bearing design line: nothing about predator or prey identity is authored at any level. The feature a creature keys on is the emitter's own surface optical datum (a covering material axis), bucketed by the creature's own resolution; the per-bucket weights are founder-zero, symmetric zero-mean, and freely-signed; and which bucket becomes avoid and which becomes approach is set by the fitness consequences the two emitter types carry (one wounds, one is safe or pays), never a stamped label. A lineage that sorts the wounding feature-bucket away and the safe or paying one toward out-survives, so the sort is selected, exactly as the single poles were.

## The emergence claim (for the gate's frame-blind), de-narrativized

"A perceiver lineage seeded with founder-zero, symmetric zero-mean, freely-signed per-feature discrimination weights, selected on its own homeostatic survival, will have those weights driven to OPPOSITE poles on two feature buckets when the two emitter types carry distinguishing surface features and opposite fitness consequences (one wounds, one is safe or a pay-off): the bucket of the wounding emitter to the avoid pole, the bucket of the safe or paying emitter to the approach pole, so a sharp emitter-specific sort emerges that the magnitude-only channel (a single net disposition over all emitters) cannot express. In a matched control where the two emitter types are perceptually indistinguishable (their surface feature falls in ONE bucket) the sort collapses to the single net disposition. The feature is the emitter's own surface optical datum, the discrimination is the perceiver's own resolution, the weights are founder-zero and freely-signed; nothing about predator or prey identity is authored."

## Section-11 smoke self-check (fail-closed, single-pass)

The claim was probed for the authored-coupling seam before proposing it, and it held on four attacks. (1) The feature-to-disposition wire reads a RAW surface optical axis (the covering's material datum), bucketed by the perceiver's own resolution, never a valence, a category, or a "predator" fact, so it is not the template case. (2) The two-way sort needs no authored bucket-to-identity map: the per-bucket weights are founder-zero symmetric, and which bucket becomes avoid versus approach is a consequence of which emitter type wounds versus pays, a DATA outcome of their differing coverings, never wired into the perceiver. (3) Admit-the-alien holds: the feature is the emitter's own covering datum (zero if absent), so an alien body with a different covering buckets differently by the same read, and the perceiver keys on its own resolution. (4) The control is clean: it makes the two emitter types share one feature bucket, so the ONLY difference from the treatment is perceptual distinguishability, isolating that the SORT (not the presence of two emitters) is what the per-feature channel adds. The smoke did not break the claim, so it is a candidate to build, pending the section-10 frame-blind panel before any build code.

## Hard constraints (carried from #144, #146, #148, and the owner's rulings)

Nothing about predator or prey identity is authored: the per-feature weights are founder-zero symmetric and freely-signed, their signs from cross-generational selection (Principle 8, Principle 9). The feature is the emitter's own surface optical datum, the discrimination the perceiver's own resolution, never a kind read. Admit-the-alien: an emitter with a different or absent covering buckets by the same read, and a lineage that does not sort is a valid selected outcome. The being-feature pass stays a gated opt-in (byte-neutral off), the four canonical pins held for any build slice that does not arm a shipped scenario, and arming is the stated re-pin flagged for a follow-on.
