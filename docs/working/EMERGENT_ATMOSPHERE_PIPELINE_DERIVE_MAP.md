The headline the owner and the verification and this manager converged on: a planet's atmosphere is not authored, it is the fixed-point OUTPUT of the physics modules already standing, run as one coupled deterministic solve, with the per-world vector the only thing that differs between worlds. This is the specification for the atmosphere-composition arc. Convergence of three independent derivations on the same structure is the signal it is right.

## The reframe: not a new substrate, the coupled assembly of existing modules

The arc does not author a composition substrate; it ASSEMBLES the modules into a coupled solve: star ignition (homology L, Wien color, XUV history) then the mass gate and escape (EOS, retention) then differentiation (the redox setpoint) then the thermal engine (radiogenic decay, the Tozer thermostat) then the tectonic regime then decompression melting and the exhale speciation then the emergent volatile roster (MO-viability proposer, the two chi columns) then climate (opacity, radiation) then the sink network (escape, weathering, photochemistry, condensation), read out at age in timescale order. Instantiated with Earth's vector it exhales Earth's abiotic sky (N2 dominant as the freezer-protected residual, CO2 at the weathering-thermostat setpoint, H2O saturated as the Clausius-Clapeyron slave, O2 abiotically near zero awaiting the one biological door). Flip single vector entries and the same trace lands the banked branches: a closer or brighter orbit takes the runaway to Venus, a smaller mass the hot-oxygen leak to Mars, a reduced redox anchor the Titan haze branch. One pipeline, all skies.

The three dependents the deferred #143 runtime physicalization waits on fall out of this solve, and they are named at their derivation loci: `M_air` is the mean molar mass over the solved roster (proposer output times the fixed-point mole fractions); the surface pressure `p` is hydrostatic exactness (g times the solved gas-phase column mass); the condensable winner is the partial-pressure-versus-saturation ladder gated by the triple point (Earth selects H2O, Titan CH4, Mars defaults to a CO2 buffered cap while H2O fails the floor gate, the observed situation on all three). CRUCIALLY these are not post-processed readouts: pressure and composition co-determine through line broadening, the lapse rate, and the greenhouse integral, so all three live INSIDE the loop. The deliverable is the loop, and building it unblocks the #143 swap as a byproduct.

## Seam one resolved: the redox anchor demotes to computed-with-bounded-override

The single most consequential authored scalar, the Fe3+ partition that fixes mantle fO2, reduces rather than being authored per world, and the sky already ran the validation. Mantle fO2 is monotone-ish in body mass across the solar system: Mercury hyper-reduced (IW minus 3 to 7, MESSENGER S/Si), Moon near IW minus 1, Mars near IW plus 1 (the SNC suite), Earth at QFM about IW plus 3.5 to 4 (Righter compilations). Depth-dependent disproportionation predicts exactly that staircase, and a per-world authored anchor makes it a fourfold coincidence.

The chain: accretion energy from the vector sets the magma-ocean depth; the depth sets the oxidation direction and rough magnitude through the pressure-driven disproportionation `3 Fe2+ -> 2 Fe3+ + Fe0` (Frost 2004 for the crystalline bridgmanite-stabilizes-Fe3+ path, Armstrong 2019 for the melt path where Fe3+/SigmaFe rises with pressure, Deng 2020 ab initio concurring); the exact setpoint rests on the measured melt-redox `dG(P,T)` curve, a formation-enthalpy-family datum with its pressure dependence through `dV` (EOS territory: measured to tens of GPa, compute-once beyond). An independent cross-check pins the depth: Earth's siderophile partitioning wants metal-silicate equilibration at 40 to 60 GPa (Wade and Wood 2005), the same depth the redox chain needs. The standing prediction is "super-Earths arrive more oxidized," and it buys a regularity nobody would have authored: reduced haze-branch skies on small bodies, oxidized skies on large ones, which is where Titan sits, and one vector entry (mass) now drives retention AND redox, a confound structure procgen gets for free.

The bounded override that survives: equilibration efficiency (derived-in-form, since turbulent emulsification caps iron droplets at the Weber-stable centimeter scale forcing equilibration, while a late impactor's core can merge unequilibrated, a contingency per Rubie 2003), the late-veneer overprint, the H2O-plus-Fe channel on small wet worlds, and the C/O greater than 1 branch. VERIFICATION FLAG on the prediction: recent high-pressure work reports a REVERSAL in the depth-to-fO2 pressure dependence, so the staircase is clean over the terrestrial range but the extrapolation to super-Earth pressures is contested, not guaranteed monotone; hold "super-Earths more oxidized" as bounded, not certain, at the high-P end.

## Seam two resolved: the arc is a deterministic memoryless continuation solver

The coupled solve (ocean and tectonics and atmosphere and climate co-determine, water depends on the atmosphere the pipeline has not built) is a hysteretic multi-attractor fixed point, and Principle 3 makes its determinism the whole game. The resolution splits into one design law and one numerical structure.

THE LAW: all memory lives in named state variables; the solver is memoryless. Output is a pure function of (vector, residue ledger, state), so hysteresis is PHYSICS state and never numerics. This makes determinism and path-dependence orthogonal by construction rather than in tension, and it is enforceable by audit because every memory channel this project derived is already a named slot: `T_p` with its `tau(beta)` kernel, the damage fields, the film state, the reservoirs, the isotope ratios.

THE STRUCTURE: do not cold-solve nature's equilibrium, march the slow manifold, because nature never solves the fixed point either, it integrates. The timescale ladder IS the architecture: fast tiers become inner solves that are contractions given slow state (hence unique away from folds), while the slow state advances as a deterministic DAE. Attractor selection has an existing name, continuation: warm-start every inner solve from the previous converged state, follow the branch until it ends at a fold, and the jump at the fold is deterministic and physical (pseudo-arclength continuation, Keller 1977, the AUTO lineage), with the history slots as literally the continuation predictor. Bit-reproducibility is then engineering with known answers: module order fixed as spec (Gauss-Seidel order-dependence is fine once the order is law), deterministic reductions only (fixed-topology tree sums or integer superaccumulators, the Kulisch and Demmel-Ahrens reproducible-summation lineage), no atomics races, pinned math libraries. The strongest move unifies with the epistemic ledger: quantize every exchanged variable onto a lattice whose spacing sits below that variable's error band, a budget the band DAG already computes, so the coupled iteration becomes a deterministic map on a FINITE set where convergence is exact equality, termination is decidable, reproducibility is trivial, and the quantization error is certified subphysical. Two payoffs confirm the structure is right: a limit cycle on the lattice is detectable and is a FEATURE (episodic-lid is a limit cycle, so classify it, do not suppress it), and critical slowing-down falls out free (near a fold the inner solve's spectral radius approaches one and iteration counts spike, so convergence rate is a computed marginality meter and "Earth marginal" becomes a measurement the solver makes, not a verdict asserted). The two failure modes are excluded by construction: cold-start with heavy under-relaxation (spurious uniqueness, erasing the bistability the field reports) and data-dependent parallelism (the determinism violation).

## The honest authored-closure accounting (the count correction)

The "about a dozen authored scalars" conflates two categories, and the fix is the same discipline the redox anchor already used (booking the `dG(P)` curve to layer-3-measured rather than to an authored knob). Applied consistently:

- PADDING to remove: band strengths, photochemical cross-sections, Henry solubility constants, and the stellar activity constants are MEASURED DATA, the third floor pillar, not authored knobs; counting them as free parameters inflates the count while the provenance claim ("bottoms at measured data") already covers them.
- OMISSIONS to add: the truly authored model CLOSURES are undercounted, the escape efficiency `eta ~ 0.1` (a distinct quantity from the `epsilon ~ 0.3 to 0.5` thermospheric HEATING efficiency, which the trace conflates), the `K_zz` eddy-diffusion PROFILE (a field, not a scalar, governing both the cold-trap 3 ppm and the diffusion-limited H escape), the WHAK weathering barrier set, the H2O-continuum parameterization inside Simpson-Nakajima (MT_CKD is empirical, so the runaway threshold is model-dependent, not analytic), and the mobile-lid friction band and `lambda ~ 0.9` (empirical caps, not derived from serpentinization volumetrics).

So the honest ledger is a LARGER measured floor (legitimate) and a DISTINCT, somewhat larger set of authored closures (the real free knobs). The pipeline is not less derived than the trace says; the measured-versus-closure line just needs drawing consistently.

### The provenance-DAG discipline (the accounting law this codifies)

Every ledger line carries one of four provenance tags, and the split is decidable by one operational test: could an independent laboratory refute the number WITHOUT running this simulator? Yes means MEASURED (the pinned floor, layer-2 or layer-3, refutable by observation, carrying error bars, not free); no means CLOSURE (an unpinned or weakly-pinned degree of freedom where turning the knob changes outcomes without contradicting a measurement). The other two tags are CONTINGENCY (the per-world layer-4 vector) and DERIVED (computed from the others by a named law).

The sharpening that makes the discipline sound: provenance is NOT a per-line label, it is a DAG property inherited from the leaves, and a DERIVED value is only as pinned as its least-pinned input. A number computed as `f(measured, closure, constant)` moves when the closure moves, so it passes the operational test (refutable without the sim) exactly when its derivation DAG bottoms out entirely in measured, constant, and contingency leaves, and it is CLOSURE-TAINTED the moment the DAG touches a single closure. So authorship hides not in the lines tagged closure (already flagged and hunted) but in the lines tagged derived whose ancestry quietly passes through one. The closure hunt is therefore a REACHABILITY QUERY on the provenance DAG ("which derived outputs have a closure in their transitive inputs"), and its answer is the true free-knob surface. Provenance propagates up the DAG exactly as numerical error does, by worst-case join at each node, so this is not a second structure: annotate the existing error-band DAG with a provenance lattice alongside the numerical band. Two orthogonal axes then govern each line, implementation status (built, stubbed, spec) and provenance (derived, measured, closure, contingency), and two standing audits run over them: a spec-to-repo diff over status (so "this sky is derived" is always scoped to what is wired, never the documentation believing itself), and closure-reachability over provenance.

The honest summary sentence for any such pipeline: a large measured floor (legitimate, refutable without the sim), a closure ledger of order a dozen entries several of which are fields or brackets rather than scalars (the real knobs, each named, each banded), a contingency vector (here just lightened of its heaviest member, the redox anchor), and a derived layer certified clean only where its DAG reaches no closure.

## Two precisions and the water gate

Line positions "from masses and Pyykko radii" overstates: the reduced mass is cache, but the force constant is not derivable from covalent radii (Badger's rule is an empirical fit with per-bond parameters and admitted exceptions), so it traces to the measured or authored covalent bond stiffness, which is the periodic-table-cache finding that covalent E_b stays authored. Line positions are masses plus the measured force constant.

Scope: the mass-gate, Bondi, and radius-valley module is NOT in the tree today (the current sim assumes a secondary atmosphere rather than deriving "secondary" from primary-envelope stripping), so this trace is the DESIGN SPEC of the full pipeline, the target the arc builds toward, not a description of what the sim currently runs. Do not mistake the map for the territory.

The water gate: the chi_AR flag is QUALITATIVE (this volatile condenses out of order), and the magnitude is the authored measured `(T_b, L)` primitive (the boiling-turn ruling that two numbers per liquid is the true information content). One procgen half-step: per-donor-class `E_HB` constants (the F/O/N ladder, roughly 29/21/13 kJ/mol, Legon-Millen) price the flag for invented volatiles (`L` gains `n * E_HB`, Trouton returns the shifted `T_b` at factor grade), with measured `T_b` the override wherever it exists. Flag says out of order; primitive says how much; class constant says roughly how much when nobody has measured it.
