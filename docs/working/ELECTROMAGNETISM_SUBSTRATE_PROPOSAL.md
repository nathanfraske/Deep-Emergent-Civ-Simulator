## 1. Framing: the next discoverable deepening tier, and the one representation seam it forces

Wave 0 built the biology-and-composition floor (R-PHYS-BIO), wave 1 the mechanical-and-materials-and-thermal floor (R-PHYS-MECH), and wave 2 the classical continuum-and-field floor of fluids, chemistry, and optics (R-PHYS-W2). Those three waves are the grounded Tier-0 floor: the physics a culture lives inside from the dawn, needing no discovery to be true. Wave 3 is categorically different. Electricity and magnetism are a **deepening tier** under R-DEEPTECH-PHYSICS, discoverable and tier-gated, unlocked by a culture's instruments and theory rather than authored on. A people does not begin knowing that charge separates, that a pile drives a current, or that a moving magnet induces one; it discovers these, in the historical and instrumental sequence, and the discovery is emergent from its technology (Principle 8), never a per-race unlock table (S-EM-TIER, Section 4).

The tier opens in three rungs that mirror the real instrumental ladder, each gated by the culture's instrument-and-theory state crossing a discovery threshold, the threshold and instruments themselves data and never authored preferences.

- **Rung 1, electrostatics** (`elec.charge`, `elec.potential`, `elec.capacitance`, `elec.electric_field`; `law.coulomb_force`, `law.capacitor_energy`). Opens when a culture holds charge-separation instruments (frictional charging of amber or resin, the gold-leaf electroscope that detects and orders charge, the Leyden jar that stores it) plus the theory of two charge polarities and the inverse-square (Coulomb's torsion balance).
- **Rung 2, current electricity** (`elec.current`, `elec.resistance`, `elec.resistivity`, `elec.emf`; `law.ohm_voltage`, `law.circuit_current`, `law.power_dissipation`, `law.battery_emf`, `law.resistance`). Opens with the galvanic or voltaic cell, a current detector, and the systematic voltage-current measurement that yields Ohm's law. The wave-2 electrochemistry is the natural discovery pathway (Section 5, the galvanic link): a culture that smelts metals and observes galvanic corrosion between two dissimilar metals in an electrolyte already holds the physical seed of the pile.
- **Rung 3, magnetism and induction** (`mag.flux_density`, `mag.flux`, `mag.permeability`, `mag.magnetic_moment`, `mag.inductance`, `mag.coupling_coefficient`; the solenoid, motor, Lorentz, dipole-torque, flux-linkage, Faraday, inductive-EMF, and inductor-energy laws). Opens from the lodestone and compass (magnetostatics), Oersted's observation that a current deflects a needle (the link that turns a current detector into a galvanometer, which is why rung 2's detector where magnetism is available is a galvanometer), and Faraday's observation that a changing field induces a current. This is the electromagnet, motor, dynamo, and transformer rung.

**The one seam this wave forces on the locked representation (S-ELEC-DIM).** The `Dimension` type is a monomial over exactly four base dimensions, length, mass, time, and temperature, each an `i8` exponent (`crates/physics/src/lib.rs` lines 54 to 64; design Part 55's `AbsoluteQuantity`). Every axis in waves 0 through 2 reduces to such a monomial, which is what makes the neutrality test a property of the type rather than a check that can be forgotten. Electricity breaks this: charge, current, and voltage are dimensionally independent of the four bases, and the Gaussian alternative that avoids a fifth base does so only by giving charge the dimension M^(1/2) L^(3/2) T^(-1), whose half-integer exponents the `i8` monomial cannot hold (the identical constraint that forced `chem.solute_affinity` to be carried dimensionless rather than as a Hildebrand parameter in MPa^0.5). So the SI route, a fifth base dimension of electric current, is the only representable one, and it is recommended over charge as the base (it is the SI ampere, and it is what every datasheet lists). Concretely, `Dimension` gains a fifth `i8` field, `current`, and Part 55's base-unit set gains the ampere.

This touches the locked representation, so it is surfaced for your sign-off as an audited Principle-9 act, not decided here. Two facts make it safe. First, it is backward-compatible and neutrality-preserving: every wave-0/1/2 axis keeps its meaning with a current exponent of zero, so `mech.force` becomes (1,1,-2,0,0) rather than changing, and the neutrality test still holds by construction, now over five bases. Second, the same fifth base serves both wave-3 domains: once current is a base, magnetism's flux density (0,1,-2,0,-1), flux (2,1,-2,0,-1), and inductance (2,1,-2,0,-2) are all expressible, so one representation extension unlocks the entire electromagnetic wave. This couples to Part 55 and R-UNITS-PIN (the unit-system pin), which already carries the Ratio-class and derived-monomial registrations the earlier floors need; the fifth base is registered alongside them.

Every kernel below is closed-form integer over the `Fixed` operations that exist (`checked_mul`, `checked_div`, `checked_add`, `saturating_add`/`saturating_sum`, `min`/`max` via `Ord`, `clamp`, `abs`, `sqrt`, and the exact `from_decimal_str` for constants; verified against `crates/core/src/fixed.rs`). No float and no transcendental reaches canon: `exp`, `log`, and trig are deferred to R-GPU-CANON-PIN, named rather than faked (Section 6). Each product that can exceed the Q32.32 ceiling (2^31, about 2.147e9) is formed with checked arithmetic and routed to a physical-limit cap; each quotient guards its zero divisor and routes to the correct physical extreme; and every output is a single scalar (or a scalar plus a sign or condition bool), so all reductions are order-independent.

## 2. The volt unification, and the shared axes

**The volt promotion of `chem.standard_potential` (the wave-2-to-wave-3 unification).** The wave-2 chemistry floor carries `chem.standard_potential` as a Tier-0 proxy: a dimensionless electromotive-ordering value on a relative scale, explicitly labelled "a Tier-0 proxy until the wave-3 E and M floor" (`crates/physics/data/chem_optics_floor.toml` lines 20 to 28). Wave 3 gives it its real dimension. The axis is promoted from dimensionless to the volt, (2,1,-3,0,-1) over the five bases, unifying it with `elec.potential` and `elec.emf`. The change is dimension-and-scale only: the range stays at its reserved `[-5, 5]` band (lithium about -3.05 V to fluorine about +2.87 V, with headroom), which sits comfortably inside the one-scale volt, so there is no numeric re-fit. The reference-condition reduction potential relative to the standard hydrogen electrode is exactly what the electrode-potential series lists in volts, so the promotion is a relabelling of an already-correct number, not a new datum. The concentration-dependent Nernst correction (a log term) stays deferred (Section 6), so this remains the standard-condition value, mirroring the wave-2 deferral of the `[H+]` activity behind stored pH.

This is the load-bearing unification of the wave. It closes the loop the wave-2 `law.corrosion` opened. That law computes a driving margin `E_fluid - E_material`, a subtraction of two `chem.standard_potential` values, and reports it as a dimensionless proxy because the volt did not yet exist. Wave 3's `law.battery_emf` is the identical subtraction, `E_cathode - E_anode`, now in real volts, producing `elec.emf` that drives `law.circuit_current`. The same electromotive series that measures corrosion drives the first battery, which is why the discovery gate for rung 2 is grounded rather than arbitrary (Section 5).

**The shared axes and dimensions.** Wave 3 adds no duplicate of any earlier quantity. Its force outputs (Coulomb, motor, Lorentz) land on the wave-1 `mech.force` dimension (1,1,-2,0,0); its energy outputs (capacitor, inductor) land on the shared energy dimension (2,1,-2,0,0); its power output (dissipation) lands on the shared power dimension (2,1,-3,0,0); its torque output (dipole) lands on the torque-energy dimension (2,1,-2,0,0). The one intra-wave shared dimension is the volt, carried by three role-distinct axes, `elec.potential` (a terminal state), `elec.emf` (a source's open-circuit drive), and the promoted `chem.standard_potential` (an electrode reference value), the same role-distinction the wave-2 fluids floor uses for `fluid.driving_pressure` against the pressure-law outputs. The overlap is surfaced (S-ELEC-EMF, Section 4) so you may collapse potential and EMF to one axis if you prefer, exactly as the wave-2 `law.thermal_buoyancy`/`law.ideal_gas_density` overlap was surfaced for collapse.

- **`law.faraday_emf`** [inputs: `mag.flux` now and the prior-tick sample, a reserved turn count N, `DT`; output: induced EMF V in `[-V_MAX, V_MAX]` onto `elec.emf`, signed]. **The per-tick induction delta.** `dphi = flux_now.saturating_sum(flux_prev.checked_mul(NEG_ONE)...)` (the signed change in flux over one tick); `rate = dphi.checked_div(DT)` (DT the fixed nonzero tick duration, so no zero divisor); `emf = rate.checked_mul(N).map(|x| x.clamp(V_MIN, V_MAX)).unwrap_or(sign-appropriate cap)`. The finite difference dPhi/DT is the discrete-time stand-in for the continuous dPhi/dt: `mag.flux` is a resident state axis, the consumer threads its prior-tick value (canonical state from the previous tick, exactly as any accumulator or rate is threaded in a fixed-step deterministic loop), and the delta is a deterministic signed subtraction. Verified: a slow field change (dPhi=0.01 Wb, DT=0.01 s, N=1000) gives 1000 V, and a fast circuit break (dPhi=1 Wb, DT=1e-3 s, N=100) gives 1e5 V, both representable, with faster transients routing to V_MAX. The Lenz sign (the minus, the opposition direction) is carried on the signed delta. Basis: EMF = -N*dPhi/dt (Faraday 1831; Griffiths Ch 7).
- **`law.inductive_emf`** [inputs: `mag.inductance` (self L, or mutual M = k*sqrt(L1*L2) formed by the consumer via `Fixed::sqrt`), `elec.current` now and the prior-tick sample, `DT`; output: EMF V in `[-V_MAX, V_MAX]` onto `elec.emf`, signed]. The Faraday form specialised to Phi = L*I (self) or M*I_source (mutual): `di = i_now.saturating_sum(i_prev.checked_mul(NEG_ONE)...)`; `rate = di.checked_div(DT)`; `emf = rate.checked_mul(inductance).map(|x| x.clamp(V_MIN, V_MAX)).unwrap_or(cap)`. The self form is the back-EMF that opposes a current change (the choke, and the spark on breaking a circuit); the mutual form is the transformer and induction-coil step-up the telephone and line repeaters use, with the turns-ratio voltage transformation emerging because L scales as N^2. Closes the sharpest R-COMMS gap (Section 4, the folded inductance gap). Basis: EMF = -L*dI/dt and EMF_2 = -M*dI_1/dt (Griffiths Ch 7; Horowitz and Hill).
- **`law.inductor_energy`** [inputs: `mag.inductance` (L), `elec.current` (I); output: stored energy J in `[0, E_MAX]`]. The magnetic dual of `law.capacitor_energy`, staged against the I^2 overflow: guard `I.abs() > I2_MAX` -> E_MAX; `half_l = L.checked_mul(HALF)` (None -> E_MAX); `t = half_l.checked_mul(I)` (None -> E_MAX); `U = t.checked_mul(I).map(|x| x.min(E_MAX)).unwrap_or(E_MAX)`. Verified representable across the practical envelope (L=10 H, I=1e3 A gives 5e6 J; the pathological large-L large-I corner routes to E_MAX). Stores the magnetic-field energy; the exp(-t R/L) transient is deferred (Section 6). Basis: U = (1/2)*L*I^2 (Griffiths Ch 7).

Determinism note (magnetism): all outputs are single scalars or a scalar plus a sign, order-independent. The one novelty is that `law.faraday_emf` and `law.inductive_emf` each read a prior-tick sample of a resident state axis, the only place the floor takes a time derivative; it is deterministic (the prior-tick value is canonical state, DT is a fixed engine constant, the delta is a signed subtraction) and it is a first-order finite difference whose fidelity is tick-rate-dependent, the honest limit stated in Section 6. `MU_0` is rendered by `from_decimal_str`, not computed; `Fixed::sqrt` (exact integer isqrt, deterministic, negative to ZERO) is used only in the consumer's mutual-inductance formation.

## 4. The folded red-team fixes and flagged steering seams

**S-ELEC-RECIP (folded): conductivity is not an axis.** Conductivity and resistivity are one physical quantity, exact reciprocals at Tier isotropy, so authoring both as independent axes invites an inconsistent authored pair (the wave-2 density-unification lesson). ## 6. Honest reach ceilings this wave does not cross

Named and left uncrossed rather than faked with an integer kernel at this Tier.

**Full Maxwell electromagnetic-wave propagation and radio (deferred toward wave 4).** The radiated wave (the antenna, propagation, the displacement current, radio and light-as-a-wave) needs the coupled time-derivatives of the E and B fields over space, the wave equation, which is a partial differential equation over a spatial field solver rather than a closed-form lumped kernel over an entity's quantity vector. **AC and the frequency domain (deferred toward wave 4), including the tuned LC circuit.** Sinusoidal steady state, reactance (X_L = omega*L and X_C = 1/(omega*C)), impedance, and phase need sinusoids (trig) and complex arithmetic, neither in canon. **Transcendental transients and corrections (deferred to R-GPU-CANON-PIN).** The exp(-t/RC) capacitor and exp(-t R/L) inductor charge/discharge transients, so the energy laws report the steady-state stored field energy; the Nernst concentration correction ln(Q), so `law.battery_emf` is the standard-condition cell EMF (the wave-2 pH/activity deferral); and the temperature coefficient of resistivity, for which a piecewise-linear proxy is available (the niche-response precedent) with the resistivity value the Tier constant. **Nonlinear and microscopic physics (deferred as later-tier deepenings).** Iron saturation and hysteresis (the nonlinear, history-dependent B-H loop), so `law.solenoid_field` is the linear-permeability field and a real transformer core's saturation and a permanent magnet's coercivity are a later refinement; the Biot-Savart line integral and the Maxwell stress tensor, so the field laws are lumped and idealised (the long solenoid, the straight conductor) rather than resolving arbitrary conductor geometry, the lumped-coefficient discipline of wave 2's drag Cd; and semiconductor and quantum behaviour (band gaps, diodes, transistors), which need Fermi-Dirac statistics and an exponential, a far-tier deepening.

**Representability ceilings surfaced as reserved scales (R-UNITS-PIN, S-EM-CONST).** The Coulomb constant rides a reserved x1e9 output scale (k = 8.9875e9 exceeds the 2.147e9 ceiling) and vacuum permittivity is never directly represented (8.854e-12 underflows epsilon); charge, resistance, resistivity, capacitance, magnetic moment, and inductance carry reserved per-quantity scales; resistivity's 24-order conductor-to-insulator envelope is regime-windowed to a conductor-and-semiconductor window because it exceeds even one scaled Q32.32 window (about 18.96 orders); and mu_0 carries a ~0.019% precision floor at its magnitude, refinable via a reserved x-scale (the SIGMA precedent).
