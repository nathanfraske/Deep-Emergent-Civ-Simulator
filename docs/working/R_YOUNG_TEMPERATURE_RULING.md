## 1. Decoupling approved, but the basis is REPLACED (a corrected derivation narrative)

The proposed basis, "first-order young temperature comes from accretional binding energy plus SLR heat," is the WRONG mechanism and fails the alien test on its own: a binding-energy-first budget assigns spuriously hot starts to slow-accreted small worlds, where energy arrives in small packets and radiates between deposits instead of accumulating, so the integral is not the input, the deposit SPECTRUM is. The correct license is already constitutional: the ATTRACTOR SHORTCUT. The magma ocean is a fast attractor whose forgetting time is the cooling-to-lockup time (Myr-class beyond the critical distance, up to the ~100 Myr desiccation window inside it), and that forgetting covers the impact series entirely. Any world that melts exits at the SOLIDUS-PINNED HANDOFF regardless of whether its peak was 3000 K or 6000 K, and impacts landing in a still-molten ocean are forgotten fastest of all. So the impact list was never the SOURCE of the handoff temperature; it is the CHRONOMETER and the marginal-band resolver. The doc cites the forgetting-kernel law, not an energy budget. (Ledger symmetry: correct executable path, defective derivation narrative, the same Terran-fact-as-mechanism defect class the five-lens panel caught in the texture commit, caught pre-ship this time in the agent's own proposed basis.)

## 2. The deliverable is a REGIME VERDICT, not a temperature

Three branches:
- MELTED: the handoff is pinned at the world's own solidus through the rheological lock-up fraction phi_c. GAPPED (decidable) without the impact list for two large classes: Earth-mass-and-up assembly (giant impacts are class-generic, roughly two dozen per system) and early-formed planetesimal-fed worlds (26Al melts them on its own).
- NEVER-MELTED: late-formed, slow-accreted, SLR-poor bodies, where the cold-accretion budget applies, with the energy-retention efficiency entering as a named, banded [E] entry (legal because it is PREFACTOR-resident, never exponent-resident).
- MARGINAL: near-degenerate by the Gap Law, carried and surfaced, never asserted, until the per-world impact list resolves it.

MANDATORY SELF-TEST (in code): the retention band must NOT be able to flip any verdict tagged GAPPED. If varying the knob inside its band flips an outcome, that outcome was marginal and mis-tagged (exactly the co-determinant failure the texture audit caught, now an automated check rather than a panel finding).
