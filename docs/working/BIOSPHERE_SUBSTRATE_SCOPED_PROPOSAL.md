# The Biology-and-Composition Substrate Floor: Scoped Proposal (wave 0)

**Status: a proposal, reserved-with-basis, hardened through two independent red-team Steering Audits and now sign-off ready. Nothing here is canon. No numeric value is set; each axis and constant carries the ground on which the owner would set it.** This is wave 0 of the physics-and-materials fan-out (see `PHYSICS_SUBSTRATE_FANOUT_PLAN.md`), the biology and composition slice R-BIOSPHERE reads its composition axes from. It was produced by four scoped facet agents (composition/nutrition, toxicity/defense, consumer physiology, and the law forms), each grounded in cited science and held to the locked representation, then attacked by a first red-team Steering Audit (Section R) whose three blocking findings were folded, and then re-attacked by a second independent audit (Section R2) that recomputed the Q32.32 arithmetic and found the first fold incomplete. The second audit's confirmed determinism, steering, and reach findings are folded into the axes and laws below; its deferred findings are carried as flagged seams for the registry-population and full Steering Audit pass.

Three owner decisions taken before this revision (non-final, select-confirm) shape it: the edibility law returns only the measured tuple and the gain-versus-danger valuation moves to the agent decision layer (Parts 8, 20); the consolidation is recorded as a partial advance of R-DEEPTECH-PHYSICS with a findable sub-identifier for this floor; wave 1 is the mechanical-and-materials floor.

## The locked representation it builds on

From the substrate guide, Section 4: a `QuantityAxis { id, unit, fixed_point_range, tier }`, a `Substance` as a vector over axes plus the laws it participates in plus a provenance tag, and an `InteractionLaw { id, inputs:[AxisId], kernel (closed-form integer), output_measure, interval_bound, tier }`. Edibility, nutrition, and harm are outputs of laws over a food vector and a consumer vector, never stored flags. The same berry is food to one race, poison to another, and medicine to a third because the law reads two different consumer vectors, not because the berry carries a verdict.

Four framing decisions hold throughout. Composition is per-tissue (a fruit, leaf, tuber, muscle, organ, and bone of one organism differ), so an organism is a set of addressable tissue vectors. Supply axes are mass fractions of fresh tissue in [0, 1] with energy carried explicitly, with the standing limit that mass-fraction-of-fresh-tissue assumes a massy, tissue-eating metabolism (the reach boundary the source-vector generalization of Section I addresses, deferred). Every axis is tier-tagged so a base-physics world runs on the Tier-0 floor and a deeper or magical world adds tiers with no code change. And the cross-tier invariant is scoped precisely: a lumped parent equals the sum of its resolved children for an additive mass-fraction quantity consumed linearly (so a consumer reading the lump quantity and one reading the child quantities never disagree on the quantity), but a nonlinear law output over those quantities (harm through the Hill kernel, available energy through per-consumer assimilation) is tier-resolution-dependent by construction, so a Tier-0 reader and a Tier-1 reader can compute a different harm or available energy from the same food. That difference is the coarser physics of the aggregate tier, intended deepening rather than a leak, and it couples to R-TIER-CONSIST and R-PROJ-REGISTER (Part 58): the conserved projection is the additive quantity, never the nonlinear law output.

## A. Composition (food-side nutrition) axes

**Tier 0, the gross-composition floor (all real-with-source):** `water_fraction` (perishability and energy dilution, [0,1]); `energy_density` (gross energy per gram fresh, kJ/g, [0, ~38], the lipid ceiling); `protein_fraction` ([0, ~0.9], lean-dried ceiling); `lipid_fraction` ([0, ~1.0]); `nonstructural_carbohydrate_fraction` (storage and soluble sugars and starch, [0, ~1.0]); `structural_carbohydrate_fraction` (fiber, cellulose, and lignin, [0, ~0.9] wood ceiling, the core relational axis: energy to a fermenter, ballast otherwise); `mineral_ash_fraction` ([0, ~1.0], bone ceiling); `toxin_load_coarse` (a Tier-0 aggregate of the Tier-1 toxin-class concentrations in their shared mg/kg concentration scale (the Tier-1 scale rules below), the `saturating_sum` of every Tier-1 toxin-class child, with no [0,1] re-scaling and no per-class danger weighting, so it is dimensionally the total toxin mass and carries no consumer-independent hazard ranking). Basis for each range: the natural concentration envelope the cited food-composition tables define (USDA FoodData Central, FAO/INFOODS, Greenfield and Southgate, Van Soest, Atwater).

Two corrections the second audit forced. The carbohydrate axes are now chemistry-keyed (`nonstructural` and `structural`), not consumer-keyed: the earlier `digestible_carbohydrate_fraction` presupposed the monogastric eater for whom non-structural carbohydrate is digestible, where the relational form names the fraction by chemistry and lets each consumer's assimilation and fermentation decide what it can extract (the F2 ranking re-imported on the nutrition side, NEW-STEER-1). And `energy_density` is a stored gross axis (bomb-calorimetry gross energy), not the sum of the macronutrient fractions: gross energy includes ethanol, organic acids, polyphenols, and the full structural-carbohydrate enthalpy that the protein, lipid, and carbohydrate fractions do not separately carry, so a fermented or acidic or high-polyphenol tissue has a stored gross energy above any macronutrient-derived figure (NEW-REACH-3). The consumer-available energy is a per-consumer derived measure over the macronutrient and fermentation axes the consumer can assimilate, not equal to the stored gross, and the lump-equals-children invariant is scoped off the energy path accordingly.

**Tier 1, essentiality and specific hazards (data registries, real-with-source):** the indispensable amino-acid fractions resolving protein (a registry, since indispensability is the consumer's property, so the food carries the full profile, with a residual-protein child so the resolved children plus the residual equal `protein_fraction` exactly, the additive home for non-protein nitrogen and for compounds not yet essential to any race, NEW-REACH-6); the essential fatty-acid class fractions resolving lipid (n-3, n-6, the saturated and monounsaturated balance, with a residual-lipid child); the specific mineral-element fractions resolving ash (macrominerals and trace elements, several simultaneously essential and toxic above a threshold, with a residual-ash child); and the toxin-class concentrations resolving the coarse toxin load (see family B, all Tier 1). Two scale rules keep the trace axes both fine and additively consistent (the second audit's NEW-DET-3 and the toxin-sum reconciliation). The axes that are additively aggregated into a Tier-0 lump (the toxin-class concentrations that sum into `toxin_load_coarse`, and the resolved amino-acid, fatty-acid, and mineral fractions that sum with their residual into their parent) share their parent's common concentration scale, so the `saturating_sum` over raw bits is the exact additive sum; that shared concentration scale (mg/kg of tissue for the toxin classes) resolves a sub-ng/kg concentration without underflow, since 1 ng/kg is about 4295 Q32.32 bits, far above the resolution floor (2.328e-10). The axes that are read individually and never summed across classes (the consumer's per-class reference tolerance and the trace nutrient requirements, both per body mass and reaching ng/kg or vitamin scale) each carry their own per-quantity Part 55 scale chosen for their own range, since a distinct fine scale there breaks no lump. In both families an undefined or not-required axis is a distinct sentinel from a value that underflowed to zero (NEW-DET-3).

**Tier 2, fine constituents (real-with-source):** vitamin contents (a registry, since vitamin essentiality is species-specific, the vitamin-C and scurvy case); antinutrient and bioavailability-modifier contents (phytate, tannins, enzyme inhibitors, the food-side hook for cooking and fermentation to act on).

**Exotic tier (fantasy-reserved-with-basis):** `mana_content` (metabolizable magical energy, parallel to `energy_density`, bounded by the magic substrate's mana-density axis, with the coupling that wave 0 cannot self-verify the Q32.32 multiply headroom of a delegated bound, so when the magic substrate sets that range it is checked against the energy law's `mul` headroom or the mana path uses `checked_mul`, NEW-REACH-7); `exotic_nutrient_*` and `exotic_toxin_*` registry slots, bounded by analogy to the closest real class, gated on the world profile carrying the magic axis.

## B. Toxin and chemical-defense axes (substance-side, a terran-chemistry starting registry)

The second audit corrected the framing here. The earlier text claimed the axes were keyed by mechanism of action, but the seed list enumerates a terran chemical and biosynthetic taxonomy (alkaloid spans four distinct molecular targets; phenolic, terpenoid, and saponin are structural groupings), so the neutrality claim was contradicted by its own descriptors (NEW-STEER-3). The honest framing: this is a terran-chemistry starting registry, not a closed enum and not yet a mechanism partition. It is a registry (a new compound is a new point; a behaviour no class carries is an audited floor extension), and the demand-closure rule that drives splits is mechanism-or-target-keyed (split a class when two compounds need different consumer receptors), so the registry repartitions toward mechanism as generated races make different vulnerabilities matter. That repartition is the registry-population Steering Audit's job; what wave 0 seeds is the terran starting set, named as such.

Load axes (concentration, real-with-source, all Tier 1, summed into the Tier-0 `toxin_load_coarse`): alkaloid; cyanogenic and hydrolytic-release (the precursor-plus-enzyme, preparation-detoxifies case); cardiac and steroidal glycoside (Na/K-ATPase, mechanism-tagged, the narrow-window medicine-or-poison exemplar); oxalate and mineral-chelator; protease, amylase, and lectin (digestion-disrupting proteins, heat-labile); phenolic and tannin (protein-precipitating); terpenoid, saponin, and irritant resin (membrane-disrupting); and proteinaceous and peptide venom-toxin (the animal-defense analogue). Putting every per-class load axis at Tier 1 and the `toxin_load_coarse` aggregate at Tier 0 removes the tier-tag contradiction the second audit found (the earlier text tagged alkaloid and cyanogenic Tier 0 while defining the coarse lump as the sum of the Tier-1 classes, so the lump omitted its own dominant child, NEW-REACH-2). It also keeps the Tier-0 floor mechanism-neutral: a base-physics world carries only the total toxin mass, and no specific terran chemistry is mandatory until demand-closure opens a Tier-1 class (NEW-STEER-9, the residual edge carried as a flagged seam).

Generic dose and pharmacokinetic axes: per-class therapeutic-window width (the medicine-versus-poison carrier, the therapeutic index); bioaccumulation and persistence (acute versus chronic); heavy-metal and toxic-element load (geology-coupled); and per-class preparation-lability (a class-by-operation matrix, the cook, soak, and ferment to detoxify enabler, Tier 0 for heat and soak). The reference-potency axis is recast: it is a pure unit convention (the scale in which the consumer's tolerance for the class is read), not a substance-side per-compound multiplier on dose. The harm law reads only dose and the consumer's per-class tolerance, never a substance-side potency, so no Tier-0 path lets a substance-side potency ordering become a consumer-independent danger ranking (NEW-STEER-4). Exotic slot (fantasy-reserved): magic-coupled toxin load, bound to the magic-coupling axis and measured against the imbued physiology.

## C. Consumer physiology axes (the eater half of the relation)

Per-consumer, per-race, heritable (gene-fed phenotypes under Part 25, effect sizes reserved), paired with the food-side axes so a closed-form law contracts them. The set of relation kinds below is itself an extensible registry on the same footing as the toxin and nutrient registries, not a closed enum: a metabolism whose limit is a relation kind no existing kind carries (a co-factor dependence where one nutrient gates another's assimilation, a temporal or sensory gating of uptake) triggers a deliberate audited floor extension, the closure test being a relation a race needs that no kind expresses (NEW-STEER-6).

Family A, the demand side: per-nutrient-class requirement (the deficiency floor, a distinct not-required sentinel from a zero requirement); tolerable upper bound (closing the requirement into a window); and endogenous-synthesis capacity (the formal home of essential versus non-essential as a per-race datum, zero synthesis meaning dietary-essential). Family B, the access side: per-class-and-form assimilation efficiency ([0,1], zero where no pathway exists, the axis that carries digestibility consumer-side rather than as a food scalar); fermentation capacity (the cellulose-to-energy axis, the assimilation coefficient for structural carbohydrate, dependent on a hosted symbiont); and retention time (how long matter is held, coupling digestion and detox). The second audit forced the exponent into the consumer vector: a single global Hill exponent would author one dose-response shape for all biology, and a substance-side exponent would be a consumer-independent shape ranking, so the steepness is a per-consumer datum, and Family C's earlier continuous slope axis is reconciled to it (the integer-coefficient restriction the no-`exp`/`log` rule imposes makes the slope a choice of the small integer n rather than a continuous value, NEW-STEER-8). Family D, fantasy-reserved slots: mana requirement (structured as Family A); exotic-toxin detox and sensitivity (imbued immunity as the saturation limit); and exotic assimilation (lithotrophy, photosynthesis, a stone-eater or sun-feeder as a point over the same axes, with the unit caveat of Section I).

The gain-versus-danger risk weighting is no longer a consumer physiology axis. On the owner's decision it lives in the agent valuation layer (Parts 8, 20), not in the substrate, so the floor carries no risk attitude at all (NEW-STEER-2, below).

## E. The interaction laws (closed-form fixed-point)

All laws use only the `Fixed` ops that exist (`mul`, `div`, `checked_mul`, `checked_div`, `from_ratio`, `clamp`, `sub`, `add`, `sqrt`, the `Ord` comparison `Fixed` derives, and the order-independent `checked_sum` and `saturating_sum`); none needs `pow`, `exp`, or `log`. The Liebig minimum and the harm cap are `Ord` comparisons (equivalently a `clamp`), an exact integer comparison that is deterministic and order-independent, not a floating-point primitive. The `Fixed` semantics that bind the kernels: `mul` and the unchecked `div` narrow a 128-bit intermediate to `i64` and wrap silently when the result value exceeds the representable ceiling (about 2.147e9), so any operand product or quotient that can exceed the ceiling is formed with the checked variant and the out-of-range case is routed to the correct physical limit, never left to wrap. This discipline is the heart of the second determinism fold.

**Net nutrition** (`law.nutrition.net`): per nutrient class, the absorbed supply `num = supply x assimilation` (both in [0,1], product in [0,1], safe), then the satisfaction ratio `num.checked_div(requirement)` clamped to [0,1], with two boundary rules: a not-required class (the sentinel, the nutrient the consumer does not need) contributes `satisfaction := ONE` and never lowers the minimum, and an abundant supply against a tiny requirement returns `None` from `checked_div` and saturates to `ONE` rather than wrapping to a false zero (NEW-DET-3, the wrap that the earlier unchecked `div` produced at a one-bit requirement). Then the Liebig minimum across the required classes (the limiting nutrient, a min-fold that is associative and order-independent). There is no trailing food-digestibility multiplier: digestibility is carried entirely by the consumer's per-class assimilation and fermentation, so a fermenter's correct high value for structural carbohydrate is not suppressed by a food-side scalar (NEW-STEER-1). Output in [0,1]. Basis: Liebig's law of the minimum and the bioavailability and digestibility-coefficient concept. A multiplicative co-limitation form is a drop-in alternative if the hard minimum proves brittle.

**Harm** (`law.harm.dose_response`): per toxin class, with the consumer's per-class reference tolerance and per-class integer exponent n. The kernel forms the dose ratio safely and orders the saturation guard before any power:

```
for class c in the consumer's toxin relations:
    match consumer.tolerance(c):
        NotApplicable          => continue            # the class is irrelevant: contributes 0, skipped
        Tolerance(t):
            if dose_c == ZERO   => harm_c = ZERO
            else:
                match dose_c.checked_div(t):           # t.bits == 0 (max sensitivity) OR ratio out of range => None
                    None        => harm_c = HARM_CAP    # the correct physical limit: a huge ratio is maximum harm
                    Some(r):
                        if r > r_sat(n_c)               # guard BEFORE the power, so r^n is never formed out of range
                                => harm_c = HARM_CAP
                        else:
                            let rn  = pow_int(r, n_c)    # r <= r_sat(n_c) <= overflow-safe ceiling, so r^n is in range
                            harm_c = rn.div(rn.add(ONE)) # r^n / (r^n + 1); denominator >= ONE, never zero, result in [0,1)
net_harm = saturating_sum(harm_c over c).min(HARM_CAP)   # order-independent (i128 accumulate), then capped
```

Three corrections the second audit forced are in this kernel. The dose ratio is formed with `checked_div`, so a ng/kg-tolerance against a multi-thousand-mg/kg dose (a true ratio up to about 3.8e10, eighteen times the representable ceiling) routes to the saturation limit instead of wrapping `r` to a small or negative value the old guard could not catch (NEW-DET-2). The not-applicable sentinel is distinct from a zero tolerance: not-applicable skips the class (zero harm), while a present-but-zero tolerance is maximum sensitivity and yields maximum harm through the `checked_div` `None` branch, so an underflowed or maximally vulnerable consumer is no longer silently read as immune (NEW-TOL0, NEW-DET-3). And `r_sat(n)` is keyed off the representable ceiling of `r^n` with the guard ordered before the power: the earlier basis (the ratio where `r^n/(r^n+1)` is within one epsilon of 1) gave 1625 for n=3 and 65536 for n=2, both above the overflow-safe bound 2^(31/n), so a band of r passed the guard and still wrapped (NEW-DET-1). The relational medicinal and hormetic credit is no longer subtracted inside this law (it was an unwritten prose term, NEW-DET-4, and a global compound-to-benefit map, NEW-STEER-5); medicinal value is derived relationally at the edibility level (below) and the toxin-hormesis biphasic credit is reserved (Section I) rather than consolidated as prose. Basis: the Hill equation with the integer-coefficient restriction stated openly (a real exponent would need `exp` and `log`, which the substrate forbids).

**Edibility (the measured tuple)** (`law.edibility.measure`): the law returns the measured tuple `(net_nutrition, net_harm, margin, medicinal_value)` and emits no value-laden verdict scalar. On the owner's decision the gain-versus-danger weighting (the former `w_n`, `w_h`, and their linear trade-off) lives in the agent decision layer (Parts 8, 20), heritable per-race and acted on by selection, so the one authored physics layer carries no risk attitude and no combination form (NEW-STEER-2). `margin` is the aggregate safety ratio `tolerance_aggregate.checked_div(dose_aggregate)`, routing `None` (a near-clean meal with a tiny nonzero aggregate dose, or a zero dose) to `margin_cap` and clamping the in-range result to `margin_cap`, so the safe-meal limit is reached by saturation rather than by the wrap the earlier unchecked `div` produced (NEW-DET-5). `margin` is a coarse triage and display measure derived from aggregates, never an input to the nonlinear harm law. `medicinal_value` is the relational measure of deficiency-correction at low harm: the share of `net_nutrition` that fills a class the consumer is deficient in, attenuated as `net_harm` rises, derived from the tuple and the consumer's own deficiency vector with no substance-side medicinal map (NEW-STEER-5; the exact combination form is reserved, Section G). Any food, poison, medicine, or inert label is a strictly read-time band that a viewer or the agent layer computes over the tuple, never stored and never an input to any simulation law. Basis: the therapeutic-index and margin-of-safety construction.

## F. The Tier-0 completeness criterion

The fan-out gate requires that a tier state and meet a completeness criterion (the test that the floor is complete-enough for that tier). For the Tier-0 biology floor the criterion is demand-closure at the floor level: Tier 0 is complete-enough when the first build proof's food web (the Stage-14 convergence proof, Part 41) can be made fully relational, every eater-source pairing computing its measured tuple through an axis the floor already carries, with no pairing requiring an absent axis. Met is demonstrated by enumerating that proof's organisms and feeding relations and confirming each routes through an existing axis; the actual Stage-14 biota is enumerated against the scenario at consolidation, and a representative minimal matter-eating food web demonstrates the shape now: a plant carrying leaf, fruit, and structural tissue with macronutrient and mineral fractions and one defensive toxin class; a herbivore whose requirement, assimilation, fermentation, detox, and tolerance axes pair with those tissues to compute nutrition, harm, and edibility; and a carnivore whose axes pair with the herbivore's muscle and organ tissue. Each pairing routes through the composition axes (A), the toxin axes (B), and the physiology axes (C) with no absent axis, so the floor is complete-enough for a matter-eating food web. Field-feeders (photovore, thermovore, lithotroph, mana-feeder) are out of the first proof by construction, and that scoped exclusion is exactly what licenses the source-vector deferral (F5, Section I): the criterion and the deferral are stated together (NEW-REACH-1).

## G. The reserved values, surfaced with basis (nothing fabricated)

Every axis fixed-point range (the mg/kg, mg/kg-body, and fraction bounds): basis is the natural concentration and dose envelope the cited literature defines, the owner setting the bound at or just above the documented maximum. The per-quantity Part 55 scale for each trace nutrient and potency axis: basis is the smallest physiologically meaningful concentration the axis must resolve without underflowing Q32.32 (ng/kg-scale toxins, vitamin-scale needs near 5e-9 mass fraction), with the undefined or not-required sentinel kept distinct from an underflowed-to-zero value. Every per-tier assignment: basis is demand-closure, Tier 0 only where the first proof's food web cannot be made relational without the axis. The consumer requirement, upper-bound, synthesis, assimilation, fermentation, retention, detox, sensitivity, and reference-tolerance vectors: basis is the published figure for an anchoring taxon scaled allometrically, never a human default. The founding-distribution variance (or coefficient of variation) per heritable physiology axis: basis is the observed within-taxon physiological spread, or an explicit deferral to Part 25 heritability ownership, so the biological-diversity dial is set by a stated basis rather than by silence (NEW-STEER-7). The allometric scaling exponents: basis is the comparative-physiology literature, stated as an authored Principle-9 affordance with its bound on expressible body plans, or made a per-race datum so a race can carry a different size-to-demand law (NEW-STEER-10).

The harm-law constants: the per-(toxin-class, consumer) integer Hill exponent n in {1,2,3} (basis: the integer matching the observed sigmoidicity for the class and consumer, n=1 hyperbolic and n=2 or 3 sigmoid, the home of Family C's heritable steepness); the per-n saturation ratio `r_sat` (basis: the minimum of the within-one-epsilon ratio 2^(32/n) and the overflow-safe ratio keyed off the representable ceiling of `r^n` less one-ONE headroom, which for the Q32.32 representation is 1290 for n=3, 46340 for n=2, and the representable r ceiling for n=1, a hard upper bound the owner may set lower for earlier saturation but never higher); the per-class harm cap `HARM_CAP` (basis: the maximum per-class harm before the cross-class `saturating_sum`, set within one epsilon of ONE so the saturation limit is continuous with the computed value at `r_sat`); and `margin_cap` (basis: the safety-margin ceiling reported when the aggregate dose is zero or the checked margin division returns out of range). The relational medicinal-and-deficiency-correction credit form (basis: derived from the consumer's own deficiency state in `net_nutrition` and Family A and attenuated by `net_harm`, with any surviving substance-side datum tagged per-consumer rather than a global compound-to-benefit map). The toxin-hormesis biphasic credit (basis: the Calabrese biphasic dose-response shape expressed as a fixed-point difference or product of Hill terms with the same `r_sat` guard ordering and an order-independent `saturating_sum` reduction, reserved rather than consolidated because the curve shape is a modelling choice and not a fact to copy). The verdict-band thresholds are read-time viewer parameters only. The exotic axes and slots: fantasy-reserved-with-basis on the magic-as-grounded-laws precedent, with the `mana_content` mul-headroom check (NEW-REACH-7). The `tolerance==0` and not-applicable semantics: an owner direction with its basis stated (the chosen reading is not-applicable skips the class and a present zero is maximum sensitivity, so missing or zero data does not silently bias the tuple toward edible, NEW-TOL0). The risk weights and the gain-versus-danger trade-off, relocated to the agent valuation layer (Parts 8, 20): basis is the consumer's heritable risk tolerance, with the founding distribution surfaced there, since a prior centered near a 1:1 attitude would reinstate the authored attitude the relocation removes.

The boundary values that are defined rather than reserved: the not-required and not-applicable sentinels, `requirement not-required => satisfaction = ONE`, `dose == 0 => harm = 0`, the `checked_div None` routings (`harm = HARM_CAP` for the harm ratio, `satisfaction = ONE` for the nutrition ratio, `margin = margin_cap` for the margin). The allometric and anchoring caveats stand: the reserved physiology bases anchor to an anchoring taxon, a terran default the registry-population audit must broaden (Section H).

## I. Open seams for the owner's decision

The source-vector reach generalization (F5), deferred with corrected wording: the composition catalogue assumes a matter-eating metabolism, and several matter-adjacent metabolisms (filter-feeding, detritivory, parasitism, and hematophagy) and the field-and-gradient feeders (photovore, thermovore, chemotroph and lithotroph over a redox gradient, mana-feeder) have no clean home in a static mass-fraction-of-fresh-tissue vector. And the genesis seeding of the nutrient and toxin registries (F4 above), the deepest of the carried seams.

## R2. Second independent red pass (findings and disposition)

Before consolidation a second independent audit (a four-lens fan-out with two deeper second-pass finders) recomputed the Q32.32 arithmetic rather than trusting the first fold, on the prime directive that a conclusion is proven most of all when it is the project's own.
