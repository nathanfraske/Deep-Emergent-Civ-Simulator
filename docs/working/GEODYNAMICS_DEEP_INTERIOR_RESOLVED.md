## The chain, top to bottom

- **Interior structure and the EOS.** The Vinet EOS follows from the universal binding-energy relation (Rose et al. 1984), so a substance's cohesive energy generates its compression curve to a scaling; the per-phase reference density, bulk modulus, and its pressure derivative are per-material measured (or deeper ab-initio) data, not derivable from cohesive energy alone. Integrating hydrostatic equilibrium gives planetary radius and gravity from mass and composition (rocky worlds near `R ~ M^0.27`, Valencia/Sotin), and the moment-of-inertia factor (Earth 0.3307 vs the uniform-sphere 0.4) signs the core.
- **Thermal expansivity (the corrected column).** The built ideal-gas buoyancy law (`alpha = 1/T`) overstates a condensed mantle's expansivity about twentyfold and would misplace worlds on the regime diagram, so `alpha` is a DERIVED column, `alpha = gamma*rho*c_v/K_T` (Slater `gamma` from `B_0'`, Dulong-Petit `c_v`, EOS `K_T`), forsterite 4.3e-5 against measured ~3e-5. It self-improves with depth (K_T rises, so `alpha` falls toward the core-mantle boundary).
- **Melting curve.** The Lindemann-Debye route with a Slater Grueneisen parameter (an estimator with a 20-30% spread among alternatives), checked at the iron inner-core-boundary melt (~6200 K). The hydrous flux depression is mostly derived: water dissociates to two hydroxyls in silicate melt (Stolper 1982), so van 't Hoff cryoscopy runs with `i ~ 2`, ~19 K/wt% against Katz 2003's calibrated 43, factor-two grade with one calibrated coefficient.
- **Creep viscosity.** The Arrhenius barrier is `E* = g*R*T_m` with `g` a structural constant per bonding-class-and-mechanism by corresponding states (metals ~17-18, covalent ~26-30; copper ~18, olivine ~29.5, ice ~26); the prefactor is the lattice vibration frequency (Vineyard 1957), giving the canonical diffusion prefactor; the activation volume `V* = g*R*(dT_m/dP)` is the derived solidus slope, not a new residue. The absolute viscosity is anchored to post-glacial rebound (an in-sim discoverable).
- **Convection and the thermostat.** The critical Rayleigh number is the exact eigenvalue `27*pi^4/4`; `Nu ~ Ra^(1/3)` damps a hundredfold viscosity uncertainty to a few-fold heat-flux uncertainty, so the flux and the secular cooling are robust. The Tozer thermostat's forgetting time is itself derived, `tau ~ C*R*T^2/(beta*E*/Q)`: 0.86 Gyr at `beta = 1/3`, ~2.9 Gyr at `beta = 0.1`. The peaked ~2.9 Ga mantle-potential-temperature record favors weak feedback and multi-Gyr memory, so the initial thermal state `T_p,0` is a real contingency-vector entry and the Urey split is a derived mix (attractor pull plus a `e^(-t/tau(beta))` memory term, `beta`'s contested range propagating in).
- **The tectonic regime (the deleted authored knob).** The old sovereign yield knob authored a planetary outcome (Principle-9 steering) and is deleted. It decomposes into an effective-stress reduction (Terzaghi, exact), a phyllosilicate class friction (a bond-topology rule the table sees), and a grain-damage memory (Bercovici-Ricard, the healing barrier another `g` class constant), with cracking guaranteed by the thermal-stress-over-tensile-strength budget wherever an ocean exists. The regime selector is the Christensen-Yuen phase-buoyancy parameter `P = Gamma*d_rho/(alpha*rho^2*g*h)`, every factor derived, with layering below a critical `P_crit(Ra)` (a universal FUNCTION carrying an error band, never a per-world scalar). So plates equal `F(state, history)`, bistable with hysteresis, marginal near the transition, and a world can cross from episodic-avalanche convection early to whole-mantle later as it cools. The verification confirmed the field cannot date Earth's own plate onset to within gigayears, so a selector that called Earth's regime confidently would be overfit; the marginal flag is the correct output.
- **Radiogenics and outgassing.** Decay heat per event is the mass defect times `c^2`, so with isotope masses tabulated only the half-lives and branching ratios stay authored (a universal layer-2 table, not per-world). Present surface flow ~47 TW, radiogenic ~20 TW, geoneutrino-measurable. Outgassing crosses the solidus by decompression on the adiabat, predicting 6-7 km of oceanic crust. The WHAK weathering thermostat's EXISTENCE does not depend on the rate constant (steady state pins weathering to the derived outgassing flux); the constant only sets the equilibrium carbon-dioxide setpoint, so the residue shrinks to a bracketed solvent barrier `E_a ~ 40-80 kJ/mol` with the solvent-mediated flag.
- **Tidal heating.** The standard rate with the derived radius and eccentricity exponents; the Love number follows from interior elasticity, leaving the quality factor `Q` as the residue (rock ~100, ice lower), with the modern giant-planet astrometric surprise flagged.
- **Fluvial incision.** The concavity (exponent ratio) derives; the erodibility `K` dissolves into the strength substrate, since abrasion incision scales as tensile-strength to the minus two (Sklar & Dietrich 2001) and tensile strength is the damage-state variable, leaving one universal abrasion coefficient.
