# Life-derivation frontiers: the derive-first scoping (owner directive, in progress)

Owner-directed exploration (2026-07-11): scope how the life/biology frontiers DERIVE rather than being templated. Produced by the `explore-life-derivation-frontiers` workflow (five derive-first explorers, Opus, lenses baked in) plus this manager's cross-cutting synthesis (the automated synthesis agent hit a structured-output cap; the five scopings stand and are captured here). The headline: most of each frontier already derives from the built floor, the exotic cases the owner named are data rows, and the authored residue is short and per-organism measured, with no global authored outcome.

## The five frontiers

- **Exotic energy-capture metabolisms.** The chemotroph and lithotroph are already fully derived and built: captured free energy is `battery_emf = E_acceptor - E_donor` from the layer-2 standard potentials with the Nernst depletion correction. A radiotroph on an X-ray-bright star is a data row naming the field as the world's ionizing-flux scalar, needing one new law: a Beer-Lambert ionizing-deposition kernel (`deposited_dose = flux*(1 - exp(-mu*column))`), the same escape-and-deposition physics as the atmospheric EUV. Every pathway is a row of one table (field-id, read-shape, conversion-law-id, organism data), never a metabolism enum. Residue: floor constants plus per-organism measured data plus a fail-loud coupling efficiency.
- **Digestion and assimilation.** Mostly derives: food-ness is measured, not stored (`edibility::assess` reads a `Composition` against a `Physiology`). The primitive is catalyst-on-substrate Michaelis-Menten throughput releasing the redox free energy `-qE` of the couple. Essentially no global authored residue: `kcat` per catalyst class is a measured per-world datum, the enzyme set is heritable and selected. An alien gut is a set of data rows keyed on the being's own physiology.
- **Memory.** Most of it is already in the repo as one primitive: `Mind.beliefs` is accumulated log-odds per belief subject through the evidence engine. The primitive is a bounded `subject -> strength` map with WRITE (fold evidence when a percept co-occurs with a felt reserve delta), DECAY, READ (a controller input the weight can lift off zero), and EVICT at capacity. The one irreducible residue is the information-storage capacity, a tissue-composition floor axis like energy density. A being with no neural tissue is a data row.
- **Forgetting and memory fade.** Most of the timescale derives: the e-folding time is `tau = 1/k`, with `k` the memory medium's Arrhenius turnover (`reference_rate*exp(-Ea/(k_B*T))`), set by the being's own body temperature and metabolic throughput, retrieval re-depositing the trace. Residue: one per-organism substrate datum (the medium's decay constant and its `Ea`). A crystalline-lattice mind carries a near-zero decay constant, forgetting on geological timescales, a data row.
- **Abiogenesis, the origin of evolving life.** More derives than expected, because the pre-biotic reaction network already derives: `laws::reaction` computes reaction free energy by Hess's law over substance vectors and gates on a temperature barrier, and `battery_emf` supplies the driving free energy. The primitive is local mass-action kinetics over a compartment's substance vector, driven by the free energy its bounding gradient supplies, under mass conservation, never reading life-status. The origin is an emergent threshold crossing where autocatalysis, a compartment, and a sustained gradient co-occur; an alien abiogenesis on a different solvent is the same mechanism reading different substance rows.

## The cross-cutting synthesis

One shared spine, already built: the redox free-energy engine (`battery_emf` and Nernst) powers exotic metabolisms, digestion, and abiogenesis alike; the Hess-law reaction substrate powers abiogenesis and digestion; the evidence engine already IS memory; and the Arrhenius `Fixed::exp` staging powers forgetting, digestion kinetics, and the metabolism. The common primitive across all five is one shape: a per-being or per-compartment state written from RAW PHYSICAL co-occurrence (never a high-level fact), driven by a free-energy flux or an Arrhenius rate, decaying at a derived rate, and read into behaviour or selection. The new substrate needed is small and shared: a general decay-rate kernel (forgetting and turnover), the Beer-Lambert ionizing-deposition law (radiotrophy, sharing the atmospheric-EUV physics), an information-storage tissue axis (memory), and a pre-biotic reaction-compartment layer (abiogenesis, reusing the Stock, conservation, and `laws::reaction` machinery). The authored residue stays short and per-organism or per-world measured, with no global authored outcome.

## The bootstrap of behaviour (the crux the owner named)

The recurring wall is that a founder cannot be FORCED to eat, drink, or reproduce without authored steering, so a blank controller goes extinct. Nature never had this problem, because those were never controller decisions. Eating is metabolism (automatic uptake when co-located with edible matter, already shipped). Reproduction is automatic division on a reserve threshold (already shipped). Seeking, the hard one, is a biased random walk, not a decision: run-and-tumble, where the being re-orients LESS when the thing it senses is IMPROVING over time, biasing the walk up the gradient. The cleanest derive-first form of that feedback couples motility to the being's OWN felt reserve delta (rising means persist, falling means re-orient), which is the felt-salience substrate already built. It is always on, authors no direction (the sign comes from the being's own physical reserve rising or falling), and is alien-clean.

So the fix that ends the extinction wall is to separate two layers: the AUTOMATIC PHYSICAL layer (uptake equals eating, division equals reproduction, and a derived reserve-coupled run-and-tumble equals seeking) is always on and keys only on the being's own physical state, so the founder is never blank and survives on physics alone; and the EVOLVABLE CONTROLLER sits above it and TUNES it (directed locomotion, sharp discrimination, choice). The controller is the optimisation, not the survival floor, so a founder-zero controller is fine because the automatic layer keeps the lineage alive long enough for selection to tune the controller. The one missing buildable piece is the derived run-and-tumble taxis kernel (re-orientation rate modulated by the sign of the being's own felt reserve delta, always on, no authored direction, no controller weight), which is the survival floor beneath everything the controller evolves on top, and it lands cleanly on the abiogenesis frontier where the very first behaviour is metabolism plus a gradient-coupled motility.

## Priority

The redox spine is the engine and it is done, so three of the five frontiers are close to wiring rather than deriving. The two highest-leverage new pieces are the general decay-rate kernel (forgetting, memory fade, and biological turnover at once) and the Beer-Lambert ionizing law (radiotrophy, reusing the atmospheric physics). The derived reserve-coupled run-and-tumble taxis floor is the general answer to every founder-extinction wall and belongs near the top. The deepest payoff is abiogenesis, because the pre-biotic reaction-compartment layer makes the world self-starting and reuses the most already-built machinery.
