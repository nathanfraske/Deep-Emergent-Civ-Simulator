# Predation completion: closing the hunt-to-corpse-to-intake energy loop (design-first)

This is the design opener for the arc the gate ruled next after both predator-prey poles were demonstrated under selection (#144 approach, #146 avoid). It closes the real-ecology loop: a kill deposits a corpse, and a being's intake from that corpse returns the energy the hunt spent, so predation pays for itself. The intake-from-corpse disposition must EMERGE from selection, never an authored "predators eat corpses" rule. Design-first, grounded against source, posted for the gate's ruling before any code. No mechanism here.

## What is already built (do not rebuild)

The pieces the loop composes are all on main. The MORTALITY: an authored predator lands a lethal whole-body wound on a co-located catalog prey, which dies through the one unified INTEGRITY cull (#117/#120). The CORPSE: the opt-in corpse-matter death hook (`Runner::set_corpse_matter`, runner.rs:4136) deposits the dead being's own body composition to the located tissue field where it fell (`emb.tissue.deposit`), derived per-individual by a development-weighted fold over its body plan (`whole_body_composition_vector`), never a species-to-substance map. The INTAKE: the whole-body bite inside `geophage` (runner.rs:3053, the bite at 3206) reads that tissue field TROPHIC-AGNOSTICALLY through `physiology::physical_intake`, keyed on the eater's OWN assimilation and backing components, so a corpse is food iff the eater assimilates its composition (carnivore, herbivore, omnivore all emerge, never a tag). The DRAW: the identity-blind matter percept union (#144) surfaces a corpse's tissue content on the resource-density channel (`cell_content` over `ResourceField` plus `TissueField`), so a being is perceptually drawn to a carcass exactly as to a plant. The SELECTION loop (#144) and both behaviour poles (#144 approach, #146 avoid) are proven.

## What remains: close the energy loop, and let the intake pay-off emerge

Every piece exists in isolation. What is not yet demonstrated is that they CLOSE into a paying loop under selection: that a lineage whose weights carry it to a corpse AND drive it to intake the corpse tissue gains the downed prey's energy and out-survives a lineage that does not, so the intake-from-corpse disposition emerges rather than being authored. This is the mirror of the #144 matter-channel proof (a resource-density weight driven to the adaptive pole under a food gradient), with a CORPSE as the energy source: a kill leaves a finite, perceivable, intakeable body, and a lineage that exploits it is selected.

The load-bearing design line: nothing about "eat the corpse" is authored. The corpse is a matter deposit like any other; the being intakes it through the SAME trophic-agnostic bite it uses on any tissue, keyed on its own metabolism; the intake weight is founder-zero and freely-signed; and the pay-off is the corpse's own physics (the downed prey's assimilable matter and energy), never a stamped hunt reward. A lineage that intakes what it reaches out-survives, so the disposition is selected, exactly as the approach and avoid poles were.

## The emergence claim (for the gate's frame-blind), de-narrativized

"A creature lineage seeded with a founder-zero, freely-signed intake disposition, selected on its own homeostatic survival, will have that disposition driven to the intake pole by selection when a perceivable, intakeable corpse is a real energy source: a lineage whose weights carry it to a corpse and drive it to intake the corpse tissue gains the downed prey's assimilable matter and energy through the existing trophic-agnostic bite (keyed on its own metabolism) and out-survives one that does not; in a matched control with no corpse energy source the disposition stays at its zero seed. The corpse is the existing matter deposit; the intake is the existing bite; the pay-off is the corpse's own physics; nothing about eating a corpse is authored at any level."

## Scope: the SINGLE-EATER cycle now, the CONTESTED corpse flagged

The single-eater cycle is buildable and provable now: one being reaches one corpse and intakes it, the energy returns, the disposition emerges under selection with a matched held control. The CONTESTED-corpse case (two or more beings over one body, so the corpse mass must split between them) couples to the owner-locked snapshot-apply proportional-split contention resolver, which is UNBUILT. So this arc scopes the single-eater cycle now and FLAGS the carcass fight as the sibling that lands when the contention resolver does, never faking a split with a first-come-serve or an authored share.

The intake disposition, grounded (the gate's note 2: state what emerges here versus what #144 already proved). Build notes (for any similar arena). The corpse bite credits the eater fine, but a bred OFFSPRING gets its reserve capacity from its body's ORGANS (`Homeostasis::new` sizes each reserve from the organ composition), so an organ-less body gives an offspring a zero-capacity energy reserve that can never refill or become eligible, and the lineage cannot compound. The fix: give the arena body a fat-body organ (kind 0, the `bio.energy_density`-backing tissue), so an offspring holds the same reserve its founder does and the intake-positive lineage compounds. This is the lever that turned a modest founder-only shift into the strong compounding signal, and it is why the mirror of #144 (which drew its signal from lethal founder differential survival) reads a reproductive-selection signal here.

Honest scope and limits. Reproductive (not lethal) selection, so both arms persist and the control is a live held population at its zero seed. The single-eater cycle is the honest scope. The corpse is a replenished standing deposit (a held environment), not yet a kill that deposits once and is consumed to nothing (that couples to biosphere balance, GATED). The CONTESTED corpse (two or more eaters over one body) still waits on the unbuilt owner-locked snapshot-apply proportional-split contention resolver, and the full DOWN-LIVE-PREY-AND-EAT loop still waits on the creature-strike capability and the 2b per-feature discrimination, both flagged as before.
