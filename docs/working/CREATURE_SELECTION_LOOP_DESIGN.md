# Creature selection loop: making the predator-prey reaction emerge (design-first)

This is the design opener for the arc the owner ruled next: complete the creature reproduction-and-behaviour-selection loop kicked off in #126, so the predator-prey reaction (fleeing and hunting) EMERGES observably in a shipped `full --creatures` run and re-pins it. Design-first, grounded against source, posted for the gate's ruling before any code. No mechanism here.

## What is already built (do not rebuild)

The perception SUBSTRATE (reach, sensorium-gating, receiver-side valence) is merged (#109). The creatures-react CAPACITY is built (#118). The 2a creature reproduction-and-selection substrate is merged (#126): `Walker.lineage`, the structural pairing predicate `carries_lineage()`, `Controller::perturbed`/`midparent`, the mint-time bounded zero-mean perturbation, and the `reproduce_creatures`/`breed_offspring` beat (co-located reserve-eligible lineage-carrying creatures mint a midparent-blended offspring, eligibility on the being's own energy-backing axis, all seed-keyed). The 2b percept kind-feature floor is merged (#126): a creature can perceive a strength-independent optical feature of another being and key a founder-zero freely-signed per-bucket weight on it. This arc rebuilds none of that; it makes the loop LIVE and closes the two gradients selection climbs.

## What remains: the loop must run and both gradients must flow

The mortality pressure is live (#117/#120): an authored predator lands a lethal whole-body wound on a co-located catalog prey, which dies through the one unified INTEGRITY cull. The prey's flee sign and any hunt sign stay founder-zero, so the pressure exists but nothing selects on it yet. Two things make the reaction emerge, and the second is the load-bearing design point.

**The fleeing gradient EXISTS (confirm it flows).** A prey that avoids the predator survives the strike-to-death loop (#117), and a creature is selected on its own homeostatic survival, so surviving predation IS the selection pressure on the flee weight. What this arc must confirm at source is that a prey lineage which reproduces in-run carries the being-directed weight into its offspring and that surviving-longer converts into more offspring (the reproduction beat firing over generations), so the flee sign has a real cross-generational gradient to climb.

**The hunting payoff needs wiring (the load-bearing point).** For the hunt weight to be favoured, the predator must GAIN reserves from the kill: it must take in the killed being's own matter and energy through the existing `physiology::physical_intake` path. Grounded at source, this payoff is NOT yet wired: the opt-in corpse-matter death hook (`set_corpse_matter`) deposits the dead being's matter to the cell's tissue/soil field for the decomposer and matter cycle, but the killer does not gain reserves from its kill, so hunting has no selection gradient and will not emerge however long the run goes. Wiring the predator-eats-its-kill energy payoff is therefore part of this arc. Derive-first, per the owner's ruling: the payoff is the kill's own physics (the prey's matter and energy assimilated through the same intake path a grazer uses on any matter, keyed on the killer's own backing component), never a stamped hunt reward.

## The framing statement, amended (for the gate's frame-blind)

The gate approved the original framing to build with three amendments (intake-from-an-other with a contestable corpse, the fitness read over the being's own axes, the discrimination resolved by channel with an honest emergence claim). The amended, de-narrivatized statement, phrased as a claim to attack:

"A creature lineage that reproduces and is selected on its OWN homeostatic survival over its OWN declared reserve axes will, over generations, have the sign and strength of its heritable per-channel weights set by selection alone. The APPROACH payoff is taking in another being's matter or energy through the existing trophic-agnostic intake path (which consumes physical content from a corpse, a live other, or a tile alike, keyed on the eater's own metabolism): a lineage whose weights carry it to a perceivable patch of an other's matter it can assimilate gains reserves and reproduces more. A being-on-being strike produces a PERSISTENT, FINITE, CONTESTABLE corpse (a matter deposit), and the striker takes only what its own bounded ingestion capacity allows against the corpse mass, the remainder persisting as a perceivable resource patch for other beings to approach and contest before it rots to soil through the existing cycle, so scavenging and carcass competition arise from the corpse-as-contested-matter plus perception plus bounded intake, nothing authored. The AVOIDANCE payoff is surviving the strike-to-death loop: a lineage whose being-signal weight carries it away from a live emitter correlated with harm survives and reproduces more. The two dispositions resolve BY CHANNEL: the resource-density channel (the foraging percept) draws a being to a corpse or matter patch, and the being-signal channel carries it toward or away from a live emitter, so no single weight two-way-sorts. Approaching a live other to kill it (active predation on live prey) requires the being channel to key on a distinguishing physical feature of the other (the per-feature discrimination), absent which the being-signal weight can only sort by signal magnitude (approach a weaker signal, avoid a stronger one), which is the honest scope of what emerges. A lineage for which no trophic approach and no avoidance pays evolves neither."

## Build log

**Slice 1 BUILT (the eligibility generalization, amendment 2), byte-neutral, tested, section-9 self-audit clean.** `reproduce_creatures` (runner.rs) no longer keys reproduction eligibility on the single ENERGY_DENSITY-backed axis with an early return when that axis is absent. It now reads a surplus across the being's OWN declared reserve axes: a new pure helper `reproduction_eligible(homeostasis, reserve_axes, threshold)` requires occupancy at or above the reserved threshold on EVERY depletable backed reserve the being declares (`backing_component.is_some()`, so the derived regulated axes integrity and temperature are excluded because their occupancy is a band, not a surplus). `Homeostasis::level` is occupancy (amount over capacity in [0, ONE], verified at stocks.rs:89), so one reserved threshold is comparable across axes whatever their capacity. The `.all` is vacuously true for a being with no depletable reserve (the honest admit-the-alien read: no surplus to gate on; the structural predicates still bound it). This removes a hard Terran-bias defect: the old code locked out any lineage with no energy-density reserve (a redox, mana-field, or silicon metabolism could never reproduce). A new unit test proves the ALL semantics (a being in deficit on one reserve is not eligible), the derived-band exclusion (a cold being with reserves in surplus is eligible), and the admit-the-alien vacuous case. BYTE-NEUTRAL empirically: the four canonical pins hold bit-exact (`4bbf6b59`/`1db633b3`/`c9d5cc17`/`ad69f2bf`) by construction (no `creature_selection`, early return) and `full --creatures` holds `9701449f`, which also confirms the honest limit at source: no lineage-carrying creature reaches an eligibility beat in the shipped run (creatures starve first), so the eligible list is empty under both the old and the new read and the beat mints nothing. Full sim lib suite green (896 passed), fmt and clippy clean.

**Slice 2 grounding (the load-bearing bridge): the corpse is NOT perceivable on the resource-density channel, and bridging it is a fork for the gate.** Grounded at source, the framing's load-bearing assumption ("the resource-density channel, the foraging percept, draws a being to a corpse or matter patch") does NOT hold as built, which amendment 1 flagged to confirm. The foraging arc's resource-density percept reads `ResourceField::cell_content` (locomotion.rs:460; the runner perceive pass at runner.rs:6315 reads `emb.resources.cell_content`), and the forage INGEST enactment also reads `ResourceField` composition (`supply`/`nutrient`, locomotion.rs), which producers populate. A being-on-being death deposits the corpse to a DIFFERENT field, `TissueField` (`emb.tissue`, the corpse-matter hook at runner.rs:7460), and the corpse is eaten only through the whole-body bite inside `geophage` (runner.rs:3130), which reads `emb.tissue` trophic-agnostically (a body is food iff the eater assimilates its composition, keyed on the eater's own physiology, carnivore/herbivore/omnivore emerging, never a tag). So intake-from-an-other's INTAKE half is already wired (the whole-body bite), but a foraging being drawn by the resource-density percept neither SENSES the corpse (the percept reads `ResourceField`, the corpse is in `TissueField`) nor EATS it through the forage INGEST (which reads `ResourceField`). The bridge is a real architectural fork with steering weight, and it is the gate's to rule before I build:
> (a) Surface the tissue content into `cell_content` so the SAME resource-density channel senses a corpse, AND union tissue into the forage INGEST so the same action eats it. One channel carries producers and corpses alike (the identity-blind, valence-blind aggregate the foraging arc already commits to), at the cost of touching the INGEST intake path.
> (b) Add a distinct tissue-density percept channel (a second `PerceivableFeatureRegistry` channel keyed on `tissue.volume_at`/`axis_supply`), and rely on the EXISTING `geophage`/whole-body-bite to eat the corpse. Producers and bodies resolve by SEPARATE channels, which matches the "resolve by channel" reading of amendment 3, at the cost of a second channel and a percept-to-geophage wire.
> (c) Make the corpse deposit ALSO write `ResourceField` at death, so a corpse becomes a resource patch the existing percept and INGEST both handle, at the cost of blurring the producer/consumer matter distinction and risking a double-count against the tissue whole-body-bite path.

My read (surfaced, not chosen): (b) keys the trophic distinction on the channel the being's weights sort by, which is the cleanest fit to the by-channel emergence claim and leaves the producer field and the corpse/tissue field each read by its own path with no double-count; (a) is the most economical if the arc wants one resource channel to carry all edible matter. Either way this is a fork the gate has insisted on ruling. I hold slice 2 for the gate's ruling on the bridge shape.
