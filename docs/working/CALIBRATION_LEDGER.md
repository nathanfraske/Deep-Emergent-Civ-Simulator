# The calibration ledger

Owner-instituted 2026-07-17. THE SINGLE REGISTRY OF EVERY FIELD-INFERRED PARAMETER in the engine: the one place a derivation input was constrained by observation rather than by lab or theory alone. Its purpose is to make one failure mode LOUD, the one that kills projects like this slowly: CREEPING CALIBRATION, one licensed inference per year until the engine is secretly a fit of Earth wearing derivation's clothes.

An empty ledger is the correct state and the best state. Every entry is a debt against the engine's claim to derive rather than fit, and the length of this list is itself a REPORTED HONESTY METRIC.

## The two standing invariants

1. **THE VALIDATION SET STRICTLY EXCEEDS THE CALIBRATION SET, AT ALL TIMES.** More independent checks than inferred parameters, always. A field-inferred parameter is a row SPENT; it can never again be claimed as validation. So every entry below must be outnumbered by out-of-sample rows the engine's predictive claims truly rest on.
2. **THE LEDGER'S LENGTH IS A HONESTY METRIC, REPORTED.** If this list ever needs a second page, the engine has quietly stopped deriving and started fitting, and this file is what makes the quiet loud. A growing ledger is a growing confession.

## The entry contract (same ceremony as a re-pin window, and the owner signs)

Every entry carries, and ships with nothing missing:

- THE PARAMETER inferred, and its PRIOR BAND from lab or theory (the band the fit selected strictly within; a fit that demanded a value outside its prior band is NOT a calibration, it is the chain surfacing a defect, and it does not belong here at all).
- THE ROW SPENT: the exact hindcast row consumed, now permanently out of the validation set.
- THE INFERRED VALUE with its posterior band, stored as a SEPARATE, TAGGED, FIELD-CALIBRATED ENTRY. The lab rows are never edited (modality discipline): the two knowledge sources stay distinguishable forever.
- THE PROVENANCE MARK: the DAG node id so any consumer of any world's downstream quantity can see this Earth-inferred parameter in its ancestry, with the pure-lab-band derivation left COMPUTABLE IN PARALLEL for anyone who wants the uninfected answer.
- THE THREE-IN-LINE RECORD: what the defect hunt found (or did not), what the band narrowing achieved (or did not), and why the band remained irreducibly wide, because inversion is third in line and earns its turn only after the two cheaper options fail.
- THE OWNER'S SIGNATURE and the date.

## The entries

NONE. The engine derives; it has inferred nothing from observation. This is the state to preserve.
