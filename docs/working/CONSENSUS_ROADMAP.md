# Consensus Roadmap: the ground-truth order of things (for sign-off)

This reconciles the three ordering artifacts in the repo against what is built in code, into one authoritative sequence, each item cited to where its gate lives. It is a synthesis for the owner to sign off on, not a maintained document; nothing here is committed until you approve it.

The three sources it reconciles:

- **`ROADMAP.md`**, the near-term critical path to a living, talking world (milestones M0 through M4).
- **`docs/design.md` Part 60**, the full implementation staging (Stage 0 through Stage 14).
- **`docs/audit.md` Section 3**, the research backlog: every open item tagged `R-XXX` with its site (`Part N`), its prerequisites, and its couplings, plus the resolved-item record (Part 62).

The reconciliation was checked against a direct read of every crate's modules, tests, and the calibration manifest, so "done" below means built and proven in code, not merely planned.

## The headline reconciliation (read this first)

The build did not follow Part 60's stage numbers in order. It followed the research-gate dependency order instead, front-loading the determinism bedrock, the reasoning-and-being core, the physics substrate, and the embodiment stack, because those are the pieces the emergent design cannot be proven without. The consequence is that the actual frontier is best read as a research-gated dependency graph plus the `ROADMAP.md` M-slice, and Part 60's stage numbers are a topic index rather than a build order.

Three divergences the ground-truth read exposes, stated plainly so the sign-off is honest:

- **The code is ahead of `ROADMAP.md`'s prose.** `ROADMAP.md` still says the deep being model is "absent" and the map is "entirely unbuilt." Neither holds: the being model (genome, intrinsic beliefs, axioms, value profile) is wired end to end through `seed_dawn_populations`, and the map generates, classifies biomes, builds a level-of-detail quadtree, and renders in a windowed viewer, proven bit-identical by `crates/world/tests/zoom.rs`. `ROADMAP.md`'s "Where we are" section is stale and should be refreshed.
- **The code is ahead of the audit's bookkeeping in the determinism cluster.** The audit still lists `R-CMD-ORDER` among the open determinism items, but the mechanism is built (`crates/core/src/command.rs`: `CommandBuffer`, `EventQueue`, the total ordering key). This is the same "mechanism built, item open until consolidated" state that `R-LANG-TYPOLOGY` is in. The audit's resolved count (28) undercounts what code proves; a consolidation pass is owed for `R-CMD-ORDER`, `R-LANG-TYPOLOGY`, and the canonical-walk and reduce helpers.
- **The physics substrate and embodiment stack are built ahead of their nominal Part 60 stage.** Five physics floors (biology, mechanical, fluids, chem-optics, electricity-and-magnetism) and the full homeostasis-to-controller-to-evolve embodiment chain exist and are tested, though Part 60 places the physics substrate as a Stage 14 concern. They are substrate, proven in isolation, not yet integrated into a running world tick.

So the single biggest truth for sequencing: the engine is a deterministic, serial, CPU, promoted-tier core with a viewable generated map, a deep cognition-and-language core, and a broad shelf of tested-but-not-yet-integrated substrate modules. What stands between it and a running world is not more substrate. It is **owner calibration**, **integration into the canonical runner**, and then the **parallel, persistence, and made-world** tracks.

## Tier B: the near-term frontier (what to do next), with gates

This is where the consensus order matters most, because the substrate shelf is deep but the running world is thin. Recommended order:

1. **Owner calibration of the reserved values.** The manifest carries 113 reserved entries; 31 are set and 82 remain fail-loud sentinels (`calibration/reserved.toml`, RUNBOOK section 4). Under the Calibrated profile the engine refuses to start on any unset value, so the real (non-dev-fixture) world cannot run until these are set. This is the single largest unblock and gates everything that runs a world rather than a test. Gate: each value is reserved-with-basis at its mechanism site; you set it, the manifest graduates it to `set`.
2. **Wire the built core into the canonical runner.** `crates/sim/src/runner.rs` carries the physics field layer (Part 5.4, 5.5) with zero authored numbers. The being-and-cognition core is now composed onto that spine. Gate: integration, not research.
   - DONE (composition increment): the runner owns an optional cognition `World` and runs `World::tick` as a fixed sub-phase after the field phases, in a pinned within-tick order (field first). `Runner::with_world` (`crates/sim/src/runner.rs`), composite `state_hash` folds the field-side hash then the world's, and the determinism triad plus a clock-lockstep invariant is proven in `crates/sim/tests/determinism_harness.rs` (`composed_runner_tick_*`, worker widths [1,2,3,8]). No new authored number, RNG draw, or `Phase`. Honest limit: no field-to-cognition data flow yet, and the world hash does not yet fold the being lifecycle (genomes, ages, affect) or the dialogue log.
   - STEERING BOUNDARY (Principle 9, guiding-star audit): the canonical-emergent runner's behaviour source is the evolved controller (Part 8.4), never an authored policy. `World::decide`'s authored drive-and-action repertoire (the sentient deliberative tier, Part 8.1) is steering at the level of behaviour, so `Runner::with_world` fail-loud rejects a world that carries one (`World::has_behaviour`), quarantining the authored path off the canonical spine (proven by `the_canonical_runner_refuses_an_authored_behaviour_repertoire`). The composed cognition phases the runner does drive (belief, dialogue, gossip, language) are emergent and data-driven.
   - DONE (field-to-behaviour coupling, evolved-controller substrate, the guiding-star path chosen over the original sketch below): a runner built with `Runner::with_embodiment` owns an `Embodiment`, a located population of evolved-controller beings. Each tick, after the field step and the body-thermal exchange, a pure comfort-band map (`comfort_fraction`) turns each being's field-driven core temperature into a `TEMPERATURE` homeostatic reserve, per being from its reserved comfort band (Part 20), the being's evolved controller reads the reserve and moves-or-rests, and the beings' new coordinates re-sync `LocationIndex` for the next tick's exchange (`crates/sim/src/runner.rs`, `crates/sim/src/homeostasis.rs::dev_thermal`). The first increment is KINESIS: no directional thermal percept, so an uncomfortable being explores (`Phase::EXPLORE`, no new `Phase`) and directed thermotaxis emerges under survival rather than being authored. The composite `state_hash` folds each being's position, reserves, and controller state after the world fold; proven in `crates/sim/tests/thermal_coupling.rs` (the comfort map is even, a comfortable being rests and an uncomfortable one moves, the controller authors no heading, replay bit-identity and seed divergence, death a full half-band past the set point, and the R-EVOLVE-STEER anti-steering check that the warmth-west and warmth-east advantages match with bit-identical idle baselines). The behaviour source is the evolved controller only; no authored repertoire rides this path (Principle 9).
   - DONE (in-situ thermal selection, the guiding-star retirement of the hand-built fixture): `evolve_thermal` scores a controller by running it through the field-to-behaviour coupling (`Runner::with_embodiment`) in a frozen warm-border, cold-centre field and counting how long it keeps its temperature reserve off the floor. From random controllers, thermal homeostatic-survival selection produces a kinesis (move while cold, rest while warm) that keeps its body in the viable band, retiring the hand-built thermotaxis fixture the coupling increment used: selection produces it. `evolve` was generalized to `evolve_with(layout, params, seed, scorer)` so the scoring environment is data, not hardcoded (Principle 11), with `evolve` the water scorer and `evolve_thermal` the thermal one. Proven in `crates/sim/src/evolve.rs` tests (`thermotaxis_evolves_under_thermal_survival_selection` mean survival 129 to the 200 cap; the scoring field is invariant under a 90-degree rotation, the R-EVOLVE-STEER anti-steering check; a mover outlives an idle being that dies at the cold centre; the scorer is deterministic). No authored heading and no authored objective: survival is what is counted.
   - DONE (directional thermal percept, raw physics, sensed taxis): `Field::gradient_at` is a pure integer central-difference stencil, the raw temperature gradient (the GPU-portable counterpart of `Field::step` and `crates/gpu`'s field kernel); `Runner::step_embodiment` reads it per being and feeds it as the `TEMPERATURE` axis's directional percept through `locomotion::step_with_field_dirs`, an axis-generic per-being field-percept channel (a moisture or wind field plugs in the same way), merged alongside the known-source percept with no controller-layout change. The percept is raw physics (what a thermoreceptor senses), not comfort: no physiology is folded in, and the being's comfort reserve stays a separate input, so the evolved controller combines "am I uncomfortable" with "which way is warmer" and selection wires it. Proven in `crates/sim/src/evolve.rs` and `crates/sim/tests/thermal_coupling.rs`: a smooth rotation-invariant thermal bowl with a balanced set of four off-centre starts (`thermal_sensed_survival`, `evolve_thermal_sensed`); sensed taxis reaches the warm ring from every start (survives to the cap) while undirected kinesis random-walks and often dies (200 vs about 66 to 153); a fixed heading has a directional blind spot the percept-follower does not; the bowl is 90-degree rotation invariant (R-EVOLVE-STEER); directed thermotaxis evolves to near the cap; and `Field::gradient_at` points toward warmer and is flat on a uniform field. Honest limit: raw gradient plus the even comfort reserve lets a linear controller learn warmth-seeking in a cold world, but fleeing lethal heat needs a recurrent controller reading a signed thermoreceptive percept (a warm-versus-cold receptor, a new input channel) so selection can evolve the hot-versus-cold gating. GATE (owner-confirmed): this is deferred and rides in with the medium substrate, since lethal-hot environments (lava, desert) are what the medium work introduces; bidirectional thermotaxis lands under `R-MEDIUM`, not before there is heat to flee.
   - DONE (FieldCalib manifest loader): `FieldCalib::from_manifest` reads `field.diffusion`, `field.relaxation`, and `field.body_exchange` from the calibration manifest, fail-loud if any is still reserved (Principle 11), so a canonical run obtains its field calibrations from the owner's manifest rather than a fabricated default (the three entries ship reserved-with-basis in `calibration/reserved.toml`). Tests in `crates/sim/src/runner.rs`: it reads a set manifest exactly and refuses a reserved one.
   - REMAINING sub-increments, ordered: (a) map-backed terrain (real biomes and elevation) replacing the bounded open plane; (b) fold the being-lifecycle path (`seed_dawn_populations`, enculturate, birth, age, mortality) into the composed tick and the hash; (c) the cognition-`World` coupling (temperature as a percept to the deliberative tier, and a chosen action moving a `Coord3` against a unified `PlaceId`/`LocationIndex`) is DEFERRED behind rebuilding that deliberative tier (Part 8.1) on the evolved substrate, because its current `World::decide` path is the quarantined authored repertoire (Principle 9), not a clean coupling target.
   - OWNER-DIRECTED, the emergent-physiology direction (signed off; the organ abstraction was red-teamed and reframed, see below). First, DERIVE THE HOMEOSTATIC RESERVES FROM ANATOMY. Today reserve capacity scales with `body_mass` and only integrity derives from the per-part body. Mass-scaling is wrong for a huge, mostly-armored creature with few organs (it would be handed reserves it has no internal systems to justify): a being carries a reserve because it bears the organ that maintains it, and reserve size follows the organs, not total mass, so "no organs, no reserves" is expressible and armor and integrity decouple from metabolic reserves. RED-TEAM OUTCOME (owner-steered): the organ's function is NOT an authored tag. The first-cut design gave each organ kind an authored `OrganCategory` (fat-body to Metabolic, gills to Respiratory); red-teamed against Principle 8 that tag is a template, a closed function-per-kind authored one level down. The reframe: an organ is a tissue-composition structure (the exact pattern the body's fluids already use, a composition over the biology floor), and which reserve it backs is DERIVED from its composition (an energy-dense tissue is an energy store, a water-rich one a water store) against the physics-floor material axes (`bio.energy_density`, `bio.water_fraction`, `therm.specific_heat`). We author the floor physics and the tissue-composition vocabulary; the organ's role emerges. The first-cut authored-kind build is set aside for this composition-derived rework. Second, THE MEDIUM SUBSTRATE, generalized so aquatic life is one instance: a medium is a `Substance` in the physics substrate (`crates/physics`, content-addressed, not a label), defined by its physics-axis values (`mech.density`, `fluid.dynamic_viscosity`, `therm.temperature`, and the rest); a per-cell medium field of the GPU-portable stencil shape. The FLOOR TO AUTHOR here is a gas-exchange and respirable-medium physics law the floors do not yet carry (a medium's respirable content, dissolved-gas concentration or partial pressure, and Fick's-law diffusion across a membrane by area, permeability, and gradient), the one new authored piece, tracked as `R-MEDIUM`. Medium affinity is then EMERGENT: a high-surface-area membrane organ in contact with water respires water (a gill), one in contact with air respires air (a lung), from the organ's composition and geometry against that law plus selection, never a hardcoded "aquatic" flag, so a lava creature, a magical-fluid creature, a rooted autotroph drawing from its medium, and a sentient aquatic race all emerge. Full aquatic physiology is the chosen depth: medium, respiration, buoyancy (from body density), in-medium thermal exchange, and amphibious both-medium bands. The bidirectional-thermotaxis gate above rides in here. Both are substantial; the anatomy-derived reserves come first (composition-derived), then the medium substrate on top.
3. **Close the M1 tail.** Add the hydrology pass `ROADMAP.md` M1 lists as light-or-stubbed, and move biome membership from the in-code `BiomeSet::dev_default` fixture to a data loader (Principle 11). ## Cross-cutting gates (true at every tier)

- **Owner calibration.** Eighty-two reserved values remain fail-loud. Nothing that runs a real world, as opposed to a dev-fixture test, is unblocked until the values on its path are set. This is the most pervasive gate in the project.
- **Stay serial.** Every determinism item in Tier B is dormant only while the tick is a single stream. The first parallelised phase makes the whole cluster a live blocker at once.
- **Tier consistency.** Any kernel that runs on both a promoted individual and a pool representative (the axiom kernel, the controller expression) must run identically on both, the invariant `R-TIER-CONSIST` guarantees and `R-PROJ-REGISTER` extends.
