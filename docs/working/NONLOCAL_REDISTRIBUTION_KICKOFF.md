## Grounding: what exists, and the gap it leaves

The transport machinery on current main is LOCAL. The salt and water stencils (`environ.rs`, `step_salinity` and `step_hydrology`) advect a cell's mass to its single downhill neighbour, double-buffered so the pass is order-independent and conservative except at map-edge outflow. My #162 primitives are local too: `priority_flood` routes drainage to a downstream receiver over a sill, `fixed_cap_solve` relaxes a field against its own neighbours, `label_components` keys connected regions. The `EarthworkField` delta ledger and `recouple_terrain` (`material.rs`, `environ.rs`, off the run path today) record an elevation delta and re-route the downhill graph over the changed surface. All of these move mass to a NEIGHBOUR or relax against neighbours.

None expresses a source scattering mass NON-locally to many DISTANT destinations at once: an impact ejecta blanket deposits around a crater out to several radii; a granular runout carries mass down a slope path for a long distance past the source cell. That is the gap: a conservative, deterministic operator that takes one source cell's mass and a distribution over a NON-local destination set, and applies it so mass closes exactly and the result is worker-invariant. The proposal already anticipates a transported-mass field (`GEODYNAMICS_ARC_PROPOSAL.md`, shared-substrate item 3, a sediment field through the conservative double-buffered stencil) and flags the within-tick canonical sequence (item 1) and the erosion/hydrology transport determinism as asserted rather than demonstrated (honest limits), so a proven non-local conservative operator is a real substrate need, not a speculative one.

## The mechanism, in outline

A deterministic, mass-conserving non-local redistribution. For each source cell holding an integer mass `M`, a distribution kernel returns a set of `(destination, weight)` pairs over a non-local neighbourhood (the kernel reads the source's physical state and the terrain to place the destinations and shape the weights). The operator apportions `M` across the destinations in exact integers so the distributed masses sum to `M` (no mass fabricated, none lost to rounding), and applies the result to a double-buffered destination field so a source and its destinations never read a half-updated grid. Determinism is the load-bearing property: the apportionment, the destination ordering, and the remainder placement are all fixed by a canonical order (lowest destination index), so a parallel and a serial application are bit-identical (P3, P10). Fail-loud on any overflow of the integer accumulators, never a silent saturate on the conservation path.

## The two seams, surfaced for the gate's ruling

**Seam one, the closed-kernel-set and the derive-versus-author line.** The distribution kernels (a ballistic ejecta-blanket kernel, a granular-runout kernel) must be a DATA-DEFINED registry, the mechanism fixed Rust and the kernel membership extensible, sibling to the value, semantic, institution-function, provenance, and derivation substrates, never a closed enum of driver KINDS authored one level down. Sharper, and the reason a blind framing panel is warranted here: a kernel must read the GENERAL causal primitive and let the destination distribution EMERGE, never read a high-level driver KIND to select a runout pattern. The ballistic kernel reads a launch energy and angle against the world's own gravity and derives the range and the ejecta-blanket falloff from the ballistic trajectory; the granular kernel reads the slope, the moving mass, and the material's angle of repose (from `mech.static_friction`) and derives the runout distance from the momentum-versus-friction competition. The KIND (an impact versus a lahar) is WHICH physical kernel with WHICH per-material parameters, not a label a mechanism reads to shortcut where the mass lands. My read, matching the #169 `InputSource` ruling, is that the kernel set is a fixed physics vocabulary (a code extension when a new physical transport law appears) with data membership (the per-world material and event parameters), not a world-content closed list. Because a wrong call here authors geomorphology, I recommend a section-10 blind framing panel on the de-narrivatized kernel-interface statement before I build the kernels, per the gate's own note that a panel is warranted given the stakes. The gate's ruling sets whether the panel runs and what the kernel interface may read.

**Seam two, non-local determinism.** A scatter (a source pushing to many destinations) has a write-conflict hazard the local downhill stencil does not: several sources can write one destination in the same pass, so the accumulation order would leak into the result unless it is pinned. The double-buffered destination field plus a canonical accumulation resolves it: every destination gathers its incoming mass as an exact-integer sum whose order is fixed by the source index, so the total is order-invariant and worker-invariant. The exact-integer conservation needs one determinism decision, not a physical value: when `M` does not divide evenly across the weighted destinations, the integer remainder is placed by a fixed rule (lowest destination index first) so the sum still closes to `M` deterministically. This interacts with the within-tick canonical sequence (proposal substrate item 1): the redistribution reads the committed pre-step surface and writes the post-step buffer, so it slots into the uplift-then-weather-then-transport-then-deposit order without a read-after-write hazard. I surface the remainder rule and the gather-versus-double-buffer choice for the gate; both are determinism decisions I can settle with a section-9 self-audit, but they are where a non-deterministic bug would hide, so I name them.

## Seam-1 framing panel result (gate-authorized, run before the kernels)

The gate lifted the cost directive for a blind framing panel on the kernel interface and I ran it (six panelists, three agent types, three models, each isolated, the de-narrativized kernel-interface statement with no leaked conclusion). The verdict was UNANIMOUS (five significant-flaw-fixable, one reframe-needed), and I verified its decisive claims against source. It caught a real seam in my own opener framing: a data-registered set where each transport process is its own KERNEL FUNCTION, a new process a new function, and an event that NAMES which kernel to apply, is membership-as-code not membership-as-data, authors a closed taxonomy of transport processes and the outcome shape per process, and reads a high-level process category to select a behaviour (the template case in physics clothing), failing admit-the-alien (a mana-gradient or thermal-spallation transport would need new code, not a data row). The panel BLESSED the redistribution OPERATOR unanimously (the slice-1 mechanism, unchanged).

The corrected framing (my synthesis of the convergent panel, verified at source, PENDING the gate's ruling): keep the operator; replace the per-process kernel registry with ONE general transfer law, a single fixed integrator computing the destinations and weights PURELY from floor quantities and the situation (gravity, momentum and kinetic energy, a resistance or friction term derived from `mech.static_friction`, grain size and density, terrain), parameterized by per-world DATA; the floor carries a small data-registered set of general force and resistance PRIMITIVES (a gravity-drive term, a ballistic-carry term, a resistance-dissipation term, plus an OPEN floor-axis slot for a world-specific force such as a mana-pressure gradient), and the one integrator sums whichever terms are non-zero. Named processes (ejecta, runout, fluvial) EMERGE as regimes of that one law across parameter space; an event supplies physical initial conditions as data and never names a kernel; which regime fires is DERIVED from a dimensionless ratio of floor quantities (kinetic energy against dissipation). Any breakpoint is reserved-with-basis in the floor's friction and energy axes; any new force with no existing floor term is a new floor axis (Principle 9), never a registry parameter keyed to a process name. The emergence-preserving fallback if a single form is provably infeasible is to derive the distribution by iterating the existing LOCAL conservative operator to convergence, rather than authoring a runout shape.
