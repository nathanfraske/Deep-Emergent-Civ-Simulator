# Emergent-Anatomy / Morphogenesis Handoff: bodies grown, function derived

This is a self-contained brief for the agent building the emergent-anatomy arc. Read `CLAUDE.md` first. Owner sequencing: this is the next arc after the base-liveliness arc lands and is tested, and it lands BEFORE the biosphere-into-the-run addendum, because wiring an authored-catalog anatomy into the running world would propagate the steering into every downstream thing (combat, foraging, the sentients' own bodies). File:line anchors are verified against the tree; re-grep after any edit.

## 1. The problem: two layers of authoring, both steering

Bodies today are assembled from a hand-written catalog, and their behaviour is read off authored labels rather than grown physics. Two layers impose order from outside the simulation, and each is a Principle-8 defect.

The first layer is the closed parts catalog. `BodyPlanRegistry` (crates/sim/src/anatomy.rs:88) holds four `Vec<KindDef>` lists, one each for weapons, coverings, senses, and locomotion, and `dev_default()` (anatomy.rs:136) hand-writes their membership (claws, teeth, horns, then the magical mana-lash and frost-fang; bare-hide, fur, stone-skin; vision, smell, mana-sight; rooted, walk, swim, fly, levitate). A `KindDef` is `{id, name, fantasy}` (anatomy.rs:33) and carries no geometry and no material, so it is a name and a magic bit, nothing a physics law could read. `sample_body_plan` (anatomy.rs:415) draws these ids through `pick`/`pick_organs`, filters them by the `fantasy` bit through `gated()` against a `WorldProfile{magic:bool}`, and keys weapon count on trophic layer. The menu of what a body can ever be is a list a person typed.

The second layer is the function tagging that turns those picked ids into behaviour. `Body::from_body_plan` (body.rs:464) writes authored `FunctionId` tags onto the layout (torso to `F_VITAL_CORE`, head to `F_SIGHT` when senses is non-empty, limb to `F_LOCOMOTION`, weapon to `F_STRIKE`; the four const tags at body.rs:252), which `function_integrity`, `reach`, and `strike` then read. `homeostasis.rs` carries a closed `MorphCategory` enum `{Any, Locomotion, Weapon, Sense}` (homeostasis.rs:456) and `affords()` gates a behaviour on category presence. `locomotion.rs` reads `can_walk`/`locomotion_speed` off a non-rooted mode id scaled by `sqrt(body_mass)` (locomotion.rs:284), a label-plus-mass proxy standing in for grown-limb thrust. So a body can strike because it bears a registry Weapon part and a person tagged that part `F_STRIKE`, not because anything about its shape can injure.

Deciding what a body may have, and reading what it does off a label, are both authoring the outcomes the engine claims to discover. This arc removes both.

## 2. The goal: anatomy generated, function derived, never picked and tagged

The target substrate grows a body and reads its function from physics, with one generative path for every living thing.

Anatomy is generated. The genome expresses a developmental program that grows a structure from a seed under bounded recursion, emitting a graph of segments, appendages, surfaces, and cavities, each carrying real geometry on the compose form axes (contact_area, section_modulus, arm_length, edge_radius) plus a tissue composition over the mechanical, biology, and thermal floors. No catalog supplies the menu; the genome and the floors do.

Function is derived, never picked and tagged. A part's role is read from its own geometry and material by running the floor laws over it, blind to any id, exactly as a designed artifact's function is read today. A dense hard point at small contact area that clears `cut_penetrate` and `contact_pressure` is a weapon. A tube that clears `tube_resonance` and couples the medium is a voice or an ear. A light-transducing patch through `refractive_contrast` sees. A vane that clears `aerodynamic_lift` and `drag_force` glides and yields thrust. The outer tissue layer's `mat.indentation_hardness` is the covering's armor. The emergent name is derived from the winning physics the way `trophic_label` is read from what a thing eats (biosphere.rs:173), or it survives as a pure cosmetic with zero behavioural read. `F_STRIKE`, `F_SIGHT`, `F_LOCOMOTION`, `MorphCategory`, and the `sqrt(body_mass)` locomotion proxy all retire into physics predicates.

Magic is a field a tissue couples to. A reserved, fail-loud thaumic floor carries the ambient field and the tissue coupling, and a thaumic law reads what a tissue does in that field. A mana part is a tissue whose composition couples the field, derived the way a hard point is a weapon. The world profile's magic flag stays as an authored-physics input under Principle 9, now expressed as whether the field is present, not as a per-part catalog filter. A grounded world installs no thaumic floor, so no tissue can couple and no magical organ can be read: absence by emergence, not by gate.

Biosphere creatures and sentient bodies are unified. `Species.body_plan` (biosphere.rs:157) and `Race.body` (race.rs:111) already share the `BodyPlan` struct; under the arc both become the one grown structure, and `Body::from_body_plan`'s hardcoded layout is replaced by growth-from-genome. One generative substrate and one derive-function dispatch serve creatures and people alike.

## 3. The pattern already half-built

This arc extends the engine rather than inventing a mechanism, because the derive-from-physics move is already load-bearing in two places.

The composition evaluator of Part 41 already reads a designed artifact's function from physics over geometry and material. `eval_leaf` (crates/compose/src/eval.rs:304) resolves a material by physics content id, never by name, reads geometry and material by axis-id string, runs the structural kernels, and returns a dimensionless safety fraction whose collapse boundary is the material's own yield and fracture data, not an authored number. It is memoised by a seed-omitted content id, and `evaluate_uncached` (eval.rs:249) is a standing oracle proving warm cache equals cold, bit for bit. Every quantity is an Interval of Fixed, no float, no RNG. The one gap is breadth: the dispatch runs only the structural path (`bend_stress`, `fracture_onset`) over three geometry axes, while laws.rs already carries the kernel for every other function class the catalog enumerates: `contact_pressure`/`cut_penetrate` for pierce, `tube_resonance`/`speed_of_sound` for voice and ear, `refractive_contrast`/`optical_depth` for sight, `aerodynamic_lift`/`drag_force` for glide, `conduction`/`convective_flux` for insulation, `membrane_gas_flux` for respiration, and the floor data already carries the matching geometry and material vocabulary.

The sentient organ reserve is already derived from composition, not tagged. `OrganKindDef` carries a `TissueComposition` over the biology floor (anatomy.rs:51), and which reserve an organ backs is read from that composition against the floor, so nobody authors "fat-body is metabolic" (anatomy.rs:46). The physiology layer runs this read at whole-body scale: `whole_body_surface`, `whole_body_muscle_force`, `whole_body_specific_heat`, and `whole_body_energy_density` (physiology.rs) are pure Fixed reads over the organ set. The same discipline names species from the food web: `trophic_label` (biosphere.rs:173) derives kingdom and diet and stores nothing.

So the pieces are a proven label-blind physics-over-geometry evaluator with a proven LOD cache, and a proven derive-function-from-composition read at organ scale. The arc joins them: give the evaluator the full law dispatch, give the genome a structure to grow, and point the read at grown segments instead of authored form primitives.

## 4. The ordered, incremental build plan

The owner's three steps are the spine. Each lands on a proven substrate and produces an observable milestone before the next begins.

**Step one: derive a part's function from its own material and geometry instead of its labeled kind, while growth stays crude.** Generalize the compose leaf from the single structural path into a function-law dispatch registry, each entry binding one physics kernel to its required geometry-axis set, its required material-axis set, and which output is the capability score, in the fixed-kernel/data-membership split the `CombinatorRegistry` and `ProxyRegistry` already use. The leaf runs every kernel whose input axes the structure carries (the `geo()`/`mat()` helpers already return zero for an absent axis, so presence is the natural gate), and function is read as the resulting capability vector, never stored. Replace the closed `FORM_AXES` mechanical-only const (form.rs:38) with a geometry-axis registry over the floor's full geometry vocabulary, sibling to `QuantityAxis`. On the sim side, point the affordance read at physics: `can_strike` is a grown part clearing penetrate and pressure, `can_move` is a grown part yielding thrust, `can_sense(channel)` is a grown transducer coupling the field; `F_STRIKE`/`F_SIGHT`/`F_LOCOMOTION`, `MorphCategory.affords()`, and the locomotion label-plus-mass proxy retire. Growth stays crude: the existing `sample_body_plan` still produces the parts, but each part now carries geometry and material and its function is read, not tagged. Dependency: the compose leaf, the memo, the Interval machinery, and the organ-composition read, all in hand. Guards: Principle 8/9, the dispatch keys only on axis presence, never a substance name, a `RaceId`, or a fantasy bit, and the label stays an emergent read like `trophic_label`; determinism, every kernel is Fixed-only, counter-keyed, canonical-ordered, folded into `state_hash`, and the `evaluate_uncached` oracle is extended to the grown-part path asserting warm equals cold; Principle 11, the pierce/see/glide/insulate reference levels (target-hardness distribution, phototransduction flux floor, lift-to-weight ratio, insulation flux ceiling) enter as reserved-with-basis manifest constants, fail-loud; LOD, key each read in the content-id memo to the tier it was computed at so a coarse read widens the Interval and stays a conservative bound. Milestone: an existing hand-drawn part earns its function from its physics with the `F_*` tags deleted, and `biosphere_steering` still passes with the assertion extended to "function is a pure read of grown physics with no layer, kingdom, or race key."

**Step two: replace the crude draw with a real developmental program that grows structure from the genome.** Add a `Channel::Morphogen(id)` variant sibling to `Composition` and `Controller` (genome.rs:150), so growth-rule parameters (production rules of an L-system or gene-regulatory recursion, thresholds, growth rates, per-segment material and composition coordinates) become heritable data expressed through the same `GeneSet::express` and evolving under the pool exactly as the composition and controller channels already do. A deterministic fixed-point kernel `grow(genome, env) -> Structure` iterates the rules from a seed under bounded depth, emitting the segment/appendage/surface/cavity graph with geometry on the form axes plus a per-segment tissue vector. The step-one dispatch then reads function off the grown graph unchanged. Unification lands here: `Species.body_plan` and `Race.body` both become the one grown structure, and `Body::from_body_plan`'s hardcoded layout is replaced by growth-from-genome. Dependency: step one (the read must exist before there is novel structure to read) and the genome channel/pool machinery. Guards: determinism, the recursion is the heaviest new kernel, so it is integer Fixed-point, counter-keyed RNG on a new `Phase` with `R-RNG-COORD` slots, hard-capped in depth and segment count (a termination guarantee like the biosphere resample bound), folded into `state_hash`; Principle 8/9, the developmental program carries no per-niche, per-layer, or per-race branch; Principle 11, the growth-rate bounds, the depth cap, and the geometry-to-law thresholds are reserved fail-loud with basis; LOD, keep the current scalar `BodyPlan` as the LOD-0 aggregate digest and materialize the full grown structure only on promotion, content-addressing it with the compose memo pattern so a grown structure and its derived functions are a pure function of genome, env, and floors. Milestone: a genome grows a novel-shaped body no catalog contains, its parts read as weapon/sense/locomotion/covering purely from their physics, and the same substrate produces both a biosphere creature and a sentient body.

**Step three: let selection prune incoherent bodies rather than a niche filter.** Coherence stays a physics query that drives the two culls that already run, never a new seed-time gate. A body whose grown parts do not cohere reads a whole-body viability at or below its death floor (a negative safety fraction in the compose sense) and dies through the runtime `Stock` collapse cull (epoch.rs:221), the same over-harvest cull that removes an orphan; the seed-time food-web least-fixed-point cull (`grounded`, biosphere.rs:304) removes only the physically impossible. Generalize the single energy-reserve `birth_viable` query into a whole-body viability read reusing the compose safety fraction. Dependency: steps one and two. Guards: Principle 8/9 is the whole point of the step, nothing rejects a plan for its shape, so the anti-steering proof is extended to every new coherence dimension (`biosphere_steering` already asserts a body-swap leaves the grounded set identical and that forcing mass to each extreme never moves the verdict); determinism and LOD ride the cached archetype read from step two. Milestone: an incoherent grown body dies through the existing runtime cull with no code that inspects morphology to reject it, and the extended steering suite confirms the cull biases no morphological distribution.

The thaumic-field treatment of magic threads all three steps as its own reserved-physics track. Add a sixth base dimension `thaum:i8` to `Dimension`, following the wave-3 precedent that added `current` as the fifth base (every earlier axis carries the new exponent as zero, preserving neutrality over the monomial). Add a `thaumic_floor.toml` whose every axis carries `Provenance::FantasyReserved` (the sole authored fantasy layer, Principle 9), parallel to `mag.*`: `thaum.field_potential` (the ambient field, sourced by the Part 34 mana source riding the Part 18 GPU field machinery), `thaum.coupling_coefficient`/`susceptibility` (a tissue property, analogue of `mag.permeability`), `thaum.capacity` (storable charge, analogue of `elec.capacitance`, what `mana-sac` stands in for), and `thaum.transduction_coupling` (for mana-sight). Add closed-form integer laws `thaumic_coupling` and `thaumic_discharge` on the EM template. A magical organ then derives: a high-coupling tissue concentrating stored thaum at a sharp discharge geometry emits a mana-lash the way a hard dense point is a weapon. The `fantasy:bool`, `gated()`, `WorldProfile{magic}` filter, the magical catalog rows, the `mana-sac` fixture, and Part 35's `MeasureKind::Exotic` special case all retire; magic becomes an ordinary floor domain gated by field presence. Every thaumic constant is surfaced reserved-with-basis (the coupling and capacity ranges anchored to the `mag.*` envelope, the transduction ranges to sensory-transduction efficiencies in the literature), fail-loud, and the `R-DEEPTECH-PHYSICS` note stands: the mechanical neutrality test is blind to magic axes, so their outcome-blindness rests on the prescribed cross-seed-divergence pre-registration, which this arc must honour.

## 5. Honest limits and the LOD/caching that keeps it affordable

The limits are real and stated. Magic axes evade the mechanical steering check by construction, so the thaumic floor's outcome-blindness cannot be proven by the neutrality test and rests on pre-registration plus cross-seed divergence; this is the largest new-content risk and the largest reserved-value surface. The nonlinear kernels (resonance `sqrt(k/m)`, aero `v^2`, radiant `T^4`, Kleiber `m^(3/4)`) are tier-resolution-dependent, so a capability read over a lumped low-LOD structure is a conservative Interval bound, not the fully resolved verdict, and reading one archetype at the pool tier loses the convexity over the member size-and-temperature distribution, the Jensen gap already declared at physiology.rs:46; that is the named `R-TIER-CONSIST` follow-on, carried as a distribution moment or documented, never silently replaced by the mean. The recursive growth kernel is the heaviest new code and its termination rests on a hard depth-and-segment cap rather than a natural bound. And the whole arc lands before the biosphere is wired into the run, so it touches library and test surface rather than a live tick loop, which lowers integration risk and shifts the proof burden onto the determinism and steering harnesses.

Affordability over a deep-time radiation of thousands of species rests on three tiers, every piece of which already exists. Tier A is the species archetype: the functional read of a species (which reserves its tissue backs, its drains, its edibility physiology, the lethality of each grown appendage, its locomotion affordances) is a pure function of its one body plan and draws no RNG, so it is computed once at founding and at a body-plan-drift event, never per generation and never per individual, and cached in a non-authoritative map keyed by a seed-omitted content id, the compose memo pattern exactly. Deep-time cost then scales with the count of distinct plans, not species times generations times members, and two built levers keep that count small: a speciation daughter inherits its parent plan unchanged (epoch.rs:264), and content-id dedup collapses convergent or unchanged plans across lineages and across worlds. A generation costs a Wright-Fisher step, not a re-derivation. Tier B is the pool: the cached archetype read feeds `birth_viable` and the edibility physiology into the `Stock` carrying capacity, no per-individual work. Tier C is the promoted individual: only on promotion is the full per-part body and its per-part reads built, as a genome-perturbed delta over the cached species base. Two caching hazards carry named guards: the archetype cache must stay non-authoritative and out of `state_hash` with the warm-equals-cold oracle mandatory (a cache-as-canon leak breaks determinism), and when plans eventually drift per lineage the distinct-plan count can approach species count, guarded by an LOD quantization of the plan into a coarse content-id bucket whose grain is a reserved-with-basis value (basis: the plan difference below which the derived read moves less than fixed-point noise).

## 6. Prime constraints and the gate

Determinism and the GPU floor are absolute (Principle 3). Every new path is integer Fixed-only, counter-keyed RNG on declared `R-RNG-COORD` slots, canonical sorted order, folded into `state_hash`; the compose `evaluate_uncached` oracle is extended to the grown-structure path and must prove warm cache equals cold bit for bit; any field riding GPU (the thaumic potential on the Part 18 machinery) holds the CubeCL bit-identical floor already established for the existing fields, verified on the 5090.

Principle 8 forbids any closed enum or lookup table where world content should emerge: the developmental program carries no per-niche, per-layer, or per-race branch, and removing the fantasy gate must not reintroduce a catalog filter. Principle 9 confines the authored hand to physics: the material floor, the thaumic floor, and the world profile are the only authored inputs, and magic-gating moves from a per-kind flag to floor-axis presence, an authored physics input the Steering Audit checks, never an authored outcome; the crate boundary holds it (compose depends on physics, kernels take only Fixed, never a material or race id). Principle 11 forbids a fabricated value: every threshold, range, growth bound, depth cap, and thaumic constant is surfaced reserved with its basis, fail-loud in the calibration manifest, defaulting to a sentinel that fails if used unset. Each new axis enters through the identical load-time discipline every existing axis passed (`AxisRange::Reserved`, provenance required, dimension-monomial neutrality), so it is a data extension, not a new code path.

The gate is the standing suite: `cargo fmt --all` before every commit (CI enforces `--check`, and a violation on main breaks every branch), `cargo clippy`, `verify.sh`, plus the design-side verification. The steering tests (`biosphere_steering`, `steering.rs` no-float and provenance-required) are extended, not merely kept green. Reserved values land in the manifest with their basis and the scenarios exercise them; the consensus-roadmap gate requires citing each item's gate and tombstoning in place on any source or design change. No number the agent surfaces is set by the agent; surfacing it reserved with its basis is the correct output.

## 7. Recommended first step

Begin with step one, deriving a part's function from its own geometry and material while growth stays crude. It is the smallest change that proves the thesis, and it de-risks everything after it. It touches only the compose leaf (generalize the dispatch, replace `FORM_AXES` with a geometry-axis registry) and the sim's affordance reads (retire the `F_*` tags and `MorphCategory` for physics predicates), both on substrates already in hand and both blind to id, so no new determinism surface and no new floor domain is introduced yet. It gives an observable milestone that gates the rest: an existing part earns its function from its physics with the authored tags deleted, and the extended `biosphere_steering` assertion (function is a pure read of grown physics with no layer, kingdom, or race key) either holds or exposes a hidden label dependency before any generative machinery is built. Growing structure (step two) has nothing to read its function until this dispatch exists, and pruning incoherent bodies (step three) has no coherence physics to query until both are in place, so the read is the foundation. The thaumic floor is the largest new-content item and the one steering surface the neutrality test cannot cover, so it is deferred behind the proven mechanical read and introduced as its own reserved-physics track once the derive-not-tag pattern is demonstrated on grounded physics.

Key files: `crates/compose/src/eval.rs` (the leaf to generalize, the memo and `evaluate_uncached` oracle to extend), `crates/compose/src/form.rs` (`FORM_AXES` to replace with a geometry-axis registry), `crates/physics/src/laws.rs` (the kernels to dispatch), `crates/sim/src/anatomy.rs` (the catalog and fantasy gate to retire), `crates/sim/src/body.rs` (`from_body_plan` layout and the `F_*` tags), `crates/sim/src/homeostasis.rs` (`MorphCategory` and `affords()`), `crates/sim/src/locomotion.rs` (the label-plus-mass proxy), `crates/sim/src/genome.rs` (the `Channel::Morphogen` variant for step two), and `crates/physics/data/` plus `crates/physics/src/lib.rs` (the `thaum:i8` dimension and `thaumic_floor.toml` for the magic track).

## Acceptance: blind concept verification

Each step's observable milestone is validated by blind concept verification (AGENTIC_ADDENDUM.md section 8), not only by its co-designed test: hand a blind agent the step's concept as a plain claim (no mechanism, no hints) plus a deterministic run log and the prime directives, and confirm the concept manifests in the behaviour. A milestone is not done until a blind run returns realized rather than inert, mismeasured, or not observable. This is why each step ships its observability reader: an unobserved emergence scores not-observable and is not delivered.

## Derived application: the communication-reach substrate (synthesis sub-arc, after this arc)

Once part function is derived from grown physics, communication reach falls out as a physics of signal propagation, and it supersedes the liveliness step-5 co-location bucket (everyone in the cell hears) with real propagation. It unifies this arc (the grown emission and reception organs), the environmental-fields arc (the per-cell medium), and the perceptual-geometry acoustic substrate (extended from a being's self-confusability to spatial emitter-to-receiver reach). The physics is mostly present: `acoustic_absorption` (laws.rs:1238) already returns a medium-dependent absorption coefficient whose path attenuation runs through the `optical_depth` path kernel, and the floors for every mode exist (acoustic, chem_optics for light, em/elec for field, chem for scent), so a new mode is a floor domain plus the organ that couples it, never an authored comm table.

The stack, four derived layers: (1) EMIT, a grown emission organ (a resonant cavity, a light organ, an electric organ, a scent gland) produces a signal in its mode at an INTENSITY with a biological ceiling (the organ's size and development) and a behavioural lever the being modulates within it (whisper to yell, dim to flash) at a metabolic cost, the volume knob being effort bounded by biology; (2) PROPAGATE, the intensity attenuates through the per-cell medium by the mode's physics over distance (sound by absorption plus geometric spread, carrying far in water and dying in thin air; light by optical depth plus line-of-sight; chemical by diffusion plus wind advection; electric by field falloff), so the medium sets which modes are viable; (3) RECEIVE, a matching receptor organ detects the signal only if the attenuated intensity clears the receptor's own threshold, no matching receptor and the mode is imperceptible, with directionality falling out (sound omnidirectional, sign requiring the receiver facing and looking, scent following the wind); (4) COMPREHEND, a separate layer, a received signal is understood only if the receiver shares the code (the language), so overhearing a foreign tongue is reception without comprehension and a sign is seen if looking and known.

The emergent payoffs: whisper is private and yell is public, a scent-mark is a message left in time, a drum or beacon is a built long-range signal; the MODE itself emerges from the organs a lineage grew and the medium it lives in (light for a bioluminescent lineage, field for an electric one like electric fish, sound or electricity in deep water, seismic or chemical where light and sound both fail), never an authored per-race table; and the group-broadcast reach becomes whoever within the attenuated range carries a matching receptor, so eavesdropping, privacy, secrecy, and long-range signalling all emerge. Principle 9: the authored hand is only the mode physics and the world's medium; the organs are grown, the reach is the physics, and comprehension is the shared code, so there is no authored who-hears-whom and no per-race communication mode. Determinism: propagation is a pure fixed-point read over the medium field in canonical order, reception a threshold read per receiver in StableId order, the emission-effort draw counter-keyed; acceptance is the blind concept-verification gate on the concept "a being can raise the volume of a signal so it reaches further, and only those with a matching receptor within range receive it."
