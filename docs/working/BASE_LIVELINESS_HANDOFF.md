# Base-Level Liveliness Handoff: make the world alive and bounded

This is a self-contained brief for the agent building the base-level liveliness arc. Read `CLAUDE.md` first, then this file. The world today compiles, replays bit-identically on the 5090 GPU oracle, and runs at scale, but it is dead: static blobs at the dawn band, population climbing without bound, and a talk substrate that runs every tick over an empty current. Almost every mechanism this arc needs is already built and unit-tested. The work is wiring, feeding, and observing, not inventing. File:line anchors are verified against the tree; re-grep for true lines after any edit.

---

## 1. What is done, and why the world is quiet

The substrates are real and tested in isolation. Temperature is a fully built, GPU-pinned diffusion field (`Field`, runner.rs:268, its CubeCL twin proven bit-identical in tests/diffusion_gate). Field-coupled locomotion is complete end to end: `step_with_field_dirs` merges a field-direction percept and a signed setpoint into a genome-expressed controller that chooses MOVE-with-heading or INGEST, walkers stepped in stable-id order, positions in fixed point, folded into `state_hash` (locomotion.rs:452, controller.rs:238). The dose-versus-tolerance edibility floor is built and tested (edibility.rs `assess`/`net_harm`). The two-tier talk substrate (gossip, dialogue, aggregate diffusion, enculturation) is built and correct. The Part-15 stock machinery (logistic regrowth, take/deposit, collapse) exists and is tested (stocks.rs). The physics floor the sibling fields need is resolved and unused: `net_nutrition` (Liebig min-fold), `harm_class` (integer-Hill dose), `evaporation_rate`, `saturation_vapor_pressure`, `thermal_buoyancy` (laws.rs).

The world is quiet for three concrete, independent reasons, each a live wire with no current.

**Cause 1: blank dawn controllers, so no movement.** Founders receive a genome-expressed controller, but the founding race `GeneSet` carries no `Channel::Controller` locus (the channel appears only in the expression machinery, evolve.rs:233, never in a founding gene block). With no gene feeding the channel, every controller weight expresses to `Fixed::ZERO`, activation is zero, the MOVE arm never fires (`if d.activation > Fixed::ZERO` is false, locomotion.rs:498), and the temperature gradient the runner computes reaches weights that ignore it. Every founder idles on its single dawn cell. The harness compounds it: `mutation_rate` and `additive_mutation_step` are zero (run_world.rs:272), so a weight could not drift off zero even if a locus existed.

**Cause 2: no carrying capacity, so population is unbounded.** The registry installed at dawn is `dev_thermal` (run_world.rs:373), which carries only the TEMPERATURE axis. `ENERGY` and `WATER` are defined (homeostasis.rs:178) but never installed, so hunger and thirst do not exist in the running world: a being can die of thermal exposure and of nothing else, which is very likely the primary cause of the unbounded growth. `ResourceField` is constructed empty on the only production path (runner.rs:733) with no setter on `Embodiment`, so beings never perceive or ingest anything. `assess`/`net_harm` have zero live callers, and the harm sink in the ingest path is a deliberate no-op that reads toxins and discards them (locomotion.rs:546). With no starvation, no thirst, no depletable food, and no dose, nothing bounds a lineage, so `conflict_coefficient` has no measured scarcity to derive from and stays an authored dial (scenario.rs:728).

**Cause 3: conversation half-wired and unobserved.** Gossip is armed by default (world.rs:884) but reads `first_committed`, which walks the dynamic `Mind.beliefs` map; dawn seeding gives each founder an empty beliefs map and only intrinsic axioms, so `first_committed` returns None for every mind and gossip spreads nothing. Dialogue returns immediately because nothing is promoted (world.rs:2871). Aggregate diffusion and enculturation are never armed. The harness reader measures the wrong system: `band_belief` reads `intrinsic_of` (the axiom seed) while talk moves `Mind.beliefs`, for which there is no population reader. And conversation buckets by the frozen `place_of` (PlaceId), while the runner moves bodies in Coord3 and never syncs that back, so even a migrant keeps gossiping with its frozen dawn band: ideas cannot ride movement.

## 2. The goal

A living, bounded world. Peoples disperse from the dawn band across a varied landscape of heat, water, light, and soil; they seek and compete for food and fresh water that deplete when grazed and regrow on a logistic curve; they adapt to gradients (a salt flat is lethal to a naive line and livable to a heritable halophile line that selects in, never a gate); population per lineage settles where each race's own metabolic draw meets what the land can regrow, with no authored cap; beings that walk into a shared cell meet, mix, and trade belief and knowledge, so ideas travel with the people who carry them; and any being doing something with a story arc is lifted to full resolution so its whole arc is watchable. Every outcome emerges from rules over data, never a template, and every run stays deterministic and GPU-verifiable.

## 3. The ordered build plan

Ordered so the world visibly moves early and cheaply, then bounds itself, then adapts, then talks and grows protagonists. Each step names the mechanism and its wiring point, its dependency, its Principle-8 (gradient not gate), Principle-9, and determinism guards, and the observable milestone that proves it landed. Ship each step behind its own scenario and reader.

**Step 1 (FIRST, the keystone): field-coupled movement on the temperature field that already exists.** Effort M, pure data, no Rust change. Populate the controller substrate `Controller::express` (controller.rs:356) already reads: (a) add a Controller-channel gene block to the founding race `GeneSet`, one `GeneDef` feeding `Channel::Controller(ControllerParamId(k))` for each of `layout.weight_count()` weights (controller.rs:196), sibling to the cognition loci; (b) seed the dawn reaction norm through those alleles to the taxis pattern already written and tested (`taxis_controller`, locomotion.rs:719): bias to move-activation, here-flag to suppress-move and drive-ingest, source-direction to the move heading, reserve level to gate ingest, and the TEMPERATURE signed and gradient slots to steer toward comfort from either side; (c) leave `Walker.x/y` Coord3-authoritative and disperse it while PlaceId stays frozen at the dawn band (the deferred PlaceId-from-cell reconciliation). Dependency: none, this rides the existing temperature `Field`, `gradient_at`, and the re-syncing located index (runner.rs:1344). Guards: the taxis weight magnitudes are reserved with basis (set so the clamped activation saturates decisively toward following the gradient and ingesting when the reserve is low), never fabricated; turn on per-being developmental variation (`environment_variance`, currently 0) and, so selection rather than the author fixes the coupling, `mutation_rate`/`additive_mutation_step` (currently 0) against the movement-dependent fitness step 3 gives it, the mutation using the counter-keyed genome draw, never a sequential RNG, loci keyed off `ControllerParamId`, never a RaceId branch. Determinism is already guarded (counter-keyed exploration, id-sorted walkers, float-free controller, x/y in `state_hash`). Observable milestone: founders leave their single dawn cell and disperse along the temperature gradient; the migration reader goes nonzero on the first run.

**Step 2: the environmental field stack, hydrology and primary productivity first.** Effort L (two of the XL field arc; wind, dust, salinity land in step 4). Generalize the field layer from one scalar into a data-driven stack of pinned-stencil fields (Principle 11: the stencil is fixed Rust, the membership is data), each a `Vec<Fixed>` on the shared grid with its own derived transport and forcing, stepped in canonical field-id order in `step_inner` right after `self.field.step`. Hydrology: a water-depth field sourced by precipitation (moisture crossing `saturation_vapor_pressure`, laws.rs:1351), sunk by `evaporation_rate` (laws.rs:1375), transported by gravity downhill routing over the frozen `Tile::elevation` (a downhill-flux advection form, distinct from symmetric diffusion, so it pools in basins). Primary productivity: NOT a stored scalar and NOT a dead-zone cutoff, derived per cell each tick as `net_nutrition` (the Liebig min-fold, laws.rs:120) over water times light (latitude via `blend_latitude`) times temperature (the diffused `Field`) times soil-nutrient, then written into `ResourceField` as `bio.energy_density` supply, closing the loop step 3 consumes. Guards: Principle 8, productivity is the continuous Liebig product, never an `if water<X {barren}` cutoff; Principle 9, transport coefficients derive from the medium physics like `thermal_diffusivity`, and the forcing constants (evaporation, precipitation threshold) are reserved fail-loud with basis. Determinism: each field is the same pinned integer stencil in canonical row-major order; the new advection kernel's upwind branch is a deterministic `select` on the velocity sign, its CFL bound a fixed numerics constant like `STENCIL_STABILITY_BOUND`, not a reserved dial; pin an explicit within-tick field order and fold every field into `state_hash` in that identical order (a field omitted from the hash passes replay while hiding divergence); each field earns a GPU-oracle gate mirroring tests/diffusion_gate before it rides canonical state. Observable milestone: the per-cell field reader shows water pooling in basins and drying under heat, and a productivity map tracking the water-light-temperature product; `ResourceField` starts carrying supply.

**Step 3: the living resource loop, where carrying capacity bites.** Effort L. This stops the unbounded explosion. Add a standing QUANTITY beside the existing quality: a per-tile `Stock` per biology-floor backing class (never a biome or race id), each Stock's capacity and regen sized from the step-2 productivity field. Thread `step_with_field_dirs` from `&ResourceField` to `&mut` so the INGEST arm calls `Stock::take`; the id-sorted walk makes sequential per-tile draws deterministic competition with no new RNG. Reconcile bite-size with stock loss with a reserved conversion (like `stocks::flow`'s transfer efficiency) so a being's gain is conservation-honest against the tile's loss. Regrow once per tick in canonical Coord3 order after all beings act (`Stock::step`), giving Part-15 logistic regrowth and over-harvest collapse for free. Wire it into the real run: add the missing `Embodiment` resource setter so `assemble_dawn_embodiment` (worldbuild.rs:410) builds a `ResourceField` from `TileMap` plus productivity (mirroring the medium field fold); swap the installed registry from `dev_thermal` to one carrying ENERGY and WATER (run_world.rs:373); replace the temperature-only `Physiology::dev_for_registry` (worldbuild.rs:475) with real per-race physiology. Dependency: step 2, and it gives step 1's selection pressure teeth (a being must walk to food). Guards: Principle 8, feed the continuous standing amount into `laws::satisfaction`'s supply argument (a half-grazed patch yields half through the existing Liebig math), no boolean stock-empty gate, capacity/regen a continuous function of productivity, never a per-BiomeId lookup; Principle 9, one uniform productivity-to-capacity physics keyed off the backing-class string, never a RaceId branch. Determinism: the `&mut` introduces the desired order-sensitivity (who eats first gets more) pinned to the canonical StableId walk with no cross-being parallelism on a shared tile; regrowth walks Coord3 in the same canonical order the Field and `TileMap::state_hash` use, folded into `Runner::state_hash`; any GPU offload clears the R-GPU-CANON-PIN bit-identity bar. Observable milestone: population stops climbing and settles around what the land can regrow; a grazed-out tile empties and beings move on; the carrying-capacity pressure reader shows scarcity for the first time.

**Step 4: organism-relative carrying capacity, the conflict_coefficient derivation, and tolerance-gradient adaptation.** Effort L, plus the remaining fields (wind, dust, salinity). Three coupled wires. (A) Heritable tolerance: express `Physiology.tolerances` and the Hill exponent from the genome the way the controller is expressed, replacing the empty `dev_for_registry`, through a data-defined map from floor toxin-class id to gene channel (a sibling registry to the composition channel), never a single scalar collapsing all classes. (B) Per-cell dose and the harm sink: add the wind vector field (from `thermal_buoyancy`/pressure gradients, advecting heat, moisture, and a dust scalar, so rain shadow and dust emerge) and the soil salinity/nutrient fields (salinity concentrates where standing water evaporates), present each per-cell field as a `bio.*` toxin class on the cell `Composition.toxins`, then in `step_with_field_dirs` read the cell Composition, call `assess`/`net_harm` against the being's own `Physiology.tolerances`, and apply the measured `net_harm` as a drain via `Homeostasis::adjust` on a condition reserve, turning the no-op at locomotion.rs:546 into the live coupling. (C) Organism-relative capacity: a lineage's realized intake at a cell is nutrition satisfied minus the net_harm drain, so capacity falls out per that lineage's own tolerances with no separate capacity field; a census walk over the located index (canonical order) measures located population over summed per-lineage realized capacity, and that measured scarcity feeds where scenario.rs:728 supplies the authored dial, leaving `conflict_pressure` (value.rs:525) pure and swapping only its caller. Guards: Principle 8, the dose goes through `net_harm`'s graded integer-Hill response applied as a continuous reserve drain, never a threshold and never the tent-gate `Niche::suitability`; death stays the emergent reserve-through-floor cull, so a high-tolerance being at high salinity survives and adapts in rather than being excluded at a fixed dose. Principle 9, tolerance is per-toxin-class heritable data keyed off floor axis ids, never a RaceId branch and never one scalar; salinity is another `bio.*` class with an owner-reserved dose scale, no terran constant. Principle 11, the dose scales, tolerance-expression weights, `FloorCaps`, and salinity/dust forcing are reserved fail-loud with basis. Wind and salinity each need their own pinned stencil, GPU gate, and hash fold, and the within-tick field order extends to temperature, then wind, then hydrology and dust reading the just-stepped wind, then productivity reading all. Observable milestone: a salt flat is lethal to a naive lineage yet livable to a high-tolerance halophile line that selects in over generations, and conflict pressure moves with measured scarcity rather than a dial.

**Step 5: conversation liveliness and the dynamic readers.** Effort L. Four coordinated wirings, each reusing an existing mechanism. Belief source: feed minds real first-order beliefs through the perceive path (`perceive`/`gather_trace`, world.rs:3501, which turn a co-located trace into a `consider`-ed belief through the being's own acuity and sensorium), emitting environment traces (a water, forage, or hazard location the fields define) so a discovered fact enters `Mind.beliefs` and rides gossip. Movement coupling: the one seam is `colocated_index` (world.rs:2282); have the runner publish each being's live Coord3 cell from its reconciled located index into the World each tick before `world.tick`, and let the conversation phases bucket by a per-tick `BTreeMap<CellId, Vec<StableId>>` derived fresh in canonical (cell, being-id) order, used only inside gossip/converse, leaving PlaceId frozen for lineage. Arm the movers in `build_dawn_runner` as fail-loud manifest reads for `belief_diffusion_rate` and `stubbornness_split` (both reserved), so `diffuse_beliefs` and `enculturate` run. On the posed question: strengthened gossip plus movement-coupled co-location plus the dynamic readers is the right default, because gossip already delivers the same `told_weight` magnitude as a dialogue assertion (`apply_assertion` is the shared integration), so population-scale spread needs no per-being dialogue; dialogue promotion is the LOD refinement covered in section 4. Guards: Principle 8, belief contagion is already a gradient (evidence at a trust-scaled weight through `consider`), so keep it a dose and keep the source a trace through the being's own acuity, never a per-race injection. Principle 9, the belief source keys off per-being heritable data, never a RaceId branch. Determinism, the live-cell index is derived fresh each tick from the canonically-reconciled located index (cell-then-id order), adding no persistent state, and the GOSSIP/CONVERSE draws stay counter-keyed on speaker plus clock plus phase; conversation is CPU-only, so the GPU stencil bit-identity is untouched. Observable milestone: a belief seeded in one dawn band rides a migrant into another band's cell and the consensus reader shows the idea spreading; the knowledge-holder count climbs outward from the origin.

## 4. The promotion policy: growing protagonists (owner directive)

Promotion is the seat of individual-scale liveliness, and the first runs had it empty: `World::promote` (world.rs:965, the Part 54 lift to move-by-move dialogue and the explicit individual tier) is called only by tests, so every being ran the aggregate gossip fallback and no named life was ever visible. The two-tier LOD machinery (`lod.rs`) is built and tested with EXACT, CONSERVED promote/restrict; only the promotion POLICY (who promotes when) is deliberately unwired.

The owner ruling reframes it: promotion is not a scarce performance optimization to be used sparingly, it is the RESOLUTION KNOB on the story, and it DEFAULTS GENEROUS. The goal: any being doing something with a narrative ARC (a hunt, a journey, a fight, a courtship, a migration) is promoted to full individual modelling for the DURATION of that arc so the whole arc is watchable start to finish, then restricted back to the aggregate when the arc resolves, unless the arc made it persistently significant (a first kill that founds a reputation, a leader who stays lifted). Design shape:

- ARC-SCOPED: promote on arc-entry (a being commits to a notable activity, emergent from its drives and its controller's chosen action), restrict on arc-resolution. The steady-state promoted count is bounded to beings currently in an arc, so it scales and it does not append every mind's every assertion to the canonical event log forever (the LOD concern that motivated sparing promotion is answered by arc-scoping, not by rarity).
- DEFAULT-GENEROUS budget: the promotion budget is a reserved lever and it DEFAULTS HIGH (liveliness over frugality), the opposite of the LOD framing's sparing default; dial it down only if compute ever demands it. The 5090 and the aggregate tier for the masses absorb generous promotion.
- CHEAP BY CONSTRUCTION: promote/restrict is exact and conserved (lod.rs), so generous promotion costs compute, never fidelity; nothing is lost restricting a being back.
- EMERGENT TRIGGER (Principle 9): the arcs emerge (hunger drives a hunt, a contest starts a fight); promotion turns up the resolution on what is already happening, chosen by emergent salience (the stakes of the activity, a life event, a conflict), never a scripted "promote the hero." Protagonists are discovered, not authored.
- DETERMINISM: the promotion trigger and the budget selection are deterministic (a canonical-order, threshold-or-counter-keyed choice); promote/restrict already fold conserved into the tier-consistency hash. Guard the talk-hole trap: gossip skips a promoted speaker and converse needs a promoted partner, so promote whole co-located bands together or the promotion criterion must keep an arc's participants promoted as a set.
- OBSERVABILITY: the harness needs a reader for the promoted set and the arcs in flight, so the next runs SHOW named individuals living their arcs (the hunter on the hunt, start to finish) rather than only demographic aggregates.

This is what turns thousands of anonymous gossipers into these particular hunters, rivals, leaders, and founders whose choices ripple. Every individual-scale emergent story lives at the promoted tier, so the promotion policy is what lets them appear. Sequence it after step 1 (so beings have arcs to promote on) and alongside step 5 (so promoted individuals talk richly).

The next runs must show liveliness, so the readers are part of each step's definition of done. All read hashed state, so they never perturb the run.

A dynamic belief-spread reader over `committed_beliefs` (world.rs:1269) across a band or the population, reporting agreement and coverage on a sampled (subject, attribute), retiring the misdirected `band_belief` reader that reads the axiom seed. And a promoted-set-and-arcs reader (section 4), so named lives are legible.

## 6. Prime constraints and the gate

Determinism and GPU field-oracle bit-identity are the hard floor. Every new scalar field is the same pinned integer diffusion stencil in canonical row-major order, bit-identical and thread-invariant by construction; every field folds into `Field::hash` and `Runner::state_hash` in canonical field-id order (a field omitted from the hash passes replay while hiding divergence); every field earns its own GPU-oracle gate mirroring tests/diffusion_gate before it rides canonical state. The advection kernel's upwind branch is a deterministic `select` on the velocity sign, its CFL bound a fixed numerics constant. The within-tick field-step order is pinned and documented as the temperature-before-cognition order is, and `state_hash` folds in that identical order.

Principle 8 (gradient not gate) is the sharpest emergence risk across the arc: every field-to-organism coupling routes through an existing measured law (`net_nutrition`, `net_harm`/`harm_class`, `satisfaction`) against per-entity heritable data, never a new authored threshold and never a `RaceId` branch. No `if water<X {barren}` cutoff, no boolean stock-empty gate, no `if salinity>X {impassable}`, no per-`BiomeId` capacity lookup. Principle 9: transport coefficients derive from the medium physics like `derive_field_diffusion`, physics the one authored cultural input while every cultural outcome emerges. Principle 11: every number the mechanisms need (field forcing, taxis weight magnitudes, dose scales, tolerance-expression weights, `FloorCaps`, the belief and enculturation rates and trace salience, the productivity-to-capacity and regen and bite-to-draw conversions, the promotion budget and threshold) is surfaced reserved with its basis and read fail-loud, defaulting to a sentinel that fails loudly if used unset, never a silent `Default`. The agent surfaces these; the agent never sets one.

The mechanical gate before any commit: `cargo fmt --all` (CI enforces `--check`; a violation on main breaks every branch), clippy clean, `verify.sh` green, manifest and scenarios resolve. Each step ships behind a scenario that exercises the new field or loop. Source and design changes trip the stop hook requiring `docs/working/CONSENSUS_ROADMAP.md` updated (cite each item's gate, tombstone in place). The GPU oracle gate is verified on the 5090 box and is not part of CI.

## 7. Recommended first step

Start with Step 1, field-coupled movement on the temperature field. It is the cheapest path to visible life: M-effort and pure data, adding a Controller gene block and seeding the taxis reaction norm onto a mechanism (`Controller::express`, `step_with_field_dirs`, the temperature `Field`) already built, tested, GPU-pinned, and folded into the replay hash, so no Rust and no new field is required to turn the static dawn blobs into a dispersing population on the very next run. It is also the keystone the rest rides on: movement carries dose adaptation to the gradients (step 4), forage-and-water seeking against a depletable field (step 3), idea spreading on the move (step 5), and arcs to promote on (section 4), and it gives the later selection pressure something to act on. And it forces the first, simplest reader (migration and dispersal) into existence before the heavier fields land. Carrying capacity is the loudest correctness bug and follows immediately in steps 2 and 3, but it cannot bite until productivity and a depletable resource loop exist, so movement is the correct, cheap, unblocking first cut.

Key anchors: the founding `GeneSet` and `assemble_dawn_embodiment` (worldbuild.rs:410), `Controller::express` and `weight_count` and the taxis pattern (controller.rs:196, 356; locomotion.rs:719), the runner percept and re-sync (runner.rs:1211), and the harness dials to open, `environment_variance`, `mutation_rate`, `additive_mutation_step` (run_world.rs:272).
