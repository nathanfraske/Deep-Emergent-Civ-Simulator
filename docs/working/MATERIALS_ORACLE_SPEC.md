# The Materials Oracle: the composition-derived materials arc (owner design spec)

Owner-authored (2026-07-12). The consolidated design spec of the materials arc, provenance inline per the seven-tag register ([D] derived, [M] measured, [E] estimator, [C] closure, [A] authored, [W] written state, [X] contingency). This is the answer to the authoring-registry defect: the named-substance floor (granite density=2700 authored, the mineral registry, the formula column) is DELETED, and materials become a pure function of composition, environment, path, and seed. Status fields render per module when wired.

One oracle, one flow:
`K_solid(x_local; E; h, seed) -> {phases, modes, solution compositions, texture, properties +/- bands, records, flags}`

## Stage 0, static data plane (what exists before any world)
Layer 1: constants [M/exact by SI], electron spin with g = 2 + alpha/2pi [D]. Layer 2 columns the arc consumes: isotope masses [M]; IE [M, H exact at Rydberg]; EA [M; Slater E]; chi_M and chi_AR [D from IE, EA, Z_eff, r]; Pyykko order-resolved covalent radii [M]; Shannon ionic radii [M; Z_eff E at tens of percent]; polarizability alpha [H exact 4.5a0^3; Lorentz-oscillator E; M override]; Racah B, C and Jorgensen f, g [M class constants]; formation-energy rows dG_f(T,P) including the melt-redox curve [M/compute-once]; bond force constants k [M; Badger E for invented bonds]; E_b cohesive anchors [M/A; D for ionics]; rho0 per phase [A/M, packing]; B0 [E from E_coh/V; M override], B0' ~ 4 default; aufbau override ~20 rows [A, clustering rationale D]. Layer 2.5: the prototype library, 30 to 50 aristotypes carrying exact Madelung constants and packing geometry [D] and radius-ratio windows [D-in-form, two-thirds honesty grade]. Anchor rows: H exact, H2 compute-once spectroscopic, He metrology-grade. Backstop: the compute-once tier (Materials Project ~1e5 compounds at tens-of-meV/atom, USPEX/CCSD(T) for de novo).

## Stage 1, scenario plane (data-driven inputs, all sim-written)
x_local(r,t): composition field from GCE priors -> condensation disposer across the disk -> accretion -> differentiation (Stokes iron rain, pressure disproportionation) -> fractionation and transport [W chain rooted in the X vector]. E(r,t) = (T, P, mu-vector): geotherm, hydrostatic EOS stack, and the generalized chemical-potential vector (fO2, fS2, fH2, aH2O, aCO2...) from the atmosphere-interior kernel [W]. h(r,t): path record, cooling rate, strain, damage, fluid flux, prior assemblage [W]. seed [X]. Purity guarantee (the solver law): the oracle is a pure function of these four; no per-world materials data exists anywhere.

## Stage 2, proposer
Valences read off mu-vector via the buffer ladder (multi-valence resolved per environment, never per element); charge-balanced stoichiometries over the locally dominant ~8 elements; MO-viability tier above classical valence (the CO lesson); silicate candidates by O/Si polymerization arithmetic, 4 -> isolated tetrahedra down to 2 -> frameworks [D]; laziness invariant: nothing proposed below saturation. Memoized on (composition class, E-bucket).

## Stage 3, class dispatch (computed)
Ionicity from d-chi_M [E, Pauling fraction; Phillips refinement]; H-bond membership from the chi_AR donor set {N, O, F} [D]; metallicity from conduction columns. Five routes; the covalent-metallic middle, sulfides foremost, is mandatory measured-or-compute-once [declared soft spot].

## Stage 4, disposer
Assemble G = E_lattice + P.dV - T.S_config - T.S_vib per candidate. Lattice energy by class: ionic -> Born-Lande + Madelung [E, the 5% NaCl closure]; metallic -> EOS anchors + Miedema [M pairs, tens of percent]; covalent -> E_b sums with compute-once arbitration; H-bonded -> the E_HB ladder 29/21/13 kJ/mol [M class]; molecular -> London alpha^2.IE/r^6 [E, ~20% on argon's C6]. P.dV from the Vinet EOS, itself the Rose universal binding relation on E_b [D-in-form]. S_config exact (-R.sum x ln x) plus Margules [C class]; S_vib via Debye theta_D from the EOS sound speed [D-in-form]. Lookup order is the provenance hierarchy: measured row -> estimator -> compute-once -> authored-with-flag. Exchange equilibria partition the solid solutions through Goldschmidt windows on the radius column [D-in-form] and simultaneously write the thermometry record [W]. Buffer arbitration settles multi-valence families (the FeO/Fe2O3/Fe3O4 ladder, Boudouard per state). Output: equilibrium assemblage.

## Stage 5, freezer (the g-framework consolidated)
Barriers E* = g.R.T_m with g a class-and-mechanism constant [M class: 17 to 18 close-packed metals, 26 to 30 covalent, grain-boundary fraction 0.4 to 0.6]; prefactor nu = c_s/a [D], closing the canonical D0 ~ a^2.nu ~ 1e-5 m^2/s; V* = g.R.(dT_m/dP), Clausius-Clapeyron wearing its third hat [D]; T_m itself Lindemann + Slater gamma [E] with cryoscopic depression [D]. Dodson closure per exchange reaction against h's cooling rate quenches compositions [W]; metastable inheritance persists (diamond); sub-kT polymorphs resolve by seeded draw [X, named slot]; grain size writes from nucleation (CNT prefactor flag) and fabric from strain and damage [W]. Output: the realized assemblage, and its distance from equilibrium is the archive.

## Stage 6, property emission (each value tagged and banded)
rho from EOS; K, G from lattice curvature or measured elastic rows; stiffness scale E ~ E_coh/a^3 (1 eV/A^3 = 160 GPa) [E]. Creep via one Frost-Ashby master map per bonding class, axes T/T_m and sigma/mu, serving every substance real or invented [D-in-form + class g]. Strength as trajectory: pristine ceiling sqrt(E.gamma_s/a0) ~ E/10 [D] degraded by the written crack population (Griffith [D], Weibull family CLT-forced [D-in-form], modulus [C]), with Paris and stress-corrosion kinetics [C]; hardness = Tabor 3.sigma_y on Taylor forest hardening over written dislocation density [D-in-form on W], with the derived Hall-Petch versus diffusion-creep sign flip near T/T_m ~ 0.45. Magnetics: insulator moments fully derived (Hund + crystal-field quenching + Goodenough-Kanamori; HS/LS boolean only for octahedral d4 to d7, tetrahedral high-spin by delta_t = 4/9.delta_o; 10Dq = f.g); metallic moments [M, Slater-Pauling structure]; coercivity is domain-wall pinning on the same defect population, written state [W], soft versus hard = annealed versus damaged. Color of transition-metal phases reads off the f.g product [D-in-form]. IR modes from masses plus k [M; Badger E], feeding the opacity ledger. Oxidation: the derived triple conjunction, PBR (densities, zero cost) AND Wagner k_p class (valence multiplicity + gap, barriers in the g-framework) AND involatility (oxide p_sat), with film thickness a written time series, atmosphere-selected [W]. Tribology: mu from Bowden-Tabor A_real = N/H [D-in-form], clean limit 0.19 [D], tau_i riding the film state [C, state-dependent]; Archard wear [D-in-form, k C]; capstan and self-locking as exact corollaries.

## Stage 7, aggregation
Hashin-Shtrikman bounds on mode fractions [exact], texture selecting the point within [W]; melt and fluid connectivity by the dihedral-angle criterion on interfacial-energy ratios [D-in-form, relaxation flag]; permeability from the crack statistics [W]. Emits the effective-property struct that convection, strength, erosion, and conduction consume. Names are gazetteer, never read by physics.

## Stage 8, lazy ores
Transport raises saturation events at derived fronts: van 't Hoff cliffs, boiling horizons, buffer redox fronts, Stokes-Shields traps, evaporite ladders (solubility products at the chemistry border [C]), WHAK residua. Proposer instantiates locally, only past saturation. Taxonomy = (carrier x front) [D]; grade-tonnage log-normal by multiplicative CLT [D-in-form]; BIFs as the atmosphere-coupled validation.

## Stage 9, self-checks
Closure battery: NaCl 5%; CO 113 versus 112.8 pm; magnetite 4 versus 4.1 mu_B; iron M_s 1.73 versus 1.71 MA/m; Cu vacancy ratio 0.37 inside the 0.2 to 0.4 band; D0 at 1e-5; forsterite alpha within factor 1.4; silicon dopant within factor 2; argon C6 ~20% low as London should be; enstatite-chondrite assemblages at reduced mu-vector; Pluto's N2-CO solution; anorthosite flotation sign flip; Fo90 from pyrolite. Census battery: Hazen staircase doubling on oxygenation with the reduced-world flat null; ~99% of volume from a dozen families; LNRE frequency shape; CAI-first condensation order; Bowen series as disposer execution order. Engine checks: error-band DAG surfaces any assemblage that flips within its bands; provenance lookup order enforced; per-element mass balance exact on the lattice and latent heats posted to the heat ledger; counts rendered as queries; status fields with spec-to-repo diff; pure-function audit on the memoization.
